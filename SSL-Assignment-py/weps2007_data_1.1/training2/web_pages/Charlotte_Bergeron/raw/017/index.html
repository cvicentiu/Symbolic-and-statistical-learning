Submitter:Jane
Subject:Ruth Mildred Austin Koncel - Obituary
Message:From The Modesto Bee, December 4, 2003

Ruth M. Koncel

June 11, 1922 -- Dec. 2, 2003

Ruth Mildred Koncel, 81, of Modesto died Tuesday at her home.

Mrs. Koncel was a native of Greensboro, N.C. She moved to Southern California in 1944 and Delhi in 1973 before settling in Modesto in 1985. She was a homemaker. She was a member of Jehovah's Witnesses. She enjoyed crocheting and crossword puzzles.

She is survived by her children, Bob Koncel of Lake Isabella, Nancy Schroder of Placentia, Marie Crain of Vista, and Joe Koncel and Lisa Hampton, both of Modesto; and 19 grandchildren, 26 great-grandchildren and one great-great-grandchild. She was preceded in death by her son Bill McCall; and daughter Patricia Zechiel.

A funeral will be at 2 p.m. Saturday at the Central Congregation of Jehovah's Witnesses. Private burial. No visitation. Allen Mortuary, Turlock, in charge of arrangements.

Remembrances may be made to The Watch Tower Bible & Tract Society, in care of secretary and treasurer, 25 Columbia Heights, Brooklyn, NY 11201-2483.
