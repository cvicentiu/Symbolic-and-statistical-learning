Baton Rouge, LA- Lunch with the League

Date:   2-26-04


WHO: The League of WoMen Voters of Baton Rouge WHAT: February "Lunch With the League" public forum Topic: "Is there really transparency in Government?" A frank discussion of freedom of information in Louisiana: the citizens' rights to governmental records and open meetings. INVITED SPEAKERS NOT CONFIRMED AT RELEASE OF THIS PRESS NOTICE:: Representatives of the Governor's Office and of the City Parish Government. INVITED SPEAKERS WHO ARE CONFIRMED: Charlotte Bergeron, PAR Good Government point of view Ken DeJean, Louisiana Attorney General Office point of view Jackie Ducote, Citizens point of View Vickie Zimmerman, WAFB Media point of view WHEN: Thursday, February 26, 2004, 11:30 a.m.-12:00 p.m; Program starts promptly at Noon. WHERE: Phillippe's The Place, 5255 Florida Blvd., Baton Rouge, LA HOW: COSTS: Members: $15.00; Non-members $20.00 Reservations encouraged to ensure seating. Call 334-9999 or e-mail lwvbr@lwvbr.org
