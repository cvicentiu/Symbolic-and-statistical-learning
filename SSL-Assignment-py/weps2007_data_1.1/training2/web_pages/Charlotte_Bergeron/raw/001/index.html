NOTES: Michel and Jonathan MORISSEAU operated Hungry Hall together, a post of the Hudson's Bay Co., situated on Hungry Lake, from 1850-1890. Michel also had a dogsled and ran mail from International Falls to Rat Portage (Kenora). Michel had entered the service of the HBC, June 1, 1853 to 1858, at Fort Alexander. The obituary has the twins, Jonathan and Jacob, born in 1827 but, the death certificate reads differently (1838)? Jacob had died in 1934 at 107 years of age. Jonathan passed away Aug 5, 1937, at 110 years of age. This is taken from the book "Early Settlers of Rat Portage" Summer project 1981-82. I have more info pertaining to Michel and his descendants.
