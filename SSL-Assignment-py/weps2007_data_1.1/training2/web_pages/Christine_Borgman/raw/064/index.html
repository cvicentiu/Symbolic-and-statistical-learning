Call for Participation

International Conference on Asian Digital Libraries (ICADL2002)
11-14 December 2002, Singapore

Early Registration Deadline: 15 October 2002

http://www.cais.ntu.edu.sg:8000/icadl2002/
-----------------------------------------------------------------

On behalf of the organizing committee, I would like to invite you to participate in 
the 5th International Conference on Asian Digital Libraries (ICADL2002).

The highlights of the conference program include: 

- 3 Keynote Speeches:

   Keynote 1: Web Services and Web Integration
              Serge Abiteboul (Ecole Polytechnique, France)

   Keynote 2: Data Mining Technologies for Digital Libraries and Web Information Systems
              Srikant Ramakrishnan (IBM Almaden Research Lab, USA)

   Keynote 3: [To be announced]
              Jane Prey (National Science Foundation, USA)

- 6 Invited Talks:

   Invited Talk 1: From Digital Library to Digital Government- 
                   A Case Study in Crime Data Mapping and Mining
                   Hsinchun Chen (University of Arizona, USA)

   Invited Talk 2: Challenges in Building the Digital Library for the 21st Century
                   Christine Borgman (Univ. of California, Los Angeles, USA)

   Invited Talk 3: Dublin Core: Process and Principles
                   Shigeo Sugimoto (ULIS, Japan), Stuart Weibel (OCLC, USA), 
                   Thomas Baker (GMD, Germany)

   Invited Talk 4: [To be announced]
                   Edward Fox (Virginia Tech, USA)

   Invited Talk 5: [To be announced]
                   Ian Witten (University of Waikato, New Zealand)

   Invited Talk 6: [To be announced]
                   Zhang Xiaoxing (National Library of China, PRC)

- 12 Paper Sessions: consisting of 34 full paper and 20 short paper presentations.

- 4 Tutorials:

   Tutorial 1a: Evaluating Digital Libraries for Usability
                Christine Borgman (Univ. of California, Los Angeles, USA)

   Tutorial 1b: Dublin Core
                Stuart Weibel (OCLC, USA)

   Tutorial 2a: Digital Libraries: Theory and Practice
                Edward Fox (Virginia Tech, USA)

   Tutorial 2b: Knowledge Management Systems: A Text Mining Perspective
                Hsinchun Chen (Univ. of Arizona, USA)

- Conference Gala Dinner, Poster Paper Exhibition, Library Tours, and others.

- Conference proceedings will be printed by the Lecture Note Series in Computer
  Science, Springer-Verlag. 

- Post-Conference Workshop on Library and Information Science Education in Asia (LISEA)
  (free to all ICADL participants)

- Early registration fees of ICADL2002 Conference only are US$300(general) and US$150(student).

- ICADL2002 will be held at the same place and time as WISE2002.  Registration discount
  applies to WISE2002 participants.


For detailed conference program and registration information, please refer to 

		http://www.cais.ntu.edu.sg:8000/icadl2002/


We look forward to your participation.


Sincerely,


Ee-Peng Lim

Nanyang Technological University
