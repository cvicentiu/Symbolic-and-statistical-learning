Message to ORION Functional Sponsor Group

March 8, 2002

To:   ORION Functional Sponsor Group
Christine Borgman, Professor, Information Studies
Joseph Bristow, Professor, English
Alfonso Cardenas, Professor, Computer Science
Jim Davis, AVP for Information Technology
Eric Gans, Professor, French and Francophone Studies
Nicolette Hart, Professor, Sociology
Terry Ryan, Associate University Librarian for IT
Ruth Sabean, Assistant Director, Office of Instructional Development
From:   Associate University Librarian Terry Ryan
Subject:   ORION Functional Sponsor Group

Thank you all for agreeing to be members of the functional sponsor group for the new ORION system. Your help in incorporating faculty and other user input into the evaluation and selection process will be very valuable. I'd like to schedule an initial meeting as soon as possible and will be asking my assistant, Leslie McMichael, to be in touch with you for possible meeting times. At our first meeting, we can discuss how frequently to meet and what times will work best for all of you for future meetings.

In preparation for our first meeting, please look at the Web site we've created at:

http://www.library.ucla.edu/new-orion/index.html

for background information. In particular, it would be very helpful if each of you could try out the possible systems at other universities before we meet.

Thanks again for your help with this project. I look forward to working with all of you.
