MUSCLE post-doctoral fellowship:

Understanding and Benchmarking of Content Based Image Retrieval Methods

Fellow

Gareth Loy
 
Hosted by Jan-Olof Eklundh (KTH, Stockholm) and
Allan Hanbury (Tech University of Vienna)
This project will investigate methods for classification and recognition of objects, scenes and events in still images and video sequences. In particular the work will focus on the extraction of local features and feature descriptors, and a comparison of different feature representations in the context of CBIR. The strengths and limitations of different methods will be examined as will the scenarios best suited to the various approaches. The development of new methods and adaptations of existing techniques will also be considered.
