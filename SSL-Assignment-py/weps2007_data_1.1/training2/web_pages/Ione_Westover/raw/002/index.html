Issue date: September 01, 2000

Wine celebration Wine celebration (September 01, 2000)

Tickets for the Wine Celebration ($25 in advance, $30 the day of the event) are available in Pleasanton at The Wine Steward, 641 Main St., 600-9463, and at the wineries. Visit www.livermorewine.com for more information or to link to each winery's web site. Or call the Winegrower's office at 447-9463. The cost includes tastings at all the wineries, a commemorative glass and rides on shuttle buses servicing the entire area of the wine celebration. The wineries will offer music, crafts, gourmet foods, tours and educational programs as well as wine tastings.
Livermore Valley Winery Listing

The Big White House 7000 Tesla Road (located at Cedar Mountain Winery) Livermore, CA 94550 (925) 449-1976
Specializing in small lots of hand-crafted Rhône varietals.

Cedar Mountain Winery 7000 Tesla Road Livermore, CA 94550 (925) 373-6636
Since 1990, Earl and Linda Ault have produced award-winning Cabernet Royale Port and Cabernet Sauvignon.

Concannon Vineyard 4590 Tesla Road Livermore, CA 94550 (925) 456-2505
Established in 1883, creating world-class Petite Sirah, Cabernet Sauvignon and Sauvignon Blanc.
Chouinard Vineyards 33853 Palomares Road Castro Valley, CA 94552 (510) 582-9900 Family owned, producing award-winning varietals, a distinctive Granny Smith Apple wine and Orange Muscat.

Elliston Vineyards 463 Kilkare Road Sunol, CA 94586 (925) 862-2377
Established in 1983 as a boutique winery with a long grape-growing history, featuring a Captain's Claret, champagne, burgundy-style blends. Restaurant and event center.

Fenestra Winery 83 Vallecitos Road Livermore, CA 94550 (925) 447-5246
Since 1980, a family-owned winery with a comfortable atmosphere and award-winning wines.
Garré Vineyard and Winery 7986 Tesla Road Livermore, CA 94550 (925) 371-8200
Since 1995, Pleasanton owners Steve Aracne and Bob Molinaro have specialized in estate bottle Merlot wines. Restaurant and event center.

Iván Támás Winery 5443 Tesla Road Livermore, CA 94550 (925) 456-2380
Steve and son Steven Mirassou create Cal-Ital wines such as Trebbiano and Sangiovese and new ultra-premium Steven Kent Winery Cabernet Sauvignon.

Jackson Cellars 7986 Tesla Road (Tasting will be at Garré Vineyard and Winery for the Harvest Wine Celebration) Livermore, CA 94550 (925) 606-9195
Livermore Valley's newest winery producing Livermore Valley Cabernet Sauvignon and Merlot at Garré Winery.

Livermore Valley Cellars 1508 Wetmore Road Livermore, CA 94550 (925) 454-9463
Family-owned and funky, producing wines exclusively from Livermore Valley grapes.

Murrieta's Well 3005 Mines Road Livermore, CA 94550 (925) 462-2390
Founded in 1989 by Phil Wente and Sergio Traverso, producing ultra-premium wines from the historic estate vineyard. Tasting room recently reopened.

Retzlaff Vineyards 1356 S. Livermore Ave. Livermore, CA 94550 (925) 447-8941
Using only estate-grown grapes, this family-owned winery emphasizes quality with its Cabernet Sauvignon, Merlot and Sauvignon Blanc wines.

Stony Ridge Winery 4948 Tesla Road Livermore, CA 94550 (925) 449-0458
New Pleasanton owners Clyde Vaughn and Dale Vaughn-Bowen feature Cal-Ital wines such as Nebbiolo and Malvasia Bianca. Restaurant and event center.

Thomas Coyne Winery 51 E. Vallecitos Road Livermore, CA 94550 (925) 373-6541
Family-owned winery located at the historic Chateau Bellevue site with panoramic views of the valley, producing Rhône varietals and Merlot.

Wente Vineyards Estate and Winery Tasting Room 5565 Tesla Road Livermore, CA 94550 (925) 456-2305
The Valley's largest winery, operating since 1883, continuing the family tradition of making award-winning full-bodied, richly flavored wines.

Wente Vineyards Restaurant & Visitors Center 5050 Arroyo Road Livermore, CA 94550 (925) 456-2405
In 1981, the Wentes purchased the historic Cresta Blanca Winery and transformed it into a special events center for dining, concerts and golf.

Westover Winery & Event Center 34329 Palomares Road Castro Valley, CA 94552 (510) 537-3932
Named after the owner's grandmother, Ione Westover Smyth from Palomares Canyon. Produces more than 20 wines. Event center and spa services.
