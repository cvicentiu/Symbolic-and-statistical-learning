About Windover
Windover Art Center is celebrating its 20th anniversary this year!
Since 1981, Windover has offered unique art experiences for Maine children. Nestled in the hills of Newburgh, Maine, Windover is located on a 160 acre tree farm, with fields, a pond, woodlands, mountain streams, and miles of hiking trails. Our facilities include the Art Barn, the Pottery Studio, the Birch Tree Theatre, the Photography Lab & Darkrooms, the Wood and Glass Shop, the pool and changing rooms, and the teepee. Windover is a non-profit organization dedicated to children and the arts.

Our Philosophy
"There is much to learn, much to experience, and to contribute through involvement. Each and every child is special to us and we do everything possible to meet their individual needs. Our philosophy is that people can develop their creativity, practice making decisions and learn responsibility while having fun in a caring cooperative community. We believe that through learning to create and share in a peaceful camp environment, children will develop the skills to transform the greater community of the world."
Mari Abercrombie
