SUMMER READING BOOKS

Required Summer Reading List
Students in 6, 7, and 8 grades need to be prepared to take a Reading Counts computerized reading comprehension test over two of these books during the first two weeks of school in August. If the test is passed (7 out of 10 correct anwers) the student will receive points that will apply toward those required for 20% of their first six weeks Language Arts grade. Therefore, if the student does not complete the summer reading, they will be accountable for more work during the first six weeks of the new school year.
Advanced 7th grade students select one of these books and are required to read A Corner of the Universe by Ann M. Martin. Advanced 8th grade students must select one of these titles and Ghost Boy by Iain Lawrence.
In addition to this list any of the 2004-5 Middle School Sunshine State Young Readers books may also be read. These titles may be found at: www.firn.edu/doe/instmat/ssyrap.htm
If you want to read this list on line, go to our school web page - summer reading at : www.mb-ms.pinellas.k12.fl.us/
There is also a suggested book summary on this site to help students remember what they have read.
