Civic House Resource and Reading Rooms

Found on the third floor of Civic House both the Resource and Reading Rooms have the tools you need to make your tutoring effective, meaningful, and fun!

In the Resource Room you can find:

· Text books and workbooks for all subjects
· Worksheets for various literacy and math skills
· Games and activities for you and your tutee

In the Reading Room you can find:
· Fiction and Non-fiction books for all reading levels
· Arts and crafts activities and materials

Please stop by and check out a book or explore our resources. These rooms are for YOU; you and your tutee are always welcome and encouraged to put them to use!
