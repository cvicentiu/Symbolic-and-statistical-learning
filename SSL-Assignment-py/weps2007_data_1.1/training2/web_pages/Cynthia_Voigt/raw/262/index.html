REQUIRED SUMMER READING—2004

Announcement

The following reading list is for the summer of 2004.  

Thompson Middle School

Required Summer Reading 2004

6th Grade:  Choose one

  • The View From Saturday – E. L. Konigsburg
  • The Landry News  – Andrew Clements
  • Our Only May Amelia –  Jennifer Holm
  • A Long Way From Chicago – Richard Peck
  • Getting Near to Baby – Audrey Couloumbis

7th Grade:  Choose one

  • Treasure Island – Robert Louis Stevenson
  • Roll of Thunder, Hear My Cry – Mildred Taylor
  • The Lion, the Witch, and the Wardrobe  – C. S. Lewis
  • A Wrinkle in Time – Madeleine L’Engle

 8th Grade:  Choose one

  • Homecoming – Cynthia Voigt
  • The Lottery Rose – Irene Hunt
  • The Outsiders – S. E. Hinton
  • The River – Gary Paulsen

 

Summer Reading for GRC Students

6th Grade:  Choose two of the following.  If you have already read a book, then choose a different one.  You will receive points for taking the Reading Counts test on the books you read, so DO NOT choose a novel you have already read.  You cannot take a test on a book you have read in elementary school.

  • The View From Saturday by E.L. Konigsburg

  • A Long Way From Chicago by Richard Peck

  • The Tale of Despereaux by Kate DiCamillo

  • The Ear, the Eye, and the Arm by Nancy Farmer

 7th Grade:  Read all of the following: 

  •   The Giver – Lois Lowery  

  •   The True Confessions of Charlotte Doyle – Avi

  •  The Westing Game – Ellen Raskin

 8th Grade:  Read three of the following:

  • The Outsiders – S. E. Hinton

  •   The Pigman – Paul Zindel

  • Choose one:  Homecoming by Cynthia Voigt or Redwall – Brian Jacque
