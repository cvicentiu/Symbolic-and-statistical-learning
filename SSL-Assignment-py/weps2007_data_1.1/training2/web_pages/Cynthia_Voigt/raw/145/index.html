STUDY GUIDE: Homecoming by Cynthia Voigt  

Before Reading

Summary: One sunny afternoon, the Tillerman children find themselves alone. Their mother, who has medical problems, has abandoned them, leaving them homeless. Dicey is the oldest and, at 13, must now make all the decisions for the family. Dicey must keep the children fed, find places for them to sleep, and make sure, above all, that they stay together. Will they find an adult they can trust? Will they ever find a home to call their own?

About the Author: Cynthia Voigt enjoys writing books with complex characters who face difficult problems. Her characters often create something good from the bad things that happen. Cynthia Voigt hopes her readers will find her books to be both thought-provoking and entertaining.

During Reading

Main Characters

  • Dicey
  • James
  • Mary
  • Beth
  • Sammy
  • Abigail

Topic: Families

Key Ideas related to Topic:
(1) Family members can help each other through hard times.
(2) Disappointment and frustration are part of life, but you should work hard to make things better.
(3) Each family member, young or old, can help keep the family together.

After Reading

1. What do you think the author is trying to tell you about family? Use examples from the book to explain your ideas.

2. How does Abigail change by the end of the book? Why do you think this change happens?
