A veritable listing of books

This is a listing (born of extreme boredom and hoarding of draconic proportions) of my books. Its not all of them, mostly due to the fact that the other books are with my dad, but its a lot of them. Some are the fruit born of various yard sales and secondhand store shopping sprees, afternoons spent riding the bus to get to the thrift store and then discovering paper gems on the very last shelf in the very farthest corner for dimes, nickels, pennies.

Others yet were handed down or given, some slightly water damaged or sandy from hours on the beach, languishing in sand or wooden docks, sitting under picnic shelters as outside, the rain poured down. There are a few that smell of Christmas, curling up under the covers in ridiculous Winnie the Pooh pajamas and reading book after book after book, engraving the words and the plots and the characters into my mind...

Some are leftovers from my childhood, favourites that lose a little of their shine in an adulthood reread but are still precious nonetheless. I'll be systematically adding pop-ups with comments pertaining to a few of the books.
