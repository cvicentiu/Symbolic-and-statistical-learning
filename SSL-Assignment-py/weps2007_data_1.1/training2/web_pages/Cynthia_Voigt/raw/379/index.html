I am a book obsessive, who loves chocolate a little bit too much. I am attempting to study for a psychology degree with the Open University, but find myself reading novels rather than text books all too often!!!

My favourite books are thrillers, although I do enjoy most of the books that I read and I do have an increasingly large soft spot for romance!
I am constantly buying books, and am the proud owner of a huge To Be Read pile, that never seems to shrink no matter how much I read!
