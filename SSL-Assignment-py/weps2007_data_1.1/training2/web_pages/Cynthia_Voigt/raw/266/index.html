Nominees From Past Years
Young Adults' Books
The Children's Literature Association of Utah does not necessarily recommend that all young adults read all books. These nominees offer a balance in reading level, interest level, types of fiction, and relevant, contemporary social issues for young adults in grades 7 to 12. We encourage parents to read and discuss these books with their teenagers.
