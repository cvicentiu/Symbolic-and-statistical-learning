show/hide extended profile

I am a bibliophile. I have too many books, and I have been a bookcrosser since before the site was invented. I have always tried to supply crisis shelters with books, and have given away too many to remember.
I like Doris Lessing, Andrew Vachss, James Lee Burke, Alison Bechdel, Kaz Cooke, cheap sci-fi anthologies, classic fiction, (Jane Austen, Dickens) feminist novels, kids' books, Cynthia Voigt, Lynda Barry, Iain Banks, JK Rowling, and I will read the street directory if nothing else is available.

My goal is to release as many books as I can bear to part with! My first reaction was....oh, no, I can't let that one go! I'm sure most book lovers feel the same way.
Happy reading!
P.S. by donating to shelters you may not get a registered find, as not everyone has internet access, but you may entertain or even help someone going through a rough patch.

O.K. this is going to sound like I'm a real book snob, but I would just like to let you know that newk and I are trying for a Bogan bookbox. NO WAY would I have Barrett or Laws on my shelves otherwise!
'Nuff said...

Please Note: I wild release most books. Those marked available are mostly for local trade or wild release, due to the cost of international postage.
