Re: Dicey's Song by Cynthia Voigt

From: Alyssa Fountain
Date: 24 Mar 2000

Comments

This was one of my favorites as well! I totally wanted to be like Dicey. This is a great "journey" story, full of adventure. There are a lot of themes in the novel that could be explored in a class study of it: mental illness, family, pride, personal strength, individual talents. I wonder if boys would enjoy it as much? Probably not, but I think it is still worth teaching.
