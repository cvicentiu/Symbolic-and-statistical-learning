7th Grade  Language Arts Reading List

During Reading Workshop you will read novels. You may read any novels your parents approve, and once every four weeks you must finish a book from this list and take a test on that book. You may take more than one test every four weeks, and I will record only the highest score.

Newbery Medal and Honor Books are marked with a double asterisk (**).  

If you read a book from the History/Social Studies section and pass the test on that book, you will get the usual points in Reading Workshop, AND Mrs. Brotherton, Mr. Hancock, or Mrs. Hanschen will give you extra credit points in World Geography class.

The Giver and The Miracle Worker are not on the list because these works are read and studied in class.

The categories from which you may choose are as follows:

Tales of Adventure and Survival           Tales of Realistic Fiction

Tales of History/ Social Studies             Tales of Fantasy and Fiction

Tales of Mystery and Suspense              Biography and NonFiction
