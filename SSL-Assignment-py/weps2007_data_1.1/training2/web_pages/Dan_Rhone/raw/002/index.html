Music Programs

At Eleanor Roosevelt we are privileged to have a wonderful Musical Staff. We have both vocal and instrumental teachers.

Students meet once a week for Music Class. The fifth level students have a Chorus rehearsal in addition to music class. Chorus also meets once a week and performs at our Holiday Program in December and Spring Concert in May.

Students in levels four and five have the opportunity to take Instrumental lessons once a week and also perform in the spring. Click on the pictures to find out more about the Music Program at Eleanor Roosevelt Elementary School.
