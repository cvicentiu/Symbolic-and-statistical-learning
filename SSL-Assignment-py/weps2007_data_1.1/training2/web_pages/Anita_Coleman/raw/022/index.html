IT WAS my pleasure to present the 3rd annual CARL Research Award certificate and $1,000 to the project, "The Definition of a Digital Library for California Community Colleges," on September 12th at the 6th Annual CARL Conference. Dr. Anita Coleman, Assistant Professor and Systems Librarian at Santa Ana College, designed and submitted this project.
Dr. Coleman's project will develop a model digital library and accompanying courses for use in community college settings. The project's results will include a statement of collection development principles for digital libraries, a proposed model collection, and reports of course evaluations.
The CARL Web site will post these results, and Dr. Coleman will provide updates to the CARL membership through the CARL Newsletter.
