President's E-Letter
August 25 , 2006

DISCLAIMER
The opinions expressed on the President's Page are those of the author and do not reflect official policy or opinion of the Macomb County Bar Association membership.

Macomb County Bar Association's
78th President
Jacob M. Femminineo, Jr.
MCBA Honored with Alexander Macomb Award

As you are undoubtedly aware, the Bath City Festival is going on this weekend in Mt. Clemens. The festival officially opened today at noon with music, a military salute and the presentation of the Alexander Macomb 2006 Historic Award.

I was privileged to receive the award on behalf of the Macomb County Bar Association from Macomb County Board of Commissioners Chair, Nancy White. The annual award is presented to organizations or individuals that have demonstrated a long term commitment to the preservation of Macomb County's history, architecture, archeology, engineering or culture and promotes the use of Historic Preservation for the education, pleasure and welfare of the citizens of Macomb County and the State of Michigan. This award honors all members of the MCBA over the past 100 years of our history.

Additionally, long time MCBA member Ray Glime was awarded an Alexander Macomb Award for his 50 years of service to the community. Congratulations Ray! You are a lawyer's lawyer and a shining example of what our profession brings to the community.

Thank you Nancy White, Karl Mark Paul and the entire Macomb County Historical Commission for bestowing this honor upon the Macomb County Bar Association.

I encourage you to visit the MCBA historical display at the Macomb County Administration Building.

Until next time, Ill see you walking down the streets of the great Bath City.
Nancy White, Macomb County Board of Commissioners Chair presents the Jacob M. Femminineo Jr. on behalf of the Macomb County Bar Association the Alexander Macomb 2006 Historic Award.
