Macomb Teacher of the week: Donna Gauthier, Alexander Macomb Academy

Comment on this story
Send this story to a friend
Get Home Delivery

Who she is: Donna Gauthier teaches a fifth- and sixth-grade classroom at Alexander Macomb Academy in Mount Clemens.

Background: Gauthier has taught in the district for more than 30 years.

Why nominated: She is a member of the school improvement team and an advocate for science education. She has organized and attended the residential science camp annually with her students. She is one of the first to embrace new educational technology including distance learning, video streaming and computer-assisted microscopes. Her interest in science and ability to engage students in the learning process has spurred Gauthier to apply for specialty grants.

She says: "I became a teacher because I had two excellent men teachers in middle school and high school and I thought, 'Wow, that looks like a great job.' And it is still a great job, and I have been at it 35 years!"
