Case Study Assignment Template

Instructions: Use the form below to create an assignment form. The steps are as follows:

  1. Fill in your name. Replace the generic email address with your email address (e.g. the address to which you want your students' responses sent).
  2. Edit the text or labels for any of the items below. For example, you may want to add information to the student instructions.
  3. Click on the Submit button. Your changes will be processed and the modified form will be sent to your email address.
  4. Once you receive the file in your email, copy the text and save it as a text file. Give it a filename with .html as the extension (e.g. webeval.html). Upload the file to your server as you normally do.
  5. Authorized users may upload their forms to an existing E-Reserves page.
  6. Your form is now ready for use by your students. Their responses will be sent to the email address you specified. The computer will also display a copy of their completed form on the screen. You may want to have your students print/save that copy for their records.
