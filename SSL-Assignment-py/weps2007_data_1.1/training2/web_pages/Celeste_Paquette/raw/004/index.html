ORANGE COUNTY HIGH SCHOOL GIRLS ALL-TIME TOP 25
TRACK & FIELD MARKS - Compiled by Don Chadez (dchadez@aol.com)

For the purpose of ranking athletes and their performance, hand times have been converted to equivalent auto times and yard performances to metric. Hand times have been converted by
adding 0.24 seconds for races less than one lap, and 0.14 for one lap races. Races of more than one lap have no conversion from hand to auto timing. The yard time was then multiplied by
.9942 to get the metric equivalent. Common ratio races follow this formula. 100yard/100 meters and 110 meters/120 yard hurdle events are not common ratio events.

Abbreviations:
+ -mark converted from yards to meters.
c -mark converted from hand time to auto time
y -original mark attained in a race in yards.
* -before athletes name indicated junior year of school.
** -before athletes name indicates sophomore year of school.
*** -before athletes name indicates freshman year of school.
