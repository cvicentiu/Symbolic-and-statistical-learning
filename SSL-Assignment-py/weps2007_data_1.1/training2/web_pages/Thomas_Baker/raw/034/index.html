Thomas S. Baker joined Plunkett & Cooney in 1998 as an attorney in the firm’s Grand Rapids office. He focuses his practice in the areas of insurance defense, municipal law, product liability, motor vehicle and trucking & transportation liability and general liability.

Mr. Baker received his undergraduate degree, cum laude, from Hillsdale College in 1991 and his law degree from the University of Detroit-Mercy School of Law in 1996.
