28. Thomas4 Baker (William3, Samuel2, Alexander1) (#2612) was born in Pa 8 Jan 1710/11.(4) Thomas died 10 Jan 1777 in Culpepper County, Va, at 66 years of age.

He married Dorthey Davenport ABT. 1733. (Dorthey Davenport is #2506.) Dorthey was born 2 Nov 1716. Dorthey died ABT. 1790 in Burke County, Nc.
