The Ultimate in Personal Legal Service

Welcome to the Web site for the law office of Attorney Thomas J. Baker.  With offices in Killeen and Temple, Texas, I am committed to providing high-quality, assertive, and effective legal representation to people throughout the Killeen and Temple areas, including Gatesville, Coppers Cove, Belton, Fort Hood, and other communities in Bell and Coryell counties.

To best meet my clients’ needs, I focus on the following practice areas:

  • Divorce and Family Law
  • Personal Injury
  • Criminal Defense
  • General Civil Practice

I realize that most people do not enjoy, or look forward to, retaining the services of a lawyer. The circumstances that require legal assistance often leave people feeling vulnerable and helpless. I alleviate those concerns by treating each situation according to its unique circumstances, and treating every client with the highest level of respect, professionalism, and confidentiality as I guide you through the legal process.

My understanding of the law, trust, and respect are the basis of my longstanding relationships with clients. Every effort is made to anticipate clients' needs and concerns and address them early. I am always available for my clients, including late and unusual hours. I am dedicated to providing warm, personal attention by listening carefully, seeking to understand the unique qualities of each case and the special needs of each client. Phone calls are returned promptly, and I personally handle all client matters. I consider you to be a respected partner in the resolution of your legal concerns and therefore keep you fully informed of the progress of your case.

Whether it is through a free initial consultation, flexible office hours, or the attention to detail, I go out of my way to deliver the best possible service in a professional, compassionate, and caring manner.

Contact me today for the best possible representation.
