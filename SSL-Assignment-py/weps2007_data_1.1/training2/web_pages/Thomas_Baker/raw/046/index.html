BAKER, THOMAS.

English account book.
1751 - 1786.
1 item (237 p. on 121 leaves).

Item is record of expenditures for pocket money, housekeeping and clothing expenses, and gaming proceeds and losses from 1752 to 1767. Verso of first page includes an entry signed by Daniel Baker and an entry dated 1786. Currency used is pounds, shillings and pence.
Holograph. - Item damaged and some entries lost.
Donated by Mrs. W.E. Finch in 1975.
Text in English.
No restrictions on access.
