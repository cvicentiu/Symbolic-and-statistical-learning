   Acerca de The Thomas Baker Band

The Thomas Baker Band is a collaboration of two good friends that would be doing something together even if they weren't making music. Ben Baker and Robert Thomas have known each other since their early teens and have been best friends since. They both started playing guitar around twelve years old and in the many years since then, have they have both grown to be excellent musicians, each bringing his own style of guitar to contribute to their overall sound. Ben and Robert both grew up in the small town of Florence, South Carolina which had a major impact on their own original sound. Influences include artists of almost every genre from Jim Croce and Jack Johnson to Afroman and Gnarls Barkley. Currently, The Thomas Baker Band has been playing the local music scene in South Carolina but is planning to build on its fan base by touring the east coast sometime in early 2007. You can expect to see The Thomas Baker Band in a venue near you, or very far far away from you. Whatever the case may be Robert and Ben will be making good music for your ears to hear so stay tuned.
