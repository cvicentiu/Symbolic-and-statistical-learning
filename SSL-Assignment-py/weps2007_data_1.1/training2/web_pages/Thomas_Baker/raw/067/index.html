Copy of a message sent to me by Bruce

My wife Carolyn is a descendant of Elias Baker, who lived in Gwinnett County at one time. I forget the dates, but he or his sons removed to Searcy County, Arkansas. Many of the Bakers that went to Arkansas, then came to Texas in the 1890's and settled around Oenavile, Bell County, Texas.
