Brittany Barnett - Miss Lubbock 2007      

Brittany Barnett

My Legacy ...

       "Accept the challenge to meet the needs 
                                         of Children with Disabilities "
 

Brittany was crowned Miss Lubbock 2007 in a pageant  held January 6, 2007 In Lubbock's, Louise Hopkins Underwood Center for the Arts.
 

Brittany is a Lubbock native and is the daughter of Terri & Homer Morse and Jay Barnett of Lubbock. She is a Lubbock-Cooper High School Senior where she is a Varsity Head Cheerleader and a Varsity Basketball player. She also won the Talent  competition for the pageant.

Brittany was awarded a variety of gifts and services including an educational scholarship, a wardrobe, makeup and hair care consultations, nail care, tanning & weight control services, a photographic portfolio, and more. She will travel to Fort Worth, Texas in July 2007 where she represent Lubbock in the 2007 Miss Texas Pageant.
