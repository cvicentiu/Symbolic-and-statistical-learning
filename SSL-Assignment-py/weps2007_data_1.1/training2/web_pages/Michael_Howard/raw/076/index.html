Guide Review
The Invention of Peace by Michael Howard
Don't be deceived by the size of this slim volume, for the content is a marvelous rumination - by an eminent historian - on the nature, and possible modern understanding, of the notion that is 'peace'. This is a work of broad concepts rather than single moments from the past, but required reading for all those interested in current larger world, as well as broad historical, affairs.
