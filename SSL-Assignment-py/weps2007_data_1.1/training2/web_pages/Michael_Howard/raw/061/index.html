Profile

Location: Canyon Lake, CA
My Story: I am involving myself in politics for the first time. While I have always been politically minded, I have recently decided to become politically active as well: I do not feel that America as a nation, nor as people, is being properly served by those we place in position of power. It is my desire to locate and support a politician that truly has the best interests of America at heart and exhibits this in action, or, failing in that, becoming one myself. In short it is my position that America today, our nation, our people, desperately needs a champion.
