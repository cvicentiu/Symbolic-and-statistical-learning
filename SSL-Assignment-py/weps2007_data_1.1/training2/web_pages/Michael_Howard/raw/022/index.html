Michael Howard

From SourceWatch

For Michael Howard the professor, see Michael Howard (professor)

Michael Howard is the current leader of the Conservative Party, and was generally 'acclaimed' leader after Iain Duncan Smith was sacked. Under John Major he had been Home Secretary, after being Employment Secretary and Environment Secretary. He was quite unpopular at the time.

He is a member of the America APPG, and the Australia and New Zealand APPG. He is president of Atlantic Partnership.
