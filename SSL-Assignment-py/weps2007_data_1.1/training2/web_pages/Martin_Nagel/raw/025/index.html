Sentra And Toro Announce Completion Of Plan Of Arrangement And Property Acquisition
Sep-10-2003
CALGARY, ALBERTA--Sentra Resources Corporation ("Sentra") and Toro Energy Inc. ("Toro") announced today that they have completed their previously announced statutory plan of arrangement (the "Arrangement") pursuant to which Sentra acquired all of the issued and outstanding common shares of Toro. The Arrangement was overwhelmingly approved by securityholders of both Sentra and Toro at special meetings of the securityholders of both companies held on September 9, 2003. The final order of the Court of Queen's Bench of Alberta approving the Arrangement was also granted on September 9, 2003.
 
In connection with the Arrangement, the Toronto Stock Exchange (the "TSX") has conditionally approved the listing of the Sentra common shares under the trading symbol "SRA". Upon Sentra satisfying the TSX's listing conditions, the TSX will issue a bulletin announcing the commencement of trading of the Sentra common shares. Following listing on the TSX, application will be made to delist the Toro common shares from the TSX Venture Exchange.
 
In connection with the Arrangement, Sentra is pleased to welcome Christina M. Fehr and Donald J. Rowden, former directors of Toro, to the Sentra Board of Directors. Ms. Fehr and Mr. Rowden join the existing Board of Directors of Sentra consisting of Uldis Upitis, Gerald M. Deyell, Q.C., Carl-Martin Nagel and Daniel M. Weingeist.
 
The issued and outstanding share capital of Sentra is currently comprised of 21,161,807 common shares, 1,401,749 options to acquire common shares and 133,028 warrants to acquire common shares. All of the warrants will expire 90 days from the date hereof and 541,749 of the options will expire 30 days from the date hereof.
 
Concurrently with the closing of the Arrangement, Sentra completed the previously announced acquisition of complementary working interests in oil and gas properties owned by private company vendors that had conducted joint venture operations with Toro. This acquisition has increased Sentra's working interest by 30% in the assets of Toro in the Peace River Arch area of Alberta and British Columbia.
 
Sentra is a junior oil and gas exploration company actively engaged in the exploration, development and acquisition of crude oil and natural gas. Current production is approximately 1000 - 1100 boe/d, weighted 70% towards natural gas, with an additional estimated 500 boe/d of oil and natural gas currently being tied in from properties located at Valhalla and Knobhill.
