ARP Synod Meeting on Erskine Campus Ends Today

Sisters Julia and Abby Watkins (above, left to right) wave farewell as the 2003 Bicentennial Meeting of the Synod of the Associate Reformed Presbyterian Church ended today. Topics covered included accepting Erskine's requested slate of trustees and allocating to Erskine more than $500,000, representing 20 percent of available denominational funds.
