Young Dawkins A Slugger In Training! 6/10

Who'd a thunk it? Johnny Dawkins is taking his son to boxing classes.  He says - correctly - it's a fine discipline and good for building confidence and self-esteem.  One hopes Sean loses interest in it before he gets to the serious level of boxing, where passion often leads to brain injuries.  Incidentally, a superb book on boxing is one by Joyce Carol Oates, called, we think, On Boxing.  She's an odd but passionate fan, and one who believes that boxing has become America's tragic theater.
