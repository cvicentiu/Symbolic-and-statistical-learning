REGINA RESS tonight brings stories from her recent wanderings through Latin America & Spain. Armen Donelian, piano;  Jay Clayton, voice
Pianist/composer ARMEN DONELIAN broke into the Jazz world in 1975 in Mongo Samtamaria's vibrant Afro-Cuban octet, spent time in the bands of Sonny Rollins, Chet Baker, and Billy Harper, and has earned international praise for his work as a leader in formats from solo piano to sextet with Eddie Gomez, Billy Hart, Dick Oatts, Thomas Chapin, Bill Stewart and others. Tonight, he is joined by vocalist JAY CLAYTON, an impeccable musician whose collaborations for over 40 years with Kirk Nurock, Fred Hersch, Ed Neumeister, Stanley Cowell, Muhal Richard Abrams and others have also earned her kudos from around the globe.
