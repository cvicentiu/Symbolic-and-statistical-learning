Your generosity has made it possible

 

Dear Friends,

I am honored to report that The Next Horizon: The Campaign for the University of New Hampshire has surpassed its goal two years ahead of schedule. Our total of $102,333,521 is a remarkable achievement that holds enormous promise for UNH. Your generous support is at the core of this great success.

The attainment of our fundraising goal says many important things about the University of New Hampshire and the important role we have to play in its future. When we join together, we are capable of setting a positive and constructive course and effecting important change on behalf of UNH.

As we welcome Ann Weaver Hart as the 18th president of the University, our success in this most ambitious undertaking is a clear demonstration of our belief in the value of a UNH education. Already, we are looking ahead to new vistas and opportunities with enthusiasm and confidence that we will be able to meet the challenges that lie ahead.

I urge you to visit campus soon. I am sure that you will be impressed with its vitality and purpose. I hope as well that you will visit us at www.foundation.unh.edu for news and information as we continue to build a strong future for the University of New Hampshire.

Sincerely,
Young Dawkins III
