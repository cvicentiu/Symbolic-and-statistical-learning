Welcome to the all new and improved web site of the DALnet IRC channel #Aviation! Currently #Aviation is celebrating it's four year anniversary! I would like to thank all the people who have helped out with the channel as operators and have stayed with it to make it a fun place where people the world over can enjoy chatting with other aviation professionals and flight enthusiasts.

As always, this site was created for #Aviation regulars and anyone interested in #Aviation, Internet Relay Chat, and the people who operate it. The *official* home page of #Aviation on DALnet is The Aviation Home Page developed and maintained by Garret Wilkerson. If you haven't already been to this exciting and information-packed site, be sure to visit.
