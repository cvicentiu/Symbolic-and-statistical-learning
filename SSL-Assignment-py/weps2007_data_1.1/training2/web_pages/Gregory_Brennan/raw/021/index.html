Board of Directors

 

In January 2004, the ASTech Foundation confirmed these representatives as the Board of Directors (listed with executive committee first then alphabetical by last name):

 

  • E. H. Guy Mallabone, SAIT (ASTech Chair)
  • Martin P. J. Kratz, Bennett Jones LLP, (ASTech Vice-Chair, Resource Development and AHTGT Chair)
  • Carmen Forster, Alberta Research Council (ASTech Past Chair)
  • Anne Thomas, Alberta Ingenuity Fund (ASTech Awards Chair)
  • Lynn Sutherland, iCORE (ASTech Gala Chair)
  • Jason Darrah, University of Alberta (ASTech Communications Chair)
  • Fred Stewart, ASTech Executive Director and Strategic Planning/Patron Chair*
  • Gregory Brennan, KPMG
  • Jim Fleury, Western Economic Diversification Canada
  • Glenn Guenther, Alberta Science and Research Authority (ASRA)
  • Jacek Jarmoc, Nortel Networks
  • Peter Kinash, independent business representative
  • Ed Knash, AVAC Ltd.
  • John W. Masters, Calgary Technologies Inc.
  • Gary Otto, Dow AgroSciences Canada Inc.
  • Dr. Roger Pederson, TELUS Corporation
  • Christine Picken, University of Lethbridge
  • Elaine Sagan, ATCO Electric Ltd.
  • Barbara Shumsky, Syncrude Canada Ltd.
  • Dr. Pamela Sokol, University of Calgary
  • Mark Taylor, Alberta Heritage Foundation for Medical Research (AHFMR)

    

*ending June 2004
