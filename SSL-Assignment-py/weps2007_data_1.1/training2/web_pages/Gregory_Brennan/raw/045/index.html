Greg Brennan

  • Dip.Ex.Sci. CNZ L2
  • Martial Arts Instructor

Greg has worked within the fitness industry for the last five years during which he has taken on roles such as fitness consultant, personal trainer, sales consultant, martial arts trainer, nutrition consultant, group training instructor, duty/fitness manager and assistant health spa manager. Over this time, Greg has helped hundreds of people achieve their fitness goals and has gained in-depth knowledge and experience, both in New Zealand and overseas, in a multitude of fitness and exercise disciplines.

Greg has a no-nonsense approach to fitness training, which is reflected in his passion for physical development and his enthusiasm to share this passion with others.

Training is about pushing the boundaries past what you believe to be possible and this is only achieved through a combination of hard work, perseverance and applying proven scientific principles to your training. Anything less is a waste of time, energy and money.

If you have a real desire to get in shape and are serious about accelerating your results, effective personal training will take you to that next level of fitness success.
