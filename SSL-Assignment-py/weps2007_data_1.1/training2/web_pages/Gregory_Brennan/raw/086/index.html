Quote:
Originally Posted by beowulf7
Both the 2.0L S2000 and the 2.2L S2000 have (had) 240 HP? What was the torque improvement? I think the new 2.2L has 162 or 165 ft-lb. I'm guessing the 2.0L was in the 150s.


Well, look at it like this

2.0 WHP = 195-205
2.2 WHP = 215-230

Basically if we take an average of 200 HP for the 2.0, that assumes a 17% drivetrain loss. So an average of 223 HP for the 2.2, gives the car about 268 crank horsepower.

So I think thats a pretty good improvement.

In terms of torque, the 2.2 makes about 12-20 ft-lbs more at any given point in the rev range over the 2.0.
