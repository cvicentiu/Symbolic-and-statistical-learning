JUNIOR GOLF


Keep it simple

Start your child with a baseball grip and let them swing naturally.  Early on, it's more important to have fun than to develop swing mechanics.  

 

At about age eight, kids are able to understand and enjoy learning the mechanics of the swing.

 

 

 

Be patient

Golf is a game for a lifetime and can be an excellent way to develop relationships within a family.  

 

Kids get tired after only a few shots, so be patient with the progress; it's worth the investment of time.
