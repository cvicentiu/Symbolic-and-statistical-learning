BNY Securities Group
The Power of Choice

Over the past few years, The Bank of New York has greatly enhanced its brokerage and clearing capabilities through both targeted acquisitions and internal growth. This focused strategy is part of our continued efforts to provide clients with the resources and highly personalized service required to succeed in the investment marketplace. The result? BNY Securities Group – a sector of the Bank comprising our extensive range of institutional agency brokerage, clearing and financial services outsourcing businesses.

BNY Securities Group seeks to maintain a leadership role in the industry by creating a new breed of institutional agency brokerage. Through our broad range of brokerage, clearing and financial services outsourcing organizations, we offer creative solutions in trade execution, commission management, independent research, securities clearing and financial services outsourcing including web development, and more. We call this suite of combined capabilities, “The Power of Choice”.

So whatever your business model, BNY Securities Group has the right choice for you: 

  • BNY Brokerage  –  institutional trade execution and soft dollar services
  • Pershing – clearing and financial services outsourcing 
  • BNY Wholesale Execution – wholesale trade execution for market intermediaries and direct access for institutions
  • BNY Direct Execution –direct access to NYSE-listed securities
  • BNY Plan Services – commission recapture for plan sponsors
  • BNY Global Transition Management – transition management services
  • Westminster Research (WRA) – commission management and soft dollars through an introducing broker
  • BNY Jaywalk – independent, third-party research consultancy and on-line delivery platform
  • B-Trade – electronic execution for U.S. equities through the Bloomberg Tradebook System
  • G-Trade – electronic execution for global equities through the Bloomberg Tradebook System
  • G- Port – electronic execution of basket and portfolio trading in the global markets

BNY Securities Group provides the power of choice through global agency brokerage and correspondent clearing business models that expand and differentiate their offerings to institutions, broker-dealers and corporations. BNY Securities Group includes a global institutional agency brokerage organization, a top correspondent clearing firm, the world’s largest institutional electronic broker for global execution and the leading provider of commission management services, with a global presence through offices in the U.S., Europe and Asia. The Group’s member firms together have 17 New York Stock Exchange seats and offer global execution and clearing services in over 65 markets with offices in the U.S., Europe and Asia.
