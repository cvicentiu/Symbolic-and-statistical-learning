More About This Site:

1. About Us:

We are a group of book lovers. This site was created for other book lovers. We still remember the old days. We run into different bookstores / bookshops / book sellers in town, tried to compare book prices, tried to find a coupon, tried to find discount books / discount textbooks / cheap books / used books for sale, tried to find half price books, and tried to get the best book deal / book sales / bargain books. Now it's Internet age, you don't need to physically run into different online book stores / book shops / book sellers to do cheap book price search and comparison. However you still need to check different online bookstores' web sites. It's quite time consuming. Different online book stores / book sellers / book dealers / book shops, have different channels, different inventory level, and cost structure. There are lots of discounted books and cheap books there. Discounted bookstores / book shops / book sellers constantly put selected cheap books on sale. However people need to visit different site one by one, compare book price, and to search the best book price for the bargain books. It's really hard to follow those discounted books on sale. Our goal is to help people find the lowest book price more efficiently. So we created this search all discount book site. All you need to do now is to type in the book you want, click the button, and let our computer program run into different store / shop / seller sites to fetch price information, do book price comparison, and get the best book buy for you. Going one step further, our computer programs also get book availability, find book coupons, and calculate tax and shipping cost.

2. How does your program achieve this auto book price comparison?

Our computer program runs in a parallel fashion. After our site receives your search book price request, a bunch of subtask programs are lunched. Each of the subprogram is responsible for searching a best book deal at one bookstore / book shop / book seller / book marketplace. The book search includes new books, used books / used textbooks / used text books, and out of print books. Then all the search results are compared, sorted by total price, and finally displayed. You just need to click the "Buy" link to go to a bookstore / bookshop / book seller site to further look at the book detail or complete the book purchase.

If you are searching and try to find a web site to automatically check all the bookstores / book shops, compare the price, and find the best book deal for you, you have come to the right place. Or more specifically, if you go to a search engine and have any of those search phases in mind, "buy text books, buy college books, college textbooks, used college textbooks, cheap college textbooks, college text books, cheap textbooks, buying low price books, buy used books online, buy used textbooks, buy textbooks, buy book online, harry potter book 6, book summaries, book reviews, medical books, used medical books, medical textbooks, book club price, bargain books, buy book uk, used textbooks uk, buy book online uk, buy book Canada, used textbooks Canada, or buy book online Canada", then you have come to the right place. This site is devoted to book lovers as a quick discount book, low price book, and half price book finder. You can use this site to find the best deals for all kinds of books including new books / books in print, used books / second hand books, out of print books, rare to find books / hard to find books, antique books, and audio books / books on cd / electronic books. We have a huge book catalog, and you will find almost any books here. We will search the best book prices for you.

4. Compare to other book finder site, what are your strong points?

Firstly, we support search books from a large book catalog collections. You should notice that relatively a large number of books show up after you do a popular keyword, title, or author search. This provides a much wider option for user to choose. Secondly, our site is designed to find discount books, and best book deal worldwide. Currently we do search from new & used book stores / book shops in US, UK, Canada, Germany, France, and Japan. Accordingly, search result can be displayed in different currency. Thirdly, we know the most important thing is to find the lowest book price for user. Therefore, we go one step further to fetch all available book coupons, and calculate the corresponding saving. The final cost is automatically calculated after consideration of book price, shipping cost, tax, and coupon saving.
