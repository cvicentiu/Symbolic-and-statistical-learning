Paul Collins: new missionary with Campus Bible Fellowship

Sending Church: First Baptist Church, Elyria, Ohio

 

While attending community college, God gave Paul a burden to share his faith with his fellow students and professors. He transferred to Bible college, desiring to train to be a more useful tool in the Lord’s hands. 

 

Paul had sensed God’s call to serve since he was eight years old, but during his senior year in college he struggled to know if God would place him in ministry. Learning of Campus Bible Fellowship, a ministry that opens the Bible to American and international students, Paul found a ministry to match his experiences and heart.

 

At his home church, Paul connected with BMM administrator Rev. Steve Fulks, who was scheduled to speak at the Cleveland chapter of CBF, coordinated by missionary Mary Amesbury.  That meeting led to great things—Paul eventually found a ministry and a wife. He and Mary plan to be married in November and to minister together in Cleveland.

 

To contact Paul or to have him at your church for a meeting, click on his speaker information or email his field administrator, Rev. Steve Butler, at the link to the right.
