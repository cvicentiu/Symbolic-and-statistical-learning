   Acerca de Paul Collins
I edited my profile with Thomas' Myspace Editor V4.4Paul Collins' Beat can be heard on the Caddyshack official movie soundtrack. Paul's first band, The Nerves, formed in 1974 and is considered one of the early U.S. punk groups. Led by Jack Lee, their song "Hanging On The Telephone" was recorded by Deborah Harry and Blondie on the album entitled "Parallel Lines. "When The Nerves disbanded, Jack Lee went solo, Peter Case formed The Plimsouls and Paul Collins formed The Beat. In his bands The Nerves and The Beat, Paul Collins has toured with numerous acts, including The Ramones, The Jam, The Police, Pere Ubu, Ian Gomm, Huey Lewis, Eddie Money and countless other names. Paul Collins Beat continues to tour the world, concentrating primarily on the United States, Europe and Japan.
