Artist Biography

Paul Collins Beat contains Paul Collins from The Nerves. One of the first U.S. punk bands, The Nerves wrote and recorded Hanging On The Telephone, a hit song later recorded by Deborah Harry and Blondie. The Nerves included Peter Case, Jack Lee and Paul Collins. When The Nerves disbanded, Paul Collins formed The Beat and Peter Case would go on to form The Plimsouls. Paul Collins has had much success, touring with the likes of The Ramones, The Jam, Sting And The Police, Pere Ubu, Huey Lewis And The News and Nikki Corvette among others. To avoid confusion with The English Beat, Paul Collins changed the name of his band to Paul Collins Beat. Paul Collins Beat has reformed and continues to record new material and tour the world.

Artist Mission


Paul Collins Beat promises to continue playing high energy rock and roll and punk rock in the style of The Ramones.

Music Influences


Ray Charles, Tommy James, The Beatles, Willie Nile, Sex Pistols, Paul McCartney, John Lennon, 60s motown
