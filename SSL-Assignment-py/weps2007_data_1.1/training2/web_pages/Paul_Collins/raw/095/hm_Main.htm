Collins Industries
Collins Industries is one of Western Canada’s leading providers of structural steel.  With over 25 years experience in the structural steel fabrication industry, Collins Industries has established relationships with clients throughout Western Canada and as far away as Russia.

Collins Industries works on some of the biggest and most prominent projects in Western Canada. Over the past 5 years, Collins Industries has worked on the Winspear Centre for the Performing Arts, Belvedere LRT Station, The Chinook Shopping Centre in Calgary, and the southeast Air Terminal Expansion at the Edmonton International Airport to name a few.

Collins Industries maintains its competitive advantage by focusing on its core competencies. By continually upgrading its manufacturing processes and encourages its' personnel to upgrade their skills, Collins Industries has the expertise to help you.
Collins is pleased to announce that it upgraded its ISO standard on Oct 8, 2003 from ISO 9002:1994 to ISO 9001:2000. The effect of this is that every division of Collins Industries including estimating, drafting, production, shipping and erection is monitored to ensure its customers receive a quality product.
