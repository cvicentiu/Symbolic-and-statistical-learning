  • gpc

Haha! Valmorel is no more! (yipeee) We've outgrown the sleepy slopes of Valmorel and the Christmas trip has moved to L'Alpe d'Huez!
It's a much bigger resort with more challenging slopes for those of us that are getting better (while still having loads of the more gentle slopes for the less experienced/less nutty amongst us), potentially a hell of a lot more good quality off piste for exploring and a lot more bars to go out in!

Dates for this year are: 23rd of december to the 6th of January.
Those driving will leave late on friday the 22nd of December and leave on tuesday the 2nd of January.
