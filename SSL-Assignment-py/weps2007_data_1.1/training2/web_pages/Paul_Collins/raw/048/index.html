Biography
Alongside Peter Case and Jack Lee, Paul Collins was a founding member of legendary U.S. punk rock band The Nerves. The Nerves originally wrote and recorded the song Hanging On The Telephone, later recorded by Deborah Harry and Blondie. After The Nerves disbanded, Peter Case formed The Plimsouls and Paul Collins formed The Beat, also known as Paul Collins' Beat. Between his solo project, The Paul Collins Band and Paul Collins' Beat, he to tour and record new material in addition to touring, playing dates in Japan, USA, France, Australia, Germany, UK, Sweden, Italy and Spain. Catch The Paul Collins Band and Paul Collins' Beat on tour in a city near you!
