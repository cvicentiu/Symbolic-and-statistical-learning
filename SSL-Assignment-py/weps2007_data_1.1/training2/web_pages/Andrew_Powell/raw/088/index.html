Late basket lifts Lancers by Colts.(Sports)
Publication Date: 04-JAN-05
Publication Title: The Register-Guard (Eugene, OR)
Format: Online
Subscription to Research: $89.95/month (learn more)

NOTE: All illustrations and photos have been removed from this article.

Full Article
Byline: The Register-Guard

Andrew Powell's only basket of the game was a three-pointer with 25 seconds left to lift Churchill to a 57-56 Midwestern League boys basketball victory over visiting Thurston on Monday.

The Colts turned the ball over in the final seconds before they could get a shot off.

Chris Mackovjak had 14 points to lead four Lancers in double figures. Jason Bell had 24 points for Thurston.

Matt Bramow scored 28 points and JR Morgan and Trenton Newton-Jones each added 11 to help South Eugene defeat host Sheldon 65-56.

Alex Brock scored 12 of his 28 points in the fourth quarter to help North Eugene remain unbeaten in league play with a 62-43 victory over Marshfield at Coos Bay.

Bo Shrum and James Clark each scored 17 points and Springfield sealed a 59-58 victory over host Willamette from the free-throw line in the fourth quarter.

Far West League

Logan Wechter had 17 points and Erik Pflaum added 15 to lead Coquille to a 58-53 victory over visiting Brookings.

Zach Bailey had 15 points and seven rebounds and Justin Parnell added 10 to lead Sutherlin to a 41-32 victory over visiting South Umpqua.

Michael Bishop scored a game-high 16 points and Andrew Grief added 10 points in North Bend's 62-45 victory over Douglas at Winston.

Girls

Far West League

Jannee Olds scored 19 points, and No. 3 North Bend stymied No. 7 Douglas in the fourth quarter for a 39-29 victory in the teams' league opener at Winston.

Monique Tribble had 21 points and 13 rebounds to lead Coquille to a 48-46 victory over visiting Brookings.

Dinah George had 13 points to lead Sutherlin (9-1) to a 44-12 victory over visiting South Umpqua.
