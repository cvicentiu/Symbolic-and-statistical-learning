Andrew Powell

Class:
Senior
Hometown:
North Easton, MA
High School:
Oliver Ames
Birthdate:
05/23/1980

Stanford: Seeking to recover from injuries that have plagued him the last several years.

2002 Cross Country Season (Junior): Did not compete.

2001 Cross Country Season: Redshirted.

2000 Cross Country Season (Sophomore): A member of the Cardinal teams that finished fourth at the NCAA Championships and first at the Pac-10 Championships.

1999 Cross Country Season (Freshman): Competed in two races (BYU Invitational, Pre-NCAA's) ... Finished fourth at the BYU Invitational after which an injury ended his season early.

2003 Track & Field Season (Junior): Did not compete.

2002 Track & Field Season (Sophomore): Competed twice in the 1500 meters during an injury plagued season.

2001 Track & Field Season: Redshirted.

2000 Track & Field Season (Freshman): Gained entry into the NCAA's with a time of 3:43.1 in the 1500 meters ... The top-ranked freshman in the NCAA's at 1500 meters ... Ran the 5000 meters at the Pacific-10 Championships with an eighth place time of 14:18.75 ... In the summer of 2000, he lowered his time in the 1500 meters to 3:40.65, narrowly missing a spot at the U.S. Olympic Trials ... Finished the year ranked 21st in the United States in the 1500 meters.

High School: Enjoyed a tremendous running career at Oliver Ames High School ... Ran 4:02.7 in the mile, the fastest prep performance in New England in 33 years and seventh-fastest time on the all-time national High School outdoor list ... Finished first at the 1999 U.S. Junior National Championships in the 1500 meters (3:49.81) and the 5000 meters (14:51.81) ... Won the gold medal in the 1500 meters at the 1999 Junior Pan American Games ... The 1999 Golden West Invitational champion in the mile ... Ran 4:07.35 in the mile, the fastest prep indoor time in 1999 ... Ran 8:50.29 in the two-mile at the 1999 state indoor meet ... The 1999 Massachusetts state meet champion in the mile (4:06) ... The 1998 mile champion at the Millrose Games and the Penn Relays ... The Massachusetts state cross country champion ... Ran a course record (Franklin Park) 14:52 over 5000 meters ... Competed at the 1999 Footlocker Nationals in Florida.

Cardinal Catalogue: American Studies major ... Parents are Bernie and Sheryl Powell ... Born May 23, 1980.
