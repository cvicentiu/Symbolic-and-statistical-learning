The Scoop On: "The Bachelor"
By: W. Andrew Powell on Apr 24, 03 @ 8:52 am

Citytv's "Water Cooler Weekly": All the Scoop You Need to Know About: "The Bachelor" - from April 23rd.

On last night's episode:

WHO GOT A ROSE:
"Tina Fabulous" (big surprise since she wouldn't even let him kiss her on the lips!), Kirsten, Jennifer S. and Cristina C.

WHAT EVERYONE'S TALKING ABOUT THIS MORNING:
Cristina. She's aggressive, sensitive and crazy in love with a man she hardly knows. Throughout the show , she complained about not having any one-on-one time and that she doesn't do well on the group dates. When the opportunity arose to talk to Andrew alone, he confessed he finds her very aggressive, much more than the other ladies. Of course, everyone's talking about Liz's "performance" after she learned she wasn't getting a rose. "I have nothing left to give." She should get an Academy Award!

HOW TO ENTERTAIN "FIRESTONE STYLE":
Tennis anyone? Andrew arrived by limo to pick up the three girls for a group date. Cristina, Liz and "Tina Fabulous" arrived at the country club, looking cute in their new tennis outfits. Cristina's game got better as the day went on, "Tina Fabulous" had the most natural ability, and Liz definitetly had the energy, but no coordination.

ANDREW AND HIS ONE-ON-ONE'S:
Jennifer S. and Andrew took a helicopter to a spa, Anne-Michelle and Andrew enjoyed a fondue, but Kristen and Andrew had the date to remember. Theirs was a "classic roadster" theme, complete with a trip to the drive-in. While sipping champagne in the car, they watched home movies and photos from their childhood on the big screen. They learned a lot about each other, and Andrew confessed he thinks love is definitely happening.

THE ONE TO WATCH:
Jennifer S., she's fun, active and intelligent. Andrew has mentioned on many occasions that he feels really comfortable around her. This 26 year-old could easily become the next Mrs. Andrew Firestone!

EDITOR'S NOTE:
Isn't anyone else gonna miss Liz besides me? It was like she was the only sweetheart and the rest of the 'ladies' are all just a bunch of jackals or something. Maybe she was too sweet for Andrew though, but still, she was awesome!

Don't miss next week's episode of "The Bachelor" Wednesday, April 30th starting at 9:00pm ET/PT on Citytv Toronto and Citytv Vancouver (simulcast with ABC)... where else?
