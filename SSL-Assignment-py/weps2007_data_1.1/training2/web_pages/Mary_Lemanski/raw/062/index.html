  Invasion of the GoGirls
March 18, 19 & 20, 2004 - Austin, TX
A GoGirls - SXSW party weekend! NO BADGES OR WRISTBANDS REQUIRED!

GoGirlsMusic.com proudly presents the 4th annual "Invasion of the GoGirls" in Austin, TX during South by Southwest music conference weekend. We'll be showcasing the best emerging talent in our five "unofficial" showcases. Plus our first ever music panels and keynote speaker! click here for current press release...

SEE PHOTOS FROM SXSW!

GoGirls Buzz Band 2004 - Breech
Through sales of baked goods outside various music venues in the L.A. area, Breech raised $7,000 to record their current release, "Apron Strings". read more...

GoGirls Buzz Artist 2004 - Kelly Z
For the past 4 years, Kelly Z has used her music to raise awareness about hepatitis c in her hometown of Los Angeles and around the country. read more...
