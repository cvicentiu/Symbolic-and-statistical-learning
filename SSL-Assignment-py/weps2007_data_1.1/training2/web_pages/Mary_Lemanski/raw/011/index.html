Mary Lemanski is an independent singer/songwriter/musician. After high school, Mary earned a degree in Music Business with a concentration in Musical Composition and an emphasis in piano and voice from Millikin University. In the summer of 1999, Mary completed studies in audio engineering at the Conservatory of Recording Arts & Sciences. During that summer, she interned at BMG Entertainment and Epitaph Records. Ms. Lemanski is an active participant in the independent music scene in Phoenix, Arizona, and she has opened for acts such as Marky Ramone & the Speed Kings, D.I., and Val Emmich. In addition to hosting the annual GoGirlsMusicFest (www.gogirlsmusicfest.com), she is also involved with the Phoenix chapter of Just Plain Folks (www.jpfolks.com) and Songsalive! (www.songsalive.org). Mary recently placed runner-up in VH1's Song of the Year Contest, had a Top 10 Hit on Broadjam.com's New Wave charts, and is currently writing new music and recording tracks for a full-length CD.
