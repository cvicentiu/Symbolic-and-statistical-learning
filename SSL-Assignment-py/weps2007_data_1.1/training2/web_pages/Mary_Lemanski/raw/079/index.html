Review from GoGirlsMusic.com
 
Loni Pont
"Pieces"
Reviewed by Mary Lemanski
 
If you are looking for good solid pop-rock, check out Loni Pont. She has a distinct, clear, beautiful voice. The backing band is phenonmenal in the interpretation of her music. Her musical style reminds me of a cross between Don Henley and Sarah MacLachlan.
 
Hit Picks : "Pieces" and "Lyla"
