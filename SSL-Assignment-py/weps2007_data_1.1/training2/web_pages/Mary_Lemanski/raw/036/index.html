   About Who's That Records
Who's That Records is an independent record label who thrives in the heart of the growing Arizona music scene. Representing mostly the punk genre of music but not limited to just this sound. Established in March of 2001 and growing more every year. Our staff is dedicated to helping up and coming musicians advance at what they love most. We specialize in many areas that can help musicians. We offer some studio time expenses, promotion, distribution, CD duplication and more. Once you're part of Who's That we do all that we can to help you succeed. Our goal is to take our bands and the label to the next level and be more than just a local. If you'd like more info on getting signed contact us. We also need street team to promote our bands. To join visit our website www.whosthatrecords.com
