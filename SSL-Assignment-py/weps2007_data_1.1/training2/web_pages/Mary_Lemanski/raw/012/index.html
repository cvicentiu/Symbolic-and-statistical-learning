Home of Painted Girl Productions and Painted Girl Records

 

        PaintedGirl.com had its beginnings in 1999 as home to Painted Girl Productions. It was created by indie singer/songwriter/pianist, Mary Lemanski, as a means to promote and produce her own music. In November 1999, Painted Girl Productions released its first demo/EP, Mary Lemanski's "Six Songs Just For You."  In March 2001, Painted Girl Productions released "Open Dress Rehearsal," which includes demo versions of most of the songs on Mary's latest upcoming release as well as some that did not make the cut.  In December 2001, they released a live demo/EP, "Mary Lemanski & The Addicktives - Live at the Phoenix Hard Rock Cafe," with Mary performing with a local Phoenix area punk n' roll band, The Addicktives, playing backup.  Mary Lemanski and The Addicktives now perform under the name Mary Lemanski & the Arrangements.  Mary is currently writing new music and recording tracks for a full-length CD.  The date of release is not yet known.

        PaintedGirl.com will soon be restructuring and expanding its operations.  Painted Girl Records was created to handle the promotion, production, and distribution of album releases.  Painted Girl Productions will now handle booking and promotion of live events for Painted Girl Records.  Painted Girl Productions will also soon be offering booking and promotion for small or unknown touring musical acts.  Please email PaintedGirlPro@yahoo.com for more information.
