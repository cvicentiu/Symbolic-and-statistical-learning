DESCRIPTION:
Female singer/songwriter/pianist, Mary Lemanski, has been playing professionally since age 12. She has played in everything from churches to pubs and everything in between. Ms. Lemanski has also had experience as a studio musician. She can play virtually any style of music. Mary's original music can be described as a mixture of pop, rock, blues, jazz, and folk. She also composes contemporary classical music.
