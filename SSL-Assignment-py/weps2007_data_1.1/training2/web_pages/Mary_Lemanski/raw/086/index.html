Dana Cunningham
"Dancing at the Gate"
Genre: easy listening
reviewed by Mary Lemanski

This twelve song album is perfect for someone who enjoys the sound of a piano and needs music for meditation or relaxation. Dana Cunningham's piano melodies are beautiful and uplifting. Her songs are emotional and reflextive. A lovely album!
