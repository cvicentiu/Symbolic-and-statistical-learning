Organic Poetry ~ Art Detour
Saturday, March 6, 2004
“A sheer calamity of spoken word, music and art performance, a total da da escapade”

PHOENIX, Ariz. – Poetry, music and the visual arts collide in a collage of performance art installation known as "ORGANIC." Cleopache and Thought Crime Artspace present: "ORGANIC Art Detour Extravaganza" show at The Firehouse, 1015 North First Street, downtown Phoenix.

The multi-cultural arts presentation was directed by Cleopache and featured spoken word, dark & light music, puppet-trees, fire artists, belly-dancing, and art performance by:

The Light Bros. | The Bedouin Tribe | Tony & Rebecca | The UnTrained Circus
The Complainiacs | The Blue Food Poets | Mary Lemanski | Tracy Thomas
Windpipe | Joey Grether | Mr. Frip | Monica Rivas | Grateful Bill | Richard E. Moore

Included special performances featuring Thought Crime Resident-Artists:

Michael 23 | Joanna 23 | Lauren Sangre-See | Jim McKay | Mattoid | Rae Sin | Cleopache
