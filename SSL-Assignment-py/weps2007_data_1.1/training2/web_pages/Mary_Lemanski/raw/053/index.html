Join Us

We are currently recruiting bands to play the first annual LocalPalooza.  Ideally, we would like to have a great mix of dynamic bands who can all put on a great show for the scene.

Bands participating in Localpalooza are doing so for charity, and all persons involved are donating their time and efforts to charity. This means that bands who are expressing interest in playing this event on a paid basis need not apply. 

The following bands have already contacted us expressing their interest in performing at LocalPalooza. The band selection committee will be meeting later in the summer, and there is no guarantee that these bands have been selected to play. To be considered for selection, you must first fill out the form below.  If you do not see your name on the list and would like to volunteer your band to play, please fill out the form below and your band will be added to the list for consideration.

The list is sorted in alphabetical order.
