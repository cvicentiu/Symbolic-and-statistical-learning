Alfred Schroeder Field Airport

Alfred Schroeder Field Airport serves Hillsboro and Marion County and is owned by the City Of Hillsboro. The paved runway extends for 3206 feet. The facility is at an elevation of 1434 feet at a distance of about a mile or less from Hillsboro.
