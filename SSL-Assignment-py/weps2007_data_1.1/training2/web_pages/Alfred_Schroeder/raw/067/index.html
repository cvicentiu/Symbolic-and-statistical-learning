"Our Junior" Register Tribune
Des Moines, Iowa, May, 1929; Vol. 13; No. 5.

Abstracted by Judy Reustle, July 2001, Judy's Stuff

A monthly publication distributed to newspaper carriers employed by the Register and Tribune
Company, Des Moines, Iowa.; The Sunday Register, The Morning Register, The Evening Tribune-Capital.
"Our Aims: Fair treatment in all our dealing with carriers. Close co-operation for mutual benefit
in the promotion of business. A warm personal contact through these columns."
