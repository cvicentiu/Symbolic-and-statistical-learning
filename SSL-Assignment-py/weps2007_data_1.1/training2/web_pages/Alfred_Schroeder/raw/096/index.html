Mr. George F. Schroeder is a co-founder of the Company. He served as its Chief Executive Officer from 1967 until 2004, and as a director since 1967. His primary responsibilities involve managing the Company's joint venture investments. He is the brother of Alfred A. Schroeder, and is also a partner in Lancer Properties.
