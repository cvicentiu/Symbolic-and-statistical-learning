THE WEEK OF JUNE 3, 2004
Luncheon Was A Congressional Affair      The Ridgewood Property Owners & Civic Association was one of the member organizations represented when the Queens Civic Congress held its fourth biennial luncheon recently in Queens Village. As shown, Senator Charles Schumer and former Queens Borough President Claire Shulman were among the attendees. Pictured from left at the RPOCA table: (standing) RPOCA Vice President Charles Ober; Patricia Grayson, board member; Senator Schumer; RPOCA Vice President Alfred Schroeder; Shulman; RPOCA Counsel Paul Kerzner; (seated) RPOCA President Peter Comber; Virginia Comber and RPOCA Treasurer Angie Tantillo.
