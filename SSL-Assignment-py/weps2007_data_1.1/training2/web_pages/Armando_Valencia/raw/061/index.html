About Us

Point 3 Printing Company was founded by Armando Valencia. A young man with the motivation and ambition of one day providing endless printing services, exceptional customer service, and spending extra time on graphic designs that other printing companies lack.

This philosophy will allow Point 3 Printing (P3P™) to fill the gap of smaller companies limited to the quality and services they provide and large companies with huge overhead and lengthy production schedules. With our experience and commitmentP3P™ is small enough to give your project the personal attention to details, but large enough to provide a full range of quality printing services. We are eager to assist you in your next small or large printing project. Call us or stop in and we'll give you our promise to satisfy your tomorrow printing today.
