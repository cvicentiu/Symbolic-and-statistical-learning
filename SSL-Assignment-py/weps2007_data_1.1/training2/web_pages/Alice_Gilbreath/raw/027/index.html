Pisgah Cemetery, Located in Section 26, Township 32 South, Range 25 East, Polk County, Florida Driving Directions: From Ft. Meade: Go South on US Highway 17 to Bowling Green. Turn East on County Line Road at the Polk/Hardee County Line. Continue East 2 miles to Mt. Pisgah Road. Turn north and go 1.3 miles. Mt. Pisgah Baptist Church is on the west side of the road, and the cemetery is behind the church. Mt. Pisgah Baptist Church 6210 Mt. Pisgah Road Ft. Meade, FL 33841 Telephone 863-375-4409 Surveyed by: Teresa McGowin and Amy McGowin, 26 August 2000, copyright 2000; checked on 02 September 2000 by Teresa and Donnie McGowin. Notes: This survey is in two parts. The cemetery is divided into two sections. The older part of the cemetery is the western part of the cemetery. Since the rows in the older section are somewhat irregular,I have arranged the information in alphabetical order. The newer section is directly behind the church. These rows are straight, and have been surveyed beginning on the south end of the first row west of the church, and going north. At the present time, there are 5 rows in the new section. Many of the older stones are broken or too old and weathered to read. I have made every attempt to record the information accurately, and I apologize for any errors. Please contact me by e-mail at teresam@strato.net if you have any questions about the survey.
