Senator Arlen Specter and Congressman Todd Platts to Tour United Defense, L.P.
Monday - April 7, 2003 | 01:00 PM
York
EVENT: Senate Specter and Congressman Todd Platts will tour United Defense, L.P.

DATE: Monday, April 7, 2003

TIME: 1:00 p.m.

LOCATION: United Defense, L.P
1100 Bairs Road
York, PA 17404

Senator Arlen Specter, a member of the U.S. Senate Appropriations Subcommittee for Defense, and Congressman Todd Platts will tour United Defense, L.P. to discuss efforts to secure federal funding for UDLP's major programs. UDLP has 1395 employees and is the primary U.S. manufacturer and systems integrator for armored combat vehicles such as the Bradley Fighting Vehicle or Bradley AO to A2, the Improved Recovery Vehicle or M88A2 Hercules and the Paldin. The Bradley and the Improved Recovery Vehicle are widely used with great success today in Iraq.
