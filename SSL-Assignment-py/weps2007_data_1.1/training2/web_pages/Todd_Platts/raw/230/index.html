Congressional Child Care Caucus


The Congressional Child Care Caucus' mission is to generate Congressional and national awareness of the crisis in child care and to create support for a non-partisan child care agenda which will ensure access to quality and affordable child care for all American children. The Caucus will emphasize the positive impact that access to quality, affordable child care has on early child development and the overall well-being of our children. We will also focus on the impact that child care has on working families and the growth and strength of our economy.
