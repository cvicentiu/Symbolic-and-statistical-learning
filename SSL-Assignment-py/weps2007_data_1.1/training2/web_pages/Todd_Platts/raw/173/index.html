Please Note: We Do Not Provide Legal Services.

The Bazelon Center lacks the resources and specific knowledge of individual states’ laws to respond effectively to individual inquiries or requests for assistance. To obtain legal representation, please contact the protection and advocacy system in your state. Protection and advocacy (P&A) systems in each state are federally funded to assist people with mental or developmental disabilities in understanding and asserting their rights. To contact your state's P&A, please visit the website of their national association, http://www.napas.org.
