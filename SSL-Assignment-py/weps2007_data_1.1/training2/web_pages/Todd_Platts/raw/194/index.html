By: Barbara Kestler

We urge all citizens and especially those who are impoverished and/or minorities, to express themselves directly to legislators. You can change the politics of Pennsylvania. You pay these folks' salaries. Tell them what you think, ask them for help, make them earn their money.

Pennsylvania currently has 21 representatives in the United States Congress. Each supposedly represents a different district of the state. It's astonishing how many are so technically illiterate that they have no published email addresses.
