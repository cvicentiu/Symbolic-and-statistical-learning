Hi ,

This is to alert you concerning HR207 that was recently referred

Although this bills intent may be to limit the availability of

"chemical precursor" and "or is transformed in the body directly into

steroid" presents a real threat to natural hormones that are helping

considered a "chemical precursor" in the steroidogenesis pathway to
androstenedione - which I believe is the hormone teens are abusing and
the supposed reason for HR207.

Progesterone, natural/real progesterone (not the synthetic
progestins) is being used by many women to balance their hormones
naturally. Women are seeing great success reducing or eliminating hot
flashes, PMS, fibrocystic breasts, weight gain at hips and thighs,
water retention while restoring libido (sex drive), good sleep,
regular cycles. All naturally without the threat of cancer, heart
problems, stroke etc which are caused by synthetic HRT. Yet none of
these women are buiding muscle mass!!

The Controlled Substances Act was intended to address addictive drugs

covered by this bill meet these criteria. The DEA is currently

proper lawful procedures, and without any evidence of legitimate
public health concerns or dangers to American adults.

Please see to it that HR207 dies in committee, American women need
unrestricted access to natural progesterone.
