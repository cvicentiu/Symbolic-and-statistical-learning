Here you will find e-mail addresses for officials with influence over Educational policy and budget. I hope you will take the time to give an official or two (or twenty) a piece of your mind – or a piece of mine. Feel free to copy and paste one of the pre-formed letters I have already composed and mailed to the hundreds of addresses in this list, which I will update and enhance on a weekly basis.

The list begins with National & Regional associations and agencies, which are followed by officials organized by State. Officials include State Legislature representatives, State Regents, State Psychological Association Board Members, Departments of (or Commissions on) State Education, and even some University Faculty Senators. Just do a Control F and type in the name of your State! Hope you enjoy it!
