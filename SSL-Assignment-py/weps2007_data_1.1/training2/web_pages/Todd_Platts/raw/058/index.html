Congressional Activities for the 108th Congress
June 2-3, 2004


HEALTH AND RESEARCH AWARENESS

Members of Congress demonstrated interest in a variety of diseases and research areas relevant to NCI by introducing resolutions for consideration by the House and Senate.

HCR 206 - National Marrow Donor Program

  • A concurrent resolution introduced by Representative Michael Burgess (R-TX) on June 4, 2003 supporting the National Marrow Donor Program and other bone marrow donor programs and encouraging Americans to learn about the importance of bone marrow donation
  • Passed the House by 2/3rd majority vote on November 21, 2003
  • Received in the Senate on November 22, 2003
  • Referred to Senate Committee on Health, Education, Labor, and Pensions on December 9, 2003

HCR 317 - Mesothelioma Awareness

  • A concurrent resolution introduced by Representative Joseph Hoeffel (D-PA) on October 30, 2003 expressing the sense of the Congress with respect to the form of cancer known as mesothelioma
  • Referred to House Committee on Government Reform on October 30, 2003

HCR 322 - National Lung Cancer Awareness Month

  • A concurrent resolution introduced by Representative Clay Shaw (R-FL) on November 6, 2003 that designates the month of November as "National Lung Cancer Awareness Month" and expresses the need for an increase in Federal effort for lung cancer screening and research
  • Referred to House Committee on Energy and Commerce on November 6, 2003

HCR 362 - National Donor Day

  • A concurrent resolution introduced by Representative Jeff Miller (R-FL) on February 11, 2004 expressing the sense of the Congress regarding the importance of organ, tissue, bone marrow, and blood donation and supporting designation of National Donor Day on February 14, 2004
  • Referred to House Committee on Energy and Commerce on Feb. 11, 2004

HCR 405 - Prostate Cancer Information Access

  • Introduced by Representative Nathan Deal (R-GA) on April 2, 2004 and referred to House Committee on Energy and Commerce
  • Concurrent resolution expressing the sense of Congress with respect to the need to provide prostate cancer patients with meaningful access to information on treatment options, and for other purposes

H. Res. 262 - Pancreatic Cancer Awareness Month

  • Introduced by Representative Todd Platts (R-PA) and referred to the House Committee on Government Reform on June 9, 2003
  • A resolution supporting the goals and ideals of Pancreatic Cancer Awareness Month
  • Passed by 2/3 majority vote on October 8, 2003

H. Res. 376 - Commendation for Richard and Annette Bloch and the Bloch Cancer Foundation

  • A Resolution introduced by Representative Dennis Moore (D-KS) on September 23, 2003 and referred to House Committee on Energy and Commerce

H. Res. 403 - Recognizing the 30th Anniversary of the National Foundation for Cancer Research

  • A Resolution introduced by Representative Jim Cooper (D-TN) on October 16, 2003 and referred to House Committee on Energy and Commerce

H. Res. 641 - Supporting the Goals and Ideals of Pancreatic Cancer Awareness Month

  • A resolution introduced by Representative Todd Platts (R-PA) on May 12, 2004 and referred to the House Committee on Government Reform
  • A resolution supporting the goals and ideals of Pancreatic Cancer Awareness Month for November, 2004

H.R. 3438 - Gynecological Cancer Education and Awareness Act of 2003 - Johanna's Law

  • Introduced by Representative Sander Levin (D-MI) on November 4, 2003
  • A bill to provide for programs to increase the awareness and knowledge of women and health care providers about gynecological cancers
  • Referred to House Committee on Energy and Commerce on November 4, 2003

SCR 10 - Designating April 2003 as "Human Genome Month" and April 25 as "DNA Day"

  • Introduced by Senator Judd Gregg (R-NH) and passed in the Senate by unanimous consent on February 27, 2003
  • Received in the House on March 3, 2003
  • Commemorates the 50th anniversary of the elucidation of the double helical structure of DNA and the addition of the sequence of the human genome to the public domain

S. Res. 140 - A Resolution designating the week of August 10, 2003 as National Health Center Week.

  • Introduced by Senator Ben Nighthorse Campbell (R-CO) and referred to the Senate Committee on the Judiciary on May 9, 2003
  • Passed the Senate by unanimous consent on July 15, 2003

S. Res. 201- National Prostate Cancer Awareness Month

  • Introduced by Senator Jeff Sessions (R-AL) and passed the Senate by unanimous consent on July 25, 2003
  • A resolution designating the month of September 2003 as "National Prostate Cancer Awareness Month"

S. Res. 222 - National Mammography Day

  • A Resolution introduced on September 10, 2003 by Senator Joseph Biden (D-DE) designating October 17, 2003 as "National Mammography Day"
  • Passed Senate by unanimous consent on September 25, 2003

S. Res. 252 - National Cancer Prevention Month

  • A Resolution introduced by Senator Ernest Hollings (D-SC) on October 23, 2003 designating the month of February 2004 as "National Cancer Prevention Month"
  • Passed Senate by unanimous consent on October 23, 2003
  • To recognize research advances and the importance of the public knowing about such advances and taking advantage of early detection opportunities

S. Res. 305 - National Donor Day

  • A concurrent resolution introduced on February 23, 2004 by Senator Bill Nelson (D-FL) designating February 14,2004 as "National Donor Day"
  • Referred to Senate Committee on the Judiciary on February 23, 2004
