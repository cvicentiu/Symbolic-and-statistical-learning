National Housing Trust Fund in your Community

Endorsers participate in the campaign by taking action, reaching out to other organizations to promote the campaign, and offering feedback and ideas. Endorsers meet the first Thursday of the month on a conference call. For more information, contact Matt Achhammer at (202) 662-1530 x229 (matt@nlihc.org). To be involved, you must endorse the campaign.
