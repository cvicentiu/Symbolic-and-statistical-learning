Congressman Todd Platts visits York, comments on situation in Iraq


May 7, 2004

YORK -- U.S. Congressman Todd Platts greeted people in York County Friday.

He answered questions on a variety of subjects, including his time in Iraq.

The congressman visited U.S. Troops in Iraq in September, and says he never say any questionable behavior from either the Iraqis or the Americans.

Plats calls the video of the Iraqi prisoners "inexcusable" and says the troops responsible should received the harshest punishment allowed.
