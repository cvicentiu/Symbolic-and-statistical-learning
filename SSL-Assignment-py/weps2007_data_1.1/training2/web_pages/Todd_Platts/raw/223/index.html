H. Res. 641—Designating November as Pancreatic Cancer Awareness Month

On May 12, 2003, Representative Todd Platts (R-PA) introduced H. Res. 641, a resolution supporting the goals and ideals of Pancreatic Cancer Awareness Month. The measure was referred to the House Committee on Government Reform.
