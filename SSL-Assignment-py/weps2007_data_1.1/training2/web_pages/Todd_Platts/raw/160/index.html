Congressional Task Force on Responsible Fatherhood

Being a member of the Congressional Task Force on Responsible Fatherhood signifies a commitment to the responsible fatherhood movement and a devotion to supporting legislation that promotes and fosters responsible fatherhood. Members are invited to all NFI events that are held throughout the country, including the Annual National Summit on Fatherhood, and are regularly updated on any developments and new research findings relevant to the fatherhood movement.

The Congressional Task Force is chaired by Reps. Joseph Pitts (R-PA), Jim Turner (D-TX), and Mike McIntyre (D-NC), Robert Aderholt (R-AL), John Sullivan (R -OK), and Eleanor Holmes Norton (D - DC).
