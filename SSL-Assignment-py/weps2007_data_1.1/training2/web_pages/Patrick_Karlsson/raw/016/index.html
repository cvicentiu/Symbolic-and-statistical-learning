Who Am I?
I'm just a lactose tolerant guy in his mid twenties...

I was born and bred on the Swedish east coast. Just love the archipelago. Will probably end up in a small coast town eventually. But that's far in the future. I'm currently a student in Uppsala. A really nice 200k people city with a profound small town feeling.

Education
I've almost got a M.Sc. degree in engineering physics and have taken some pedagogy as well. I've spent a year in Scotland as a foreign student, which was one of my most memorable years this far. There's lots of pics from my year in Scotland to check out elsewhere on this page.

Future
I'm thinking about spending a few years in London. Doing what, I don't know. I just like it there and would enjoy the experience of having lived and worked there for a few years. We'll see if I get my ass over there when I'm through with university here. It would be easier just to take an ordinary job here in Sweden, but we'll se what happens when the day comes to decide.
