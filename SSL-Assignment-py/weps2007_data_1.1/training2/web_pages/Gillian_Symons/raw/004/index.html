This team of staff and consultants are supported by a range of committees and working groups including: Schools, Youth, Adult & Community, Higher Education, Training, DECs and Black Organisations. The membership of each group is drawn from representatives from member organisations and other key stakeholders in the relevant sector. They support the staff team on the focus and delivery of programmes of work. A 22 member Council/Board of Trustees, nominated from within and elected by the membership of the DEA, leads on the strategic direction of the Association.

The Association is also a network of networks. We work with our members and partners at a local, national and international level to promote the principles of development education and sustainable development as central to all sectors of education. Our member organisations in turn work closely with local, national, international and Southern partners.
