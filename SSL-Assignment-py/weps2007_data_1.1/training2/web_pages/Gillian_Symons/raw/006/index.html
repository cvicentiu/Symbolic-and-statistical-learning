Late Summer Offers

For August-September 2004 we are featuring a collection of remarkable series, all with special prices for this limited period.

OUP Biology of Habitats
This excellent series from Oxford University Press aims to provide attractive texts which give an integrated overview of the design, physiology, ecology and behaviour of the organisms in given habitats. We are delighted to be able offer these volumes at reduced prices for a limited period, including the forthcoming Biology of Freshwater Wetlands.

Sheldrake Wild Guides
The aim of this outstanding series - with volumes on Britain, France, Ireland, Italy and Spain - is to provide the general outdoor traveller with the means of discovering the genuinely wild places of Europe. They combine detailed travel advice with illustrations and descriptions of the landscapes and wildlife to be found, region by region. All five volumes are available at 20% off the normal price.

Windgather Landscapes of Britain
Britain has an extraordinary variety of landscapes, shaped by human involvement over millennia. Windgather Press are specialists in landscape history and are launching a superb new series. It draws on the latest archaeological and historical research, whilst bringing to life the deep processes which have given unique character to the islands. The first four volumes are offered at special prices during August and September.

Two major CUP Series
We are proud to feature two prestigious series from Cambridge University Press: Conservation Biology and Studies in Biological & Evolutionary Anthropology. They have consistently provided landmark texts over the last decade and many of the titles will be recognised as classics, including Carnivore Conservation and Conserving Bird Biodiversity. This is an excellent chance to update your collection at reduced prices.
