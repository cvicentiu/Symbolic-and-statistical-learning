FROM: Creative Exchange
EMAIL:<mailto:info@creativexchange.org>info@creativexchange.org

FOR: ALL PARTNERS / CONTACTS

TOPIC: NEWS FROM CREATIVE EXCHANGE PARTNERS - PUBLICATIONS AND 
RESOURCES, 25 NOV 2003

CONTENTS:
1. SENSES OF THE CITY AND LONDON ARTS' SUPPORT FOR REFUGEES 
1999-2002: AN EVALUATION
2. CREATIVE SOLUTIONS AND SOCIAL INCLUSION - CULTURE AND THE COMMUNITY
3. RESOURCE CENTRE MANUAL - HEALTHLINK WORLDWIDE
4. DEVELOPMENT EDUCATION JOURNAL
5. ANIMATED - FOUNDATION FOR COMMUNITY DANCE
6. BRITISH COUNCIL ARTS NEW DESIGNED WEBSITE

1. Senses of the City and London Arts' support for refugees 
1999-2002: An Evaluation
By Candida Blaker
An evaluation of Senses of the City, a strand of the London Arts 
Development Fund, for refugee-led arts.
Available to download on line at:
WEBSITE: http://www.artscouncil.org.uk/information/publications.html

2. Creative Solutions and Social Inclusion - Culture and the Community
By Penny Eames, Executive Director of Arts Access Aotearoa, New Zealand
This book has philosophy and examples of projects that encourage 
cultural development and illustrate the right of all to be creative. 
The book has chapters on culture, the environment, participation, 
social Inclusion, employment of time strategies, income generation 
and keys to success and includes examples from the arts in prisons, 
hospitals, the environment and in communities where people have had 
limited access to the arts.
Published and distributed by Arts Access Aotearoa:
TEL: + 64 4 916 4885
FAX: + 64 4 473 2905
EMAIL: <mailto:penny.eames@artsaccess.org.nz>penny.eames@artsaccess.org.nz
WEBSITE: http://www.artsaccess.org.nz

3. Resource Centre Manual - Healthlink Worldwide
Building on from the original manual launched in February 2000 this 
new version has been updated.  It is designed for health and 
disability workers planning to set up and develop a resource centre 
within resource-poor communities around the world.
It is available online at:
WEBSITE: http://www.healthlink.org.uk/pubs.html

4. Development Education Journal
Edited by Gillian Symons for the Development Education Association
This journal published three times a year aims to create a forum for 
debate on development education and the climate in which it is 
working, and to report on and discuss current practice, theory and 
research in Britain and overseas.
Published by Trentham Books for the Development Education Association:
TEL: +44 (0)20 7490 8108
FAX: +44 (0)20 7490 8123
EMAIL: <mailto:dea@dea.org.uk>dea@dea.org.uk
WEBSITE: http://www.dea.org.uk

5. Animated - Foundation for Community Dance
The current issue of 'animated: making dance matter' the magazine of 
the Foundation For Community Dance focuses on education, learning and 
creativity.
Published by BCPublications on behalf of Foundation for Community Dance:
TEL:  +44 (0)116 251 0516
FAX: +44 (0)116 251 0517
EMAIL: <mailto:info@communitydance.org.uk>info@communitydance.org.uk
WEBSITE: http://www.communitydance.org.uk

6. British Council Arts New Designed Website
As well as a whole section on the arts for development with a direct 
link to their arts and culture for development directory, the new 
site contains information and news on all artforms in the UK, the 
creative industries, education and training, support and funding.
WEBSITE: http://www2.britishcouncil.org/arts

ENDS
