Crane

Gregory Crane
Stack, Boat, Train, 1998
oil and wax on linen
29" x 60"

Gregory Crane combines views of the Hudson River from three vantage points in Stack, Boat, Train. The viewer is drawn though the landscape along several paths and train tracks. The sun rises on the right side of the painting and the light changes as day passes to the night on the left. Crane combines perspective and light, devices of landscape painting, to take the viewer on a journey via the artist’s personal route.
