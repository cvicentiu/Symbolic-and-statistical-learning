Gregory Crane - Mechanical Services Manager Gregory Crane received his Agricultural Economics degree from Texas A&M in 1981. After serving in the U.S. Army from 1982 to 1985 as Operations Officer, Supervisor of Basic Training (including Drill Sergeants) and being a member of Airborne and Ranger School, Mr. Crane joined Energy Industries, Inc. in 1985 as Sales Representative. In 1987, he was promoted to District Manager and was responsible for the sales, rental, service and maintenance of oilfield reciprocating equipment, both onshore and offshore. Mr. Crane also provided service to clients for equipment inspections (monthly and annually) and overhauls. Since 1994 Mr. Crane has been with Operators and Consulting Services, Inc. as Mechanical Services Manager, responsible for implementation supervision and sales of Field Management and Mechanical Services.
