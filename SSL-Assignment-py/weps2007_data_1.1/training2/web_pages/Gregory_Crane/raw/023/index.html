  Gregory Crane
Info
  Gregory Crane's broad vistas and enclosed gardens are visions of a heightened reality. Racing clouds, sinuous plants and intense colors combine with the man made to emit an electric tension. Born and raised on a farm in the Pacific Northwest, Gregory Crane is a keen observer and superb craftsman, his paintings are animated by his wit and idiosyncratic vision.

Crane has been widely exhibited, reviewed, and collected. Known as "a painters' painter" his pursuit of his singular vision has earned him Robert Mahoney's beautiful quote; "An otherworldly poem of actual American Landscape."
