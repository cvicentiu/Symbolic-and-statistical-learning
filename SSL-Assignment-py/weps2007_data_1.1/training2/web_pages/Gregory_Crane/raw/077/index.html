2003 Joint Conference on Digital Libraries (JCDL'03)   p. 75
Towards a Cultural Heritage Digital Library
Gregory Crane, Tufts University
Clifford Wulfman, Tufts University
Full Article Text:

DOI Bookmark: http://doi.ieeecomputersociety.org/10.1109/JCDL.2003.1204845

Abstract
This paper surveys research areas relevant to cultural heritage digital libraries. The emerging National Science Digital Library promises to establish the foundation on which those of us beyond the scienti.c and engineering community will likely build. This paper thus articulates the particular issues that we have encountered in developing cultural heritage collections. We provide a broad overview of audiences, collections, and services.
