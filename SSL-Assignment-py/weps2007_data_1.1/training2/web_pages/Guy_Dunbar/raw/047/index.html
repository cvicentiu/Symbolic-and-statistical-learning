USS CORAL SEA CVA-43 ASSOCIATION
A Brief Explaination

The Official Coral Sea Association's website is at www.usscoralsea.org

This site you are in now is the Association's newsletter supplement to its quarterly printed newsletter called the SEA BREEZE. For obvious reason this site, as a supplement is called SEA BREEZE ONLINE, maintained and monitored by the Association's Secretary. As a newsletter format, entries or postings of interest to the Association will be uploaded a little more frequently than that of the quarterly printed version. If you sign the guestbook within this site as a member, you will be considered a subscriber, and will received e-mail notification whenever new entries or postings are made available. As a member, you are also encourage to submit entries for posting in whatever category deem necessary in this website as well as in the paper version. You must submit your entry through the Secretary. The Secretary is also the editor of the BREEZE.

You can sign the guestbook whether you are a member or not, but you will notice that there is no clickable link to view the guestbook. You can however, view the guestbook by notifying the Secretary and he will give you the guestbook address. To conform to our privacy policy, the guestbook is not completely for public access. If you are looking for a certain person or a shipmate, e-mail the Secretary. The Secretary maintain an updated roster file of all members of the Association. Meanwhile, if you haven't done it yet, check out all the pages in this site. Use the INDEX column on the left side of your screen to get familiar with the layout. E-mail any inquiries or comments to the Secretary.
