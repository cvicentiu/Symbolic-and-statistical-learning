    A terrific selection of short stories for teenagers, February 21, 2002
    Reviewer: A reader from England I have used this selection of short stories for all sorts of pupils, from the highly motivated to the highly disaffected, boys and girls. The wide range of story subject and the variety from amusing to disturbing never fails. Even more important to me, the stories invariably lead to very interesting discussions and inspiration for their own writing.
