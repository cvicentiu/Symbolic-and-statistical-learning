Greetings and Salutations :) I've been on networked computer systems since my youth in the 70s and had my first webpages live in the early 90s. I am a writer and database developer by profession and an explorer of life by avocation. I'm an eclectic sort of person, and I enjoy a wide variety of hobbies. Books have had an enormous impact on my life, and two that have guided me have been the Honesty of Aragorn - Lord of the Rings and the Environmentalism of Dune.

My web site has grown into over 23,000 pages and images, all hand written and created by me. I update it frequently - the current photo of me is from June 2006. The sidebar is of morning glories in my back yard. Please enjoy yourself as you peruse through my photography, my writing, my poetry, my music, and perhaps a glimpse into my soul.
