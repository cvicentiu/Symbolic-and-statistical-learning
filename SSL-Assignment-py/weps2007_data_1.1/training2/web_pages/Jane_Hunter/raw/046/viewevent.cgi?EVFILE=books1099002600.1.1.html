Event:
Bookwarming for L&C History Prof Jane Hunter

When:Thursday, October 28, 2004 3:30 p.m.
Where:Templeton Student Center
bookstore
Cost:free
About:On Thursday Oct. 28 at 3:30 pm the bookstore will host L&C History Prof JANE HUNGER at a bookwarming reception for her new book, HOW YOUNG LADIES BECAME GIRLS, THE VICTORIAN ORIGIN OF AMERICAN GIRLHOOD, which has just won the History of Education Society's "Outstanding Book Award for 2004." These "Open Bookcase" events are always informal, friendly, and intimate--Jane will introduce and talk about her book, and be available for autographing copies which will be on sale. Drop by, if only for a minute, to help us congratulate and honor Jane for this outstanding achievement.
