Worshipful Master Anthony (Tony) Palmer, the Officers and Craft of King David Lodge No.407 welcome you to our WEB site and invite you to visit ALL the pages of the site. You may also read comments from our visitors on our GUESTBOOK  QUOTE'S page.

If you would like to have your e-mail address added to our lodge e-mail address book or have recently changed your e-mail address, please send your current e-mail address to:  Webmaster@kingdavidlodge407.CityMax.com

The Craft of King David Lodge #407 invite you to send us your FEEDBACK.   Your comments help us to know if you found our website to be of interest, if it has been of any value,  and provided you with information regarding our lodge, FREEMASONRY and the advancement of MASONRY IN IOWA!
