Welcome to the Official Website of Absalom of David Lodge No. 36 located at 89 Monticello Ave Jersey City NJ 07304

Communications are held at 7 pm every first and third Saturday of each month

Absalom of David is the largest lodge in Jersey City with seventy four members on roll. In the information age we feel the need to provide our members with 24 hour access to the lodge.

This webpage will allow fellow lodges and chapters to monitor the work of Absalom. We intend for this tool to be used by individuals searching for information on masonry. It will also provide a point of contact to those men attempting to join a Masonic organization.
