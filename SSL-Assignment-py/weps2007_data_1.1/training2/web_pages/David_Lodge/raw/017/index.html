    Welcome to King David's Lodge, No. 209 F. & A.M. web site. We are under the jurisdiction of The Grand Lodge of California and within the 510th Masonic District. There are six general pages in this site:

  • Home Page - main page of the site
  • Freemasonry -  Masonic information (two parts)
  • History of the Lodge - brief history of our lodge
  • Officers, Chairmen, & Past Masters
  • Directory - schedules, lodges in our District, other Masonic Bodies
  • Trestleboard - the Officers' monthly messages

    I recommend a 1024x768 (pixels) resolution with a maximized browser/window in viewing this web site to make your visit as pleasant as possible. There are a number of images - download time is dependent on your Internet connection. Please be patient as it may take a while (especially with dial-ups) to completely display each page. Additional pages are:

  • Image Gallery - unfinished
  • Famous Freemasons
  • How to become a Mason
  • Links
