Congratulations to you all!
 Another year over and what a great success Longford Marathon 2004 proved to be. Although the weather conditions were not as favourable as the previous two years, huge crowds still turned out to cheer on the participants. Well done to all who took part in particular to our winners and to all who helped out on the day.

The marathon results for men and women respectively were: First (men) Paul Buckley (Ferbane Athletic Club, Co. Offaly) in 2.39.14, second (men) Joe O'Sullivan (Bandon, Co Cork) in 2.45.44, third (men) Vincent Mulcahy, Nenagh, Co. Tipperary in 2.52.12, first (women) Helen Cawthorne (Derbyshire, England) in 2.58.59, second (women) Catherine Dunne (Dunboyne, Co. Meath) in 3.17.12, third (women) Mary Jennings (Waterford) in 3.17.50. Darrell Erwin took first place in the marathon wheelchair section in 2.39.58. In the men's half marathon Tony Dalton of the Belfast Harriers won in 1.13.33 with Peter Mooney in 2nd in 1.14.54, and Pauric Farrell in 3rd in 1.16.53.In the women's half marathon Joesephine Killeen from Clare won in 1.28.30 with Kate Moyna in 2nd place in 1.32.42, and Maura Dervin in 3rd place in 1.35.51.
