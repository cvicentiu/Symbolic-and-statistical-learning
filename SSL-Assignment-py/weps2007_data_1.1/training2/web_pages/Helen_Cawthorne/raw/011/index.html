Sheffield PCTs' Clincal Audit and Effectiveness Strategy

Martin Ferris, Clinical Effectiveness Coordinator, Sheffield South West PCT writes:-

When the local Health Authority was dissolved as part of "Shifting the Balance of Power", responsibility for a number of citywide initiatives in Sheffield was transferred to the four PCTs. Sheffield South West PCT now has the lead for clinical audit and clinical effectiveness in the city.

The development of a city wide strategy for clinical audit and effectiveness for primary care was under way but the announcement that all four PCTs were to be reviewed by CHI in June 2003 really concentrated the mind. As a result strategy has been developed to demonstrate our commitment to CHI, and has been agreed by all the PCTs.

The strategy has been developed with input and support from:

  • Helen Cawthorne (Sheffield South West PCT)
  • Andrew Beardsall (Sheffield West PCT)
  • Jane Haddock (South East Sheffield PCT)
  • Lisa Widdowson (North Sheffield PCT)
