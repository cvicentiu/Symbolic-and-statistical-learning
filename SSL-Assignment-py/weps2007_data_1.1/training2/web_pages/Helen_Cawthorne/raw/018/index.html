March 28th

Updates Galore

 

Club Duathlon Championships - April 21st

The first ever club duathlon will be held at Sandringham school in St Albans, probably with an 8.00a.m. start time. It will be a 2-mile run, followed by 12 mile bike and 2-mile run format. The bike course will be exactly the same course as the club triathlon and the run a simple loop around the Ridgeway Rd, Quadrant shops and back to the school More details to follow. Watch the club e-group for the latest information.

Paula’s marathon exploits.

Following her excellent race at the Reading Half Marathon, Paula will be doing the London Marathon on April 14th. A number of people from the club will be travelling up to shout for her. Travelling by train will be the only game in town. Sarah has agreed to work out when to meet up at St Albans station and will post details to the e-group. Vince is also running in the marathon! Feb 16th

The “great escape”

 

Well it is true, anyone who saw last nights Who Wants to be a Millionaire, will have seen Tri-Force quiet man Patrick Calthrop win £250,000. Patricks win came after a ‘great escape’ where he used up 2-life lines to get to £2000, and then went the rest of the way on just 1! Well done Patrick!

Feb 5th

AGM

 

Thanks to everyone that showed up at the AGM. Most of the committee stays as it was. Since we didn’t have a volunteer for News letter editor we’ve dropped that position and will work with the web site and e-group only. You can find a full list of committee members on the “Membership” page

The one position that remains unfilled is Youth Development. We have a number of girls in the St Albans area who are doing well in Junior events and want to be able to feed them into the club. For the first year the role shouldn’t be to onerous. Please have a read and see if you might help start this important club activity. Junior Co-ordinator
