Abbott’s Accountability

Published by Mark on 6 February 2006 at 11:45 am in Politics.
17 comments.

In Tony Abbott’s nanny state, you look to politicians for medical advice not doctors. Father knows best, and is just patriarchally paternally concerned for the health of the women of Australia. As the Senate prepares to vote on whether powers to approve RU486 should be stripped from the Health Minister and handed to the Therapeutic Goods Administration, not content with conjuring up yet more medical horror stories, Tony Abbott now frames the issue as one of democratic accountability.

Interesting departure from the Howard government’s standard perspective on Ministerial accountability.
