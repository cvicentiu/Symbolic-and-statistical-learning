Areas of Practice

Mr. Fox has broad experience in virtually all aspects of bankruptcy, corporate reorganizations and financial restructurings.  He represents creditors committees, indenture trustees, landlords and other creditors in some of the country’s largest bankruptcy cases.  He has also represented banks, pension funds, labor unions and other creditors in numerous Chapter 11 reorganization cases involving retailers, hotel and motel properties, office buildings, casinos and other businesses.  For more detailed information, please see Mr. Fox's Extended Biography, which includes a list of Representative Matters.

Professional Background
Prior to joining K&LNG, Mr. Fox was partner and head of the Bankruptcy Departments at several major law firms.
