THE SPIRIT OF CRICKET
CRICKET is a game that owes much of its unique appeal to the fact
that it should be played not only within its Laws, but also
within the Spirit of the Game.

The Spirit of Cricket is what guides us as we move forward with the expansion
of the sport of Cricket in Kansas.

Our goal is to introduce Cricket to as many residents of Kansas as possible ,
by encouraging any interested person to become involved with this sport. 

Cricket is an amazing sport that defies an easy explanation.

So have a look around , look at the links and if you have questions or are interested in
Participating in some way please contact us at cricket@aussiefox.com
