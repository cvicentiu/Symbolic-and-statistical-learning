Edward Fox (1937- )

Edward Fox was born on April 13, 1937 in Chelsea, London, England.

In 2002 Edward Fox was awarded the O.B.E. (Officer of the order of the British Empire) Queen's New Year's Honours List for his services to acting. Fox is a graduate from RADA, and an Associate Member of RADA.

Edward Fox's role as the cold-blooded assassin of THE DAY OF THE JACKAL is todate his best remembered role.

Actor James Fox is Edwards brother.
