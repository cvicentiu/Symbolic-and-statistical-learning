Established in 1902, Edward Fox Photography has over 100 years of experience in creating quality candid photography and studio portraiture. Owned and operated by the grandson and great grandson of Edward Fox, it is the second oldest family-owned photography studio in the United States.

From the original Chicago location on Milwaukee Avenue, Edward Fox Photography now has locations throughout Chicago and its suburbs as well as in the Detroit and Milwaukee areas. The studio is affiliated with many of the area's David's and Eva's Bridal Salons.

The pride of an immigrant establishing his own business over a century ago is instilled in those working at the studio today. For that reason, Edward Fox Studio uses only professional Kodak film and operates its own professional photofinishing lab using professional Kodak paper and chemicals. There are many less expensive products available; however the studio will not compromise quality by using them.
