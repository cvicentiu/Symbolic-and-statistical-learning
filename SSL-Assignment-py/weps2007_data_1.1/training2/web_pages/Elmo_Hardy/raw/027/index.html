2... Florida Pick Pocketing Ring Led By Grandmother... A statewide pick pocketing ring in Florida, that was aimed at the elderly, was allegedly run by a 65-year-old grandmother, who has been arrested 29 times since 1960. The women, named Ernestine Williams, led the 15-person operation, and taught each pickpocket the tricks of the trade. She has told police that she instructed her accomplices to target the elderly, because they were easy targets. Williams, a mother of 12, led the crime ring from her wheelchair, lasting 2 years until a Florida Department of Law Enforcement officer broke up the ring, arrested the members, including Ernestine and several of her family members. Ernestine Williams has pled guilty to racketeering, conspiracy to commit racketeering, two grand theft charges and three misdemeanor theft charges. She faces four to eight years in prison, according to Florida state sentencing guidelines. -South Florida Sun Sentinel: 4/2/02 -FDLE Press Release: 10/15/00 -ABC News: 10/26/00 -Associated Press: 10/26/00
3... Convicted Murderer Sues Jail Because He Can't Sleep...David Wild, 65, is attempting to sue a prison in Canada where he is being held, because the guards keep him up all night. 4... Man Found Hiding Six Years After End Of War... Even though the war in Bosnia has been over for many years, a man has been found hiding out - 6 years after the war stopped. The hermit was found hiding in the mountains of Bosnia, by British soldiers on a peacekeeping mission, and when he was told that the war was over, he was very surprised. The man, identified as Illija Panincic, had fled to the mountains surrounding the town of Sipovo, and lived off of twigs and berries for the duration of his stay. He was found in an abandon cabin in the mountains, and since they found him, British soldiers have been traveling great distances to deliver food to him. -Ananova: 2/21/02
