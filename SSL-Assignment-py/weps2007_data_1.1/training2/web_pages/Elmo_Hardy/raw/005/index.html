2003

Dr. Larry Larson   

Dr. Larson served with Dow AgroSciences in a number of roles within Discovery and Field Research and Development for over 28 years. He was highly recognized, both inside and outside of the company, for his passion for science and for his outreach to the scientific and local communities including his commitment to young people. His contributions included serving as President of the Entomological Society of America, Board of Directors and Counselor for the Entomological Foundation, and helping Dow AgroSciences introduce new methods for pest management that won Presidential Green Chemistry Challenge Awards, given by the Environmental Protection Agency.

In tribute to Dr. Larson's tireless and lasting contributions to the science and practice of entomology the following friends and colleagues have donated in his honor and memory. To give a donation, please go to: Gift.
