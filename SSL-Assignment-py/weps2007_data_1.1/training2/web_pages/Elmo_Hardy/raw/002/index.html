Research Project: Systematics of Flies of Importance in Biological Control, Agricultural Crops and Forests

Location: Systematics Entomology Laboratory

Title: Bibliography of and New Taxa Described by D. Elmo Hardy (1936-2001)

Author
Thompson, F - chris

Submitted to: The Bulletin Of Zoological Nomenclature
Publication Acceptance Date: July 26, 2002
Publication Date: August 26, 2003

Interpretive Summary: . Elmo Hardy, a world renowned specialist on the taxonomy of flies, especially fruit flies, published more than 437 articles and notes. A complete list of these publications as well as list of the taxa he described are provided. Also a note on the relative productivity of dipterists is also included. This information will make it easier for Identifiers adn Entomolgists to find these references and the species treated in them.

Technical Abstract: D. Elmo Hardy, a world renowned specialist on the taxonomy of flies, especially fruit flies, published more than 437 articles and notes. A complete list of these publications as well as list of the taxa he described are provided. Also a note on the relative productivity of dipterists is also included.

Citation: Thompson, F.C. 2003. Bibliography Of And New Taxa Described By D. Elmo Hardy (1936-2001). The Bulletin Of Zoological Nomenclature. 60: 198-202
