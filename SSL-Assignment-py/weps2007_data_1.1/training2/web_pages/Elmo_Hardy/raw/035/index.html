'The Tephritinae of Indonesia, New Guinea, the Bismarck and Solomon Islands (Diptera: Tephritidae)', by Hardy, D. Elmo
The fruit flies (Tephritidae) of the subfamily Tephritinae of Indonesia, New Guinea, the Bismarck Archipelago, and the Solomon Islands are revised. This paper treats 17 genera and 47 species of theseflies, with nine described for the first time. Keys for identification and associated illustrations are provided. 1988, 92 pp. [Bishop Museum Press]
