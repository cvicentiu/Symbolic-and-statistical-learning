Tadeusz Zatwarnicki

e-mail: zatwar@miiz.waw.pl

 

Principal research interests:

  • Relationships among major lineages of Eremoneura (Diptera).
  • Taxonomy of Ephydridae (Diptera).
  • The origin and modifications of the eremoneuran male terminalia.
  • Diptera of Poland.

 

Current research projects:

  • Revision of the genus Hecamedoides (Ephydridae: Diptera) of Oriental Region.
  • Revision of the genus Dichaeta (Ephydridae: Diptera).
  • Phylogeny and classification of the subfamily Discomyzinae (Ephydridae: Diptera).
  • Ephydridae section in the programme "Fauna Europaea".
