Carnegie Mellon Men's Tennis Nearly Sweeps Division I Opponents

(PITTSBURGH, Pa.) - A day after the men's tennis team defeated Case Western Reserve University and Allegheny College by 7-2 scores to win the Nancy J. Heath Gator Invitational, they returned home to host two local Division I opponents. The Tartans swept through the Robert Morris University line-up for a 9-0 victory before falling in a heartbreaker, 5-4, to Duquesne University.

Carnegie Mellon 9, Robert Morris 0
