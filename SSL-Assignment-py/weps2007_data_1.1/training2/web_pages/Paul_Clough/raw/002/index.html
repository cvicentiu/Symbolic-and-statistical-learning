ALPHA COURSE

A collaboration of the churches of Ushaw moor and surrounding areas.

Alpha has been done before, but such a long time ago we thought the village would like to have another one to attend.

What is Alpha?

The Alpha course is an opportunity for anyone to explore the Christian faith.

Alpha is a place where no question is too simple or too hostile.

And it's supported by all the main Christian denominations.

Starting Wednesday 27 th September 2006 at 7.00 pm
In Ushaw Moor Catholic Club (Then each Wednesday)
