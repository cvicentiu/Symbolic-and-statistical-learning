DTI Announces New Board of Director Appointment

Distribution Source : CCNMatthews

Date : Tuesday, July 20, 2004

VANCOUVER, BRITISH COLUMBIA--(CCNMatthews - Jul 20, 2004) - DTI Dental Technologies Inc., one of North America's leading operators of dental laboratories, is pleased to announce the appointment of Paul Clough to DTI's Board of Directors. Mr. Clough is the second independent appointment to the Board of Directors in the last two months and reflects DTI's continued commitment to strong corporate governance.

Mr. Clough has over 35 years experience in senior executive positions in a variety of industries. Paul moved to Imperial Parking in 1968 and in a very short space of time became a driving force in the development of this company. From the mid 1970's onward, he was Chief Operating Officer and became CEO in 1985.

He is currently Chairman of Imperial Parking Canada Corporation and Chairman and CEO of Robbins Parking Services. In April 2000, Paul became principal shareholder of the Queen Charlotte Fishing Lodge, an exclusive Fishing Resort in the Queen Charlotte Islands.

He is Director of the National Parking Association (Washington, DC), the Canadian Parking Association, and a recent member of the Advisory Board of the Faculty of Commerce and Business administration at the University of British Columbia.

In 1996 he was elected Chairman of The Okanagan Skeena Group, a British Columbia broadcasting company with radio stations, cable systems, two CBC television affiliates and a variety of other assets (later sold to Telemedia Group).

He is a former partner of Griffiths Milne Clough Projects Inc. a privately held development company with offices in Vancouver and Victoria.

Mr. Kalaw said, "We are pleased to welcome Paul to DTI's Board of Directors. He brings to DTI a breadth of operational management experience. We have every confidence that he will make an invaluable contribution towards preparing DTI for the future."

About DTI Dental Technologies Inc.

DTI is one of North America's fastest growing, multi-site operators of premium quality dental laboratories, with fourteen dental laboratories in Canada and the United States. DTI's core business strategy is to develop and operate a national network of high quality dental laboratories in order to manufacture and distribute restorative and cosmetic dental prosthetics to dentists in Canada and the United States. DTI's experienced management team is committed to building shareholder value by increasing market share, revenue and cash flow through carefully targeted acquisitions and improved marketing, training, and business processes. DTI is well positioned to capitalize on growing demand by our aging population for high quality cosmetic and restorative dental products.
