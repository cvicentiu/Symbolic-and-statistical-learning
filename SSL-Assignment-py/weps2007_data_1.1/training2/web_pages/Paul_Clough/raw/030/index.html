Paul Clough
 Senior Vice President, Strategic Marketing, Planning and Analysis
ICT Group
pclough@ictgroup.com
(800) 201-1085 

Paul Clough, Senior Vice President, Strategic Marketing, Planning and Analysis of ICTGroup, has more than twenty years' experience in business development and strategic marketing management.  He joined ICT in 1999 as vice president/general manager of Internet Support Services.  

Paul is responsible for ICT’s tactical marketing initiatives, including market analysis and product planning for continued growth and entrée into new markets with value-added products and services.   Also responsible for overseeing ICT’s partner alliances and channel strategies, building a portfolio of complementary products/services, as well as its Marketing Communications, Lead Generation and Sales Communications organizations.

Before joining ICT, Paul served first as vice president of consumer marketing and then as senior vice president of commercial business for Music Choice, a leading provider of business and consumer subscription music services.  He also formerly served as director of business development for the communications division of General Instrument Corporation.

Paul received his MBA from Harvard Graduate School of Business Administration and his BS from Bucknell University.
