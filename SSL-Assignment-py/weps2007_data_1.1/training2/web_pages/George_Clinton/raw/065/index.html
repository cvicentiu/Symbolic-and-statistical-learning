News: Check out Bootsy's Christmas Album
Posted by: NewFunkTimes on Wednesday, October 11, 2006 - 03:37 AM
Amazon.com accepts pre-orders for Bootsy Collins' upcoming CD "Christmas Is 4 Ever" which will be released on October 31, 2006. You can listen to samples of the 13 songs at Amazon.com already.

According to US label Shout Factory and German distributor Soulfood Music Distribution, "Christmas Is 4 Ever" features Snoop Dogg, who raps on the new song "Happy Holidaze," and Charlie Daniels, who provides fiddle and background vocals for the holiday standard "Sleigh Ride" (which also features vocals by Ohio Players lead singer Sugarfoot).

Other guests include Bobby Womack, the late Roger Troutman (Zapp), and former James Brown collaborators Fred Wesley, Bobby Byrd, and MC Danny Ray, as well as Parliament/Funkadelic/P.Funk All-Stars members Blackbyrd McKnight, Bernie Worrell, Mike „Kidd Funkadelic“ Hampton, Garry Shider, and Catfish Collins.
