George Clinton

Musician George Clinton was one of the early pioneers of funk music, leading to the nickname Dr. Funkenstein. Clinton’s music contains elements of several different styles, including rock, psychedelic music, and rhythm and blues. Many rap artists of the 1990s borrowed snippets of Clinton’s music for their own songs, cementing his status as a musical icon.
