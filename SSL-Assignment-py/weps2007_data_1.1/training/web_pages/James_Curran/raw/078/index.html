Curran House

222 Eureka Street

The Curran House was built in 1927/28 by James Curran, founder of Sandstone Brick. Constructed of three different colors of sandstone brick, it has an unusual double-arched design that is both structurally sound and aesthetically interesting. Some remodeling has been necessary to adapt the house to congregational usage, but insofar as possible, the original exterior and interior have been preserved.
