Brief biography of Dr James Curran

Dr James Curran holds a PhD in history from the University of Sydney and is currently a policy officer with the Commonwealth Public Service.

His doctoral thesis on prime ministerial rhetoric and Australian nationalism from 1972 to 1996 has been published by Melbourne University Publishing under the title Power of speech: Australian prime ministers defining the national image. It is a highly original study of prime ministerial rhetoric, exposing the sources of our most powerful leaders’ beliefs about Australia.

To order the book online from the Curtin University Bookshop, go to http://www.bookshop.curtin.edu.au.
