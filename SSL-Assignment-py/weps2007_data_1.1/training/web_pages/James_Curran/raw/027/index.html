  General Notes:

Family group sheet indicates death as "1888-1890?"
According to Claude Milton Curran, born 1 Jun 1914, James Milton Curran was the first sherriff in Okalahoma during the land rush. He disappeared and was found in a well three years later along with his pistol, handcuffs, and a few other items. Claude said James Milton Curran's son, John James Curran (father of Claude Milton Curran), was 6 weeks old at the time.


James married Martha E. Circle, daughter of Daniel Circle and Julia Alphine, on 19 Apr 1876 in Summersville, Nicolas County, West Virginia 4.,105 (Martha E. Circle was born on 3 May 1853 in Nicolas County, West Virginia, died in 1933 in Fountain, Colorado and was buried in Fountain, Colorado.)
