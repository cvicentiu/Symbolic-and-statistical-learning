Curran group

According to MacLysaght in his "Irish Families...."....the name is "widespread now(1957) in all four provinces......... In the census of 1659 it was commonest in Waterford, thence north to Kildare; also in Kerry and Leitrim. Curran is the usual form....."
Then there are Curren, Currin,Curron,Curan,Curen,Curin,Curon,Corron,Churan,Curn,Currean,Currian, Kern.......
These are my mother's people. While the moving spirit in her life was her grandmother, a Douglas by birth, it was her Curran aunts and uncles and cousins among whom she spent her early life, and around whom most of her stories centered.
