You & My Old Guitar

First Hearing: The second of three CD recently released from the family archives. Taken from personal reel-to-reel recordings and polished up and, now, onto CD. The CDs have created a tremendous buzz and ripple across the pool of country music. The songs are all 'covers' of the songs that Thomas enjoyed singing, so there's a lot of enthusiasm in his performances. Certainly a good buy for fans of old style county music.
