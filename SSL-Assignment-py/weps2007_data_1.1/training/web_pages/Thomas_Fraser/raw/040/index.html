Thomas Fraser and Amelia

Posted By: Glenys Rasmussen <glenys@quietAcre.com>
Date: Tuesday, 17 July 2001, at 3:43 p.m.

We have entered the 34th year of searching for Thomas Fraser bn 1810 place unknown and his wife Amelia [Jackson?] bn 1817 New York. They married ca 1837-1840 in New York and their first child, Susan J. Fraser was born 24 May 1840 in Blooming Grove, Orange Co. NY. We have assumed the marriage took place in Orange Co. but have not been able to find it. We have searched surrounding counties, New Jersey and NYCity without success. Thomas and Amelia moved to NYCity in 1847 and remained there until 1855 when they sailed to San Francisco, CA. They died in San Francisco before 1906 and few records remain. Letters to the Blooming Grove Town Historian have gone unanswered - we feel sure there must be some record somewhere in Orange Co. that will lead us to the family. We do know that Thomas Fraser was a member of the Montgomery School Board in 1841-42 and he is found on the Blooming Grove 1840 census with wife and daughter. Is it possible he was connected to the Fraser family of Sullivan and Orange Cos. who were highly visible in school matters? Any information on Thomas and Amelia and their family in New York would be gratefully received. Glenys
