Welcome!

Tom's Hits above

Welcome all to Tom's Art Portfolio! My name is Thomas Fraser and I am 14, from the UK! I've been drawing for so long, that I cant even remember when I started......it's my passion. What can I say? Recently, I've been concentrating mainly on the Japanese comic book style drawings of Anime and Manga, mainly focusing on Bishoujo (girl's comics) because I like drawing really hot girls *winks*, but I have been know to draw a cool bad guy, and a hero or two from the Bishounen (guy's comics) origin.

I'm also a keen writer, but I don't write too often, but when I do....it can prove quite good , so I will upload some of my poems, or stories if I think they are good enough for you all.

So all I can do really, is again welcome you to my web-site, ask you to take a good look around.....and of course recommend me to all your friends!!!!

HAVE FUN!!!

Great Links

Over my time I have seen TONES of great websites. I'm a bit of an internet freak :P. So here are a few websites you may like.....

1) www.neondragonart.com  This website is absolutely fantastic! Jessica Peffer is the most talented artist I know. She is very well renound in the art world. Take a look for yourself, you wont regret it, I assure you.

2) www.deviantart.com  Deviantart is a wonderful site where thousands of artists post their art, and literature as well. If you need a friend to guide you round there if you join, just look me up.......I'm soulcaster.

3) www.sheezyart.com Again another art website, but it's much slower, more easy going and a lot less known in comparrison to Deviantart. Sheezyart's server does go down very regualarly, but their fixing it up real good, and hopefully it'll be back on the road soon. I'm on here too: huolong.

4) www.writing.com  This, as the name suggests is an art site dedicated to writing. It's wonderful...........join quick!!!

5) www.bbc.co.uk/blast  This website is one to watch....it comes up with lots of opportunities for art, music, writing etc. Take a look every once in a while....
