2. William "MacAskill" Fraser

FROM FAMILY BIBLE:

MARRIAGE: St. James Manse by Rev Mr. Carruthers McCurdy DEATH: From Family Bible

From "History of Abercrombie" based on verbal histories including memories of Susie Fraser Boutilier: William Fraser worked loading coal on vessels in Abercrombie. Later had a house in New Glasgow on Bell Street. Was highly religious and strict. Bible readings were held daily in the home. Susie was caught picking strawberries on way home from Church. Told to return them to the ground.

Catherine MacDonald
