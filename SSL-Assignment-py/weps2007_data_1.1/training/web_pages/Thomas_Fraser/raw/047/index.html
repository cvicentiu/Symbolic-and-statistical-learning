Brothers and Sisters

  1. Sophia Fraser (Cotton Weaver, Mill Worker, Munitions Worker, Fruit Shop Manageress) was born on the 20th of December 1883 in Govan, and married on the 31st of October 1919 in Eastwood to Andrew Rodger (Twister in Lace Works, Army Medic, Powerloom Tender, Fruit Shop Owner, Billiards Hall Owner).
  2. Helen "Wee Nell" Fraser born ~1887.

    3. Died as a child. Very pretty and angelic.
  3. Thomas "Tam" "Puncle" Fraser photo was born in Barrhead in 1890 and married in 1914 in Busby to Agnes "Aggie" Darroch of Busby.

    4. Sarah Rodger called him "Puncle" because she had difficulty saying "uncle" as a young girl. Lived in apartment above parent's (Thomas and Sarah) at 104 Riverford Road.
    Reported his mother's (Sarah Brisbane) death in 1946.
    Plasterer and Bricklayer.
    Both went to live with daughter Rachel in Merrylee.
  4. James "Jock" Fraser photo was born in 1893 in Eastwood (verify) and married around 1920 in Hamilton?, Ontario, Canada to an Englishwoman?

    5. Served in the Black Watch in World War I. He went to Canada and lived not far from Niagara Falls. Was buried there with full military honors.
    Was very clever with his hands. Built beautiful things like bird-cages from the spent matches from his father's clay pipe. Could also pick locks with wire!
    As a joke, called his wife terrible Scottish nicknames which she thought were endearments.
    His Canadian nickname was "Jock".
  5. Another boy, stillborn.

    6. Sarah Fraser never talked about this. Daughter Sophia heard at a seance that she had another brother. Asked her mother if this was true, and it was.

Source:  Sarah Rodger.

Occupation

  • Cotton Weaver
  • Mill worker. Coustenholm mills, Pollokshaws, Glasgow. Lace.
  • Munitions factory worker at Weirs during WW I.
  • Fruit Shop Manageress. First at Cumberland Street and then at her own shop at Sinclair Drive, Langside.

Hobbies and Interests

Politics. Socialism. Loved serving in the shops.

Other Comments

Very strong character. An early socialist.

Always took her holidays in Largs and wanted to retire there.
