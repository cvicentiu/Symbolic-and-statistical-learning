Offering four different treatment areas, a state-of-the-art physical therapy and spine stabilization area, computerized intermittent and static traction, and hydrotherapy, Dr. Robert Moore is proud to welcome you to at the Moore Spine Center.

Whether you are suffering a spinal compression injury, a disc injury, or troubling lower back pain, our patented System will have you back on your feet in next to no time. Based on over 20 years of practice and research, the System was developed to specifically identify the problem, treat the traumatized area and stabilize the spine and surrounding tissue.

The Spine is a conduit for the nervous system. Any time there is a trauma to the spine a mechanical dysfunction is created, affecting that conduit. With the Moore Spine Stabilization System, , our goal is to return patients to the normal sea of life within weeks.

Utilizing state-of-the-art diagnostic x-ray equipment that offers decreased radiation and increased quality of film, you the patient can clearly see where the injury has occurred and understand the course of treatment offered. With , our patients have consistently enjoyed a 95 - 96% success rate.
