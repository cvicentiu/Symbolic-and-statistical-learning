Robert Moore’s King, Magician, Warrior, Lover and his subsequent The King Within, The Warrior Within and The Magician Within (all in collaboration with Douglas Gillette) are all groundbreaking works in getting in touch with the archetypes of the mature masculine. This tape gives an excellent short, succinct overview of these archetypes. Even if you have read the books, it is worthwhile to listen to this overview. But their real value is in his discussion of how the archetypes and archetypal energies work in our lives. While this is in his books, his practical, down-to-earth presentation style and his answering of questions from the audience make it easy to see how these energies work in your own life.

Reviewed by BHH in M.E.N. Magazine 7/93
