Robert Moore, Ph.D., is a Jungian analyst practicing in Chicago and is internationally recognized as one of the foremost authorities on psychology and spirituality. Professor of Psychoanalysis, Culture, and Spirituality at the Chicago Theological Seminary, he also teaches at the C. G. Jung Institute of Chicago. Editor and author of numerous books in the fields of psychoanalysis and human spirituality, his latest book is The Archetype of Initiation: Sacred Space, Ritual Process, and Personal Transformation. Two other new books, The Magician and the Analyst: The Archetype of the Magus in Occult Spirituality and Jungian Analysis and Facing the Dragon (of Personal and Spiritual Grandiosity) will be forthcoming in 2001. Dr. Moore works both as a healer and a leader-in both roles seeking to call us to a larger vision for ourselves and our role in the world.
