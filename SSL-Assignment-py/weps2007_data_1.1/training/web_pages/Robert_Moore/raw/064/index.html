Robert Moore's Biography

Robert is 35 years old and a resident of Watauga County (Boone, N.C.) He has 3 children (Holly 19, Cody 12, Dakota 9) and a lovely wife Mechelle. He was born in Kingsport, TN but the majority of his life he grew up in North Carolina (Salisbury area).

He is a graduate of West Rowan High School (Salisbury, N.C.) and Appalachian State University, N.C. and earned a B.S. Degree in Commercial Art/Graphic Design from this fine school.

He started his military career in Marines but is currently a member of the NC National Guard.

Robert has always loved painting/drawing/photography so Appalachian looked like a great place to start. The first camera he used was an old Pentax K-1000 (a workhorse/student camera).
Once a B.S. degree in Commercial Art was earned, Robert started his career at Pace Communications in Greensboro, N.C. While continuing to develop skill he eventually ended up in Boone again and has worked for Watauga Democrat Newspapers, Inc. for about 4 years while always doing freelance on the side for various businesses around Boone & Watauga County.

Photography has always been an interest to Robert and the Newspaper allowed him to use his ability to "Draw with Light." He currently takes photographs of scenery, sports, and various other subjects for the newspaper and on the side. His freelance Design/Digital Photography business is Total Design Concepts. He does free lance in the Boone area welcomes any new challenges. Robert uses a Canon D-30 digital with a Sigma 28-300 lens. Contact Robert for free lance at moore549@boone.net or 828-297-6878 (leave message). Thanks for checking out his most recent work.
