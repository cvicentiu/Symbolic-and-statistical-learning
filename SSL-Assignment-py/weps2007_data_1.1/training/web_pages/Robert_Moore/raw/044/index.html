Robert R. Moore

Senior Economist and Policy Advisor
Federal Reserve Bank of Dallas

Robert Moore is a senior economist and policy advisor in the Financial Industry Studies Department of the Federal Reserve Bank of Dallas, where he has worked since 1991. From 1987 to 1991, he was an assistant professor of economics at Tulane University. He has written numerous articles on banking and financial markets.

Moore received a B.A. in economics from the University of Missouri at Columbia, where he was elected to Phi Beta Kappa. He also holds a Ph.D. in economics from the University of Wisconsin at Madison.
