Descendants of Robert Haines Moore and Malinda Wasson

Robert Haines Moore born MAY-10-1824, Howard Co., Mo., (son of Levi Moore and Rachel Haines) married (1) JUN-12-1842, in Grundy Co., Mo., Malinda Wasson, born 1820, Iredell Co., NC, (daughter of William Wasson and Sarah Delph) died MAY-09-1852, Grundy Co.? Mo., married (2) JAN-13-1854, Hester Ann Jewett, born NOV-02-1833, Fayette Co., Oh., (daughter of David Jewett and Elizabeth Toothaker) died DEC-11-1909, Mercer Co., Mo., buried: Moore cemetery, Mercer Co., Mo. Robert died MAY-24-1912, Mercer, Mercer Co., Mo., buried: Moore Cem, Mercer Co., Mo.

Children:(of Robert and Malinda)

Ferguson) died OCT-09-1867, Duncan cemetery, buried: Grand River Twp., Wayne Co., Ia., married (2) Charles O. Ellis, died abt 1924, South Dakota. Eliza died JUL-27-1909, Morgan Center, buried: Elm cemetery, Decatur Co., Ia. (a link to descendants will be coming soon)
