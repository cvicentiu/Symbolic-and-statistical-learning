Moore, Robert

An offender against the Excise regulations. He received from Burns a noted dated 26th October 1789: 'Robert Moore in Dumfries I hereby intimate to you that by decreet of the Justices of the Peace for the County of Dumfries you are fined in the sum of 1£ sterling for making bricks without entry — and if the said sum be not paid within 14 days from this date you will incur an additional expence of 2d. on each 1 sh. Sterling.'

There is no record of any relationship with Moore other than this purely business one.
