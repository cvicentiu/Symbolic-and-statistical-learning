Martha (Edwards) Swanner
Posted by: Donald Edwards (ID *****9080) Date: February 20, 2007 at 20:59:40
  of 16221

I am looking for information about the family of Martha (Edwards) Swanner. She was named as an heir in the Bill of Partition for the estate of my great great grandfather, Henry Edwards which began in Crab Orchard, IL in 1885. She apparently died before the estate was settled, because two apparent children of hers; Almeda Swanner and William Swanner collected her share in 1891. My records appear to indicate that Martha Edwards was born around 1820/1825 in NC or TN, and that her family was in TN at the time the bill was placed before the court.

Does anyone have any information about this family?
