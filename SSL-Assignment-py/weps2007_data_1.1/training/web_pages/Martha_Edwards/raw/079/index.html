Martha Edwards


Published February 24, 2006

Funeral services for Martha Edwards, 80, of Freeport will be Saturday, February 25, 2006, at 10:00 a.m. at First United Missionary Baptist Church in Freeport with the Rev. Maceo Smedley officiating. Burial will follow at Restwood Memorial Park in Lake Jackson, Texas.

She died Wednesday, February 22, 2006, at Angleton Danbury Medical Center in Angleton, Texas. She was a member of First United Missionary Baptist Church and an Eastern Star. She was an avid fisherman, made casting nets, loved to crotchet and sew and made hats.

Survivors include sons, John Goodwin Jr. (Flora) of Lake Jackson and Carey Edwards of Freeport; daughters, Ruthie Sullivan of The Woodlands and Laura Watson Hill of Freeport; brothers, Isaac Williams (Geneva) and Daniel Williams (Jimmie Lee), both of Brazoria; sisters, Lillie Edwards of Freeport, Lucille Perry of Columbia, South Carolina, and Julia Helm (Ervin) of Brazoria; 12 grandchildren; 21 great-grandchildren; 12 great-great-grandchildren; sisters-in-law, Mildred Williams of Houston, Lula Bell and Allease Williams, both of Brazoria; and a host of other relatives and friends.

Active pallbearers will be Anthony Island, Marcus Robinson, Kennard Hill, Kolhmann Hill, Samuel Edwards and Roderick Mack.

Honorary pallbearers will be John Goodwin Jr., Carey Edwards, Isaac Williams, Daniel Williams and Robert Bonner Sr.

Visitation will be Friday, February 24, 2006, from 4:00 p.m. to 8:00 p.m. at Dixon Funeral Home in Brazoria, Texas. There will be viewing at First United Missionary Baptist Church on Saturday, February 25, 2006, from 9:00 a.m. to 10:00 a.m. only. There will be no viewing afterward.

Send online condolences at www.dixonfuneralhome.com

Final arrangements entrusted to Dixon Funeral Home of Brazoria at (979) 798-9113.
