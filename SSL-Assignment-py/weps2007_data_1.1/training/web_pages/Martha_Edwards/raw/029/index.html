Generation No. 1

1. JOHN H.1 HERROD was born 11 May 1812 in Alabama, and died 25 May 1895. He married (1) LOUISA MIRACK 02 April 1834 in Gibson Co., TN.. He married (2) CATHERINE GREENE ORANGE 1838 in Smith or DeKalb Co., TN., daughter of YERBY ORANGE and SAPHRONA JONES. He married (3) MARTHA J. RUNNER 21 November 1867 in Warren Co., Ky.(The Warren County, Kentucky, Records stated this was John H. Harrod's third marriage, but it is not proven that Louisa Mirack was his first wife. However, the NARA records state he was only married twice according to statements made by his widow, Martha J. Runner and a neighbor. Evidently, he was not married long the first time to Louisa Mirack and that was while he lived in Tennessee, prior to his marriage to Catherine.)
