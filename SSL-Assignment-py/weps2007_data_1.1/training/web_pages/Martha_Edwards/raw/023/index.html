Rice Edwards

Rice Edwards was born about 1615 in England.He is possibly the Rice Edwards christened November 30, 1614 a Willoughby with Slothby, Lincolnshire. If that is the case, he would be the son of John Edwards and would have a brother John and a sister Sarah. He married Elinor about 1636 in Salem. She died in 1680. He died on April 16, 1688.
