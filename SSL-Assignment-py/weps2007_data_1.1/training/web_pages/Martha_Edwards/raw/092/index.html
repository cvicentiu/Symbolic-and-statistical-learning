89. WILLIAM PETER OWEN ("Willie OWEN") (JOSEPH WILLIAM5 ("JOE BILL" or "BILL") OWEN, TABITHA4 LEECH OWEN, JOHN3, JOHN2, DAVID1, UNKNOWN0) was born on 3 July 1855 in Hart County, Kentucky. He married Martha Wood, daughter of George W. Wood and Sarah M. Downey, on 14 March 1874 at Laclede County, Missouri. In 1882 he joined the Baptist church at old Antioch of which he was a charter member and one of its first deacons. He died on 13 July 1934 and is buried at Oakland Cemetry (row 3, grave 2), LaClede County, Missouri, at age 79.   Found in 1930.

Obituary:
He William P. Owens, 79, died Sunday morning after a long illness.  Funeral services were held Monday morning at the Antioch church, the Rev G.W.H Davidson officiating.  Burial was at the Oakland cemetery.  "Uncle Willis", as he was known among his friends in the Hazel Dell district was born in Kentucky, July 3, 1855, and came with his parents to Missouri about 1870.  In 1882, he joined the Baptist church at old Antioch of which he was a charter member and one of its first deacons.  He was a faithful member, attending the services until his health prevented.  He married Martha Wood in 1883(?), who preceded him in death July 14, 1925.  The survivors are his three foster sons:  Sammie Owen of Oakland, Clarence Ogletree of Carson, Ia. and Herman Zeither of Glenwood Springs, Colorado;  a brother, Isaac Owen of Waubleau and a sister, whose wherabouts is unknown on 13 July 1934 at Oakland, Laclede County, Missouri.

91. ELIJAH WALTER OWEN (JOSEPH WILLIAM5 ("JOE BILL" or "BILL") OWEN, TABITHA4 LEECH OWEN, JOHN3, JOHN2, DAVID1, UNKNOWN0) was born on 10 January 1867 in Hart County, Kentucky. He married Ruth Ann Overlin, daughter of John Harrison Overlin and Eletha Isabell Davis, on 5 December 1894 at Lebanon, Missouri. He died on 19 July 1910, buried at Oakland Cemetry, (row 7, grave 9) Laclede County Missouri - died of typhoid fever, Missouri, at age 43.  Found in 1910.  Children of Elijah Walter OWEN and Ruth Ann OVERLIN include:

i. Ora Belle Owen was born on 17 November 1896 at Lebanon County, Missouri. She died on 26 October 1975 at Casper, Wyoming, at age 78.  Found in 1910.
 ii. Dona Francis Owen married J. Edward Wilkes. She married A. J. Clapp. She was born on 21 October 1898 at Lebanon County, Missouri. She died on 30 October 1980 at Basin, Wyoming, at age 82. Found in 1910.
iii. Walter Harrison Owen was born on 16 February 1901 at Lebanon County, Missouri. He married Georgia McPhillamey on 18 June 1933. He died on 14 November 1987 at Buffalo, Wyoming, at age 86. Found in 1910.
iv.  Jessie May Owen was born on 23 July 1904 at Laclede County, Missouri. She married John David Blue on 2 September 1924. She died on 26 May 1992 at Las Cruces, New Mexico, at age 87. Found in 1910.
v. Ruby Viola Owen was born on 23 July 1908 at Laclede County, Mo. She married George L. Klocksiem, son of Helmuth L. Klockziem and Fleda M. Wolker, on 27 September 1930 at On the corner of 5th and ? in a house, Casper, Natrona, Wyoming. On 16 March 1970 her name was legally changed to Klockziem On this date, George L. Klockziem offically changed the spelling of his name from "ziem"  to "siem" thru the district court of Natrona County, Wyoming. She died on 17 June 1991 at Casper, Natrona, Wyoming, at age 82.  GEORGE L. KLOCKSIEM was born 1905 in Michigan.  Found in 1910.
