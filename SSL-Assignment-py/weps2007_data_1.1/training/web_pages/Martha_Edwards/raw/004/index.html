MARTHA EDWARDS
MARTHA EDWARDS, daughter of Starling EDWARDS & Amy WILES, was b. Nov. 10, 1847, in Ashe Co., NC, and d. Oct. 16, 1912, in Alleghany Co., NC.  She m. ISAAC RICHARDSON, son of Alexander RICHARDSON & Martha CHEEK, Nov. 11, 1866, in Alleghany Co., NC.  He was b. Mar. 13, 1843, in Ashe Co., NC, and d. aft. 1900, probably in Alleghany Co., NC.
