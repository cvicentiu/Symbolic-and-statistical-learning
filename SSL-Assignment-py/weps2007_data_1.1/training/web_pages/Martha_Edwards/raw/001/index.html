GENERATION 4
MARTHA EDWARDS
MARTHA EDWARDS, daughter of Isaac EDWARDS & Gilley CHEEK, was b. Feb. 25, 1852, in Ashe Co., NC, and d. Jan. 26, 1940, in Alleghany Co., NC.  She m. JOHN WILLIAM MAINES, son of Peter V. MAINES and Marzella MORGAN, Aug. 18, 1889, in Alleghany Co., NC.  He was b. Oct. 31, 1868, in Grayson Co., VA, or Alleghany Co., NC, and d. July 26, 1952, in Alleghany Co., NC.  Martha and John are buried at the Edwards & Maines Cemetery on Road 1405, Alleghany Co., NC.

Children of MARTHA EDWARDS and JOHN WILLIAM MAINES:

1. James Arthur MAINES, b. June 2, 1890, Alleghany Co., NC; d. Oct. 22, 1956; m. Flora Virginia PATTON, daughter of Ballard M. PATTON & Mary E. WAMPLER, Apr. 1, 1923, Spring Valley, Grayson Co., VA (b. Sept. 20, 1897; d. Aug. 11, 1997); bur. Edwards & Maines Cemetery, Rd. 1405, Alleghany Co., NC.

2.  Houston McCoy MAINES [twin], b. Apr. 9, 1892, Alleghany Co., NC; d. Mar. 21, 1955; m. Jettie Maude JENNINGS, daughter of John & Eliza JENNINGS, Jan. 22, 1919, Galax, VA (b. July 1, 1901; d. May 11, 1979); bur. Pleasant Home Union Baptist Church, Alleghany Co., NC.

3.  Hastin McDaniel MAINES [twin], b. Apr. 9, 1892, Alleghany Co., NC; d. Mar. 7, 1926, Alleghany Co., NC; m. Ethel Matilda POOLE, daughter of Martin Elbert POOLE & Peggy MOORE, Dec. 23, 1914 (b. May 12, 1891; d. Mar. 25, 1991); bur. Edwards & Maines Cemetery, Rd. 1405, Alleghany Co., NC.

4.  Solomon Reed MAINES, b. Sept. 28, 1895, Alleghany Co., NC; June 8, 1973; m. Velia MCGRADY, daughter of Joseph MCGRADY & Sarah TOLIVER, Nov. 10, 1926 (b. June 23, 1907; d. June 17, 1992); bur. Sparta Memorial Park, Alleghany Co., NC.


NOTES

Birth and death dates are from her gravestone at the Edwards & Maines Cemetery, located on Road 1405 in Alleghany Co., NC, near the VA state line.  Name on stone is Martha Edwards Maines, wife of John William Maines.  ("Alleghany Cemeteries Through 1986" pub. by the Alleghany Co. Hist.-Gen. Society.)

1860 Census, Alleghany Co., NC, Glade Creek Twp., p.436 (Isaac Edwards, HH#680):  Martha, 12
1870 Census, Alleghany Co., NC, Gap Civil Twp (Isaac Edwards, HH#121):  Martha, 17
1880 Census, Alleghany Co., NC, Gap Civil Twp. (Isaac Edwards, HH#90):  Martha, 24

John W. Maines m. Martha Edwards on Aug. 18, 1889, in Alleghany Co., NC.  John: age 22, son of Peter V. Maines and Marzella Morgan.  Martha: age 30, daughter of Isaac Edwards and Martha Gilley Cheek.  (G.H. Latham, "Alleghany County, North Carolina Marriages: 1849-1900" (Bowie, MD: Heritage Books, 1996).)

1900 Census, Alleghany Co., NC, Gap Civil Twp., p.161 (John Maines, HH#171):  Martha, wife, Feb. 1852, age 48, m. 10 yrs, 4 children/4 living, NC NC NC

1910 Census, Alleghany Co., NC, Gap Civil Twp., Sparta, p.15 (John Maines, HH#154):  Marthy, wife, 57, m. 20 yrs, 4 children/4 living, NC NC NC

1920 Census, Alleghany Co., NC, Gap Civil Twp. - East Side, p.6 (John W. Maines, HH#106): Marth, wife, 66

Info on children is from "Alleghany Cemeteries Through 1986"  and "Alleghany County Heritage" (Alleghany Co. Hist.-Gen. Society, 1983), pp.325-326, article no. 498 by Ruby Maines and John M. Hoppers.
