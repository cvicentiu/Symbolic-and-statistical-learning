Jim Smith believes that Ollin is the son of George Griffin based on the 1820 census record for Anson county which shows a son about the age of Ollin, and lives in reasonable distance from Ollin in 1850. He has contacts who say that George was the son of a Richard Griffin, who came from Virginia, probably a brother of Thomas Griffin.
