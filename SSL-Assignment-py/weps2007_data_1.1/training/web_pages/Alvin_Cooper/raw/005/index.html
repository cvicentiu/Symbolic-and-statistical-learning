About Alvin Cooper Wheeler

Alvin Cooper Wheeler
11/10/1928 - 6/18/2005
(Shown at age 71)
Alvin Cooper Wheeler was born on 11/10/1928 in Lanett, AL and died on 6/18/2005 in Hiram, GA. He was 77 years old.
