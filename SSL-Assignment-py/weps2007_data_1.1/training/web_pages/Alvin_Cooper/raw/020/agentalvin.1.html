   Acerca de Agent Alvin
New Zealand's Drum & Bass collective continues to grow and prosper at an incredible rate. Whether it is the international success of Concord Dawn and Bulletproof, or the eclectic innovations of Shapeshifter and The Upbeats, it's a scene of diverse artists making world-class music. Add to that esteemed list a producer by the name of Agent Alvin. In 2004, Alvin aka Warren Field released his debut album on New Zealands Capital Records, featuring Spikey Tee(UK), Ruth Minuit, MC Kyla and Meg Martin to great critical acclaim. It was around the same time that he set his sights on the international vinyl market, with his debut release on the New Zealand based Random Recordings.

Music has always been a part of his life. Field was brought up on a steady diet of classical composers, later giving way to the experimentation of jazz, rock, funk and hip hop. It was in his teens that he picked up the guitar and set upon learning everything he could about the music he was listening to. The guitar would have to give way to samplers and keyboards however, when Field caught the rave bug in the late 90`s. His first tune, a laid-back dub affair was released by New Zealand`s LOOP recordings, gaining nation-wide airplay and hinting at a sign of things to come.

If he is not tweaking beats in his studio, Field can be found on the DJ circuit having played support for many touring internationals, as well as headlining his own gigs and playing some of the biggest festivals in his native land.

2006 is shaping up to be a big year for Agent Alvin, with tunes forthcoming on labels as diverse as the legendary Full Cycle, Klute`s imprint Commercial Suicide, G2, Incite and Cyanide. Look forward to an abundance of breakbeat experimentation at its very best confirming this kiwi producer as a man to watch.
