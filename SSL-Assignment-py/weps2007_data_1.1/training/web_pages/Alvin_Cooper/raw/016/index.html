In Remembrance

Leola Lee Clanton Cooper
 E. R. Johnson Family Mortuary

 

Leola Lee Clanton Cooper, 82, received her reward of eternal rest on Sunday, January 9, 2005, at UTMB Hospital.

Leola was born July 6 1922, in Greyburg, TX to Jackson and Florence Volsan Clanton. She received her education in the Galveston Public schools. She worked at John Sealy and later at GISD prior to her retiring and was a longtime member of the St John Church.

She was preceded in death by her parents, husband, Alvin Cooper; sisters, Lena White and Lillie Sims; brother, Clarence Clanton.

She leaves to cherish memories of her life, son, Alvin Cooper, Jr.; daughter, Sandra Marie Muse and husband Ezekiel, III, both of Galveston; grandchildren, La’Tissa Nicole Muse, Na’Taria Monique Muse of San Antonio and Andre M. Cooper of League City; a host of nieces, nephews, other relatives and friends.

Visitation will begin at 4PM on Friday, January 14th, followed by a quiet hour from 7-8 PM at E. R. Johnson Family Mortuary.  A service celebrating her life will be held at 11:00 AM on Saturday, January 15, 2005, at the St John Baptist Church with Pastor Louis Simpson, Jr. officiating. She will be laid to rest in the Lakeview Cemetery.
