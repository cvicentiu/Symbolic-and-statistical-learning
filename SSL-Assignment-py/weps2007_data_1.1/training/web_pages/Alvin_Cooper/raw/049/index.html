The Teachers Technology Collaborative is a group of present and former Agassiz teachers who are committed to using technology to teach students to think critically and creatively. We design and implement technology based thematic units integrating social studies, reading, language arts and science. We share curriculum and ideas while seeking to continually improve our understanding of the role of technology in education.
