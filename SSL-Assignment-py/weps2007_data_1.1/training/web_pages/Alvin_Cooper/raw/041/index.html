Thirteenth Generation

66. Elbert Cecil13 Lones (John Washington12, Henry Washington11, Absalum10, George9, Adam8, Johann George7 Lohnes, Johann George6, Leonhard5, Johannes4, Hanss3, Hans2, Leonhard1) #1838 was born in Niota, TN August 30, 1905. Elbert died August 6, 1953 in Niota, TN, at 47 years of age. His body was interred August 9, 1953 in Niota, TN.

He married Carrie Lou Webb in Niota, TN, March 30, 1929. (Carrie Lou Webb #1839.) Carrie was born in Rocky Springs, TN February 19, 1906. She was the daughter of Lorenzo Dow Webb and Margaret Lourenia Webb. Carrie died December 8, 1994 in Lawton, OK, at 88 years of age. Her body was interred December 14, 1994 in Niota, TN.

Elbert Cecil Lones and Carrie Lou Webb had the following children:

+ 68 i. John Edmon14 Lones was born February 6, 1930.

69 ii. Patricia Ann Lones #1846 was born in Niota, TN November 29, 1931.

70 iii. Margaret Jane Lones #1847 was born in Niota, TN November 21, 1937. She married Alvin Cooper. (Alvin Cooper #1945.)

71 iv. Thomas Doyle Lones #1848 was born in Niota, TN November 26, 1940. He married Shirley Ann Lonas in Rapid City, SD. (Shirley Ann Lonas #1949.) Shirley was born in Sturgis, SD September 16, 1944. Shirley died August 9, 1997 in Wetumka, Ok, at 52 years of age. Her body was interred August 14, 1997 in Sturgis, SD.

72 v. Linda Lou Lones #1849 was born in Niota, TN January 30, 1943.
