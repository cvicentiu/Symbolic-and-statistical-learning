She was born July 05, 1873 in Hooper, Dodge County, Nebraska, and died October 07, 1907. He married (2) MARY JULIA LOGAN SMITH Aft. 1915, daughter of (UNKNOWN) SMITH and (UNKNOWN) VALLIER.
