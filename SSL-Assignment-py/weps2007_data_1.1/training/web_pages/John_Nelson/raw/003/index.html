John B. Nelson Jr. is an actor and martial artist. He has appeared in Spike Lee's feature film Inside Man staring Denzel Washington, Jodie Foster, and Clive Owen. He has appeared in the History Channel's Russia: Engineering an Empire, and in Rip Off, a comedy TV show, as Dick Masters.

John has recently appeared as Mack in Fight Night, a feature length film about the shady world of underground fighting and online gambling, and as cop in the short comedy Solidarity, and as Matt in Obsession, a DV short film.
