John R. Nelson

Digital Sales Director

JOHN NELSON is the digital sales director for the Columbia Missourian. As such, he oversees advertising sales into eMprint (the new downloadable computer newspaper developed in conjunction with the Reynolds Institute), www.columbiamissourian.com, the Mobile Missourian (which allows the sending of text advertising messages to cell phones) and other electronic delivery platforms at the newspaper.

Nelson's interest in multi-media was developed early on, when he experimented with crayons on the TV screen for the old "Winky Dink" children's show. Having lived successfully well beyond his means ever since, Nelson shows advertisers how to stretch advertising budgets to get the best return on investment. Seriously, Nelson has almost three decades of media sales and marketing management experience with newspapers in California, Wisconsin, Kansas and Missouri.
