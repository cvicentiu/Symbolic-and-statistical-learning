23 March 2001, 12:00 pm
Mini and Junior latest
The Bristol mini section up to the age of 12 are hoping the rain will stop soon so that their matches against Frampton Cotterell can go ahead this Sunday 1st 10.30am.

The Bristol Mini & Junior club are also making an appeal for coaches and assistants. Mini/Midi and Junior coaching courses are available through
the club for those people are interested. The club boasts over 300 players between the ages of 6 and 18 but Chairman Chris Brockett comments, "We are a very succesful club that has a good vision for the future, but it is
always difficult to get enough volunteers to take on the coaching responsibilities. When they do join us they find it very rewarding and
enjoyable watching their young charges fulfilling their ambitions and playing their rugby".

Any one interested in joining the club as a player or coach/assistant is asked to contact us through these web pages or call Chris on 0785 5263287 or Ian on 07860 160060.

COLTS
Any player over 17 who is interested in joining next years Colts side should also contact the numbers above, we are currently organising the next seasons Colts team and matches and new players would be very welcome.
