Baji
Baji moved in with me on September 23. We met at the Henry County Humane Society animal shelter where I have been helping with the cats. I've never adopted from a shelter before, but there was something about Baji that made me want to take her home to live with me fur-ever. She seems to have no regrets about leaving the shelter and being my baby! As for me, I feel lucky to have found her.
