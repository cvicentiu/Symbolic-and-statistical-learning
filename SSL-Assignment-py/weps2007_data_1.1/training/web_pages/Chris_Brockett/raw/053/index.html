IJCNLP-05 Workshop

October 14, 2005
Jeju Island, South Korea

Workshop Program

8:50-9:00 Opening remarks
9:00-10:40 Session 1: phrase-level
Transforming a sentence end into news headline style
Satoshi Ikeda and Kazuhide Yamamoto

Automated generalization of phrasal paraphrases from the Web
Weigang Li

Automatic paraphrase discovery based on context and keywords between NE pairs
Satoshi Sekine

A class-oriented approach to building a paraphrase corpus
Atsushi Fujita and Kentaro Inui

10:40-11:10 break
11:10-12:50 Session 2: sentence-level
Automatic generation of paraphrases to be used as translation references in objective evaluation measures of machine translation
Yves Lepgae and Etienne Denoual

Using machine translation evaluation techniques to determine sentence-level semantic equivalence
Andrew Finch, Young Sook Hwang and Eiichiro Sumita

Evaluating contextual dependency of paraphrases using a latent variable model
Kiyonori Ohtake

Automatically constructing a corpus of sentential paraphrases
William B. Dolan and Chris Brockett

12:50-2:00 lunch
2:00-3:40 Session 3: discourse-level
Structural variation in generated health reports
Catalina Hallett and Donia Scott discourse level>

Automatic generation of large-scale paraphrases
Richard Power and Donia Scott

Towards statistical paraphrase generation: preliminary evaluations of grammaticality
Stephen Wan

Support vector machines for paraphrase identification and corpus construction
Chris Brockett and William B. Dolan

3:40-4:10 break
4:10-5:10 Session 4: panel and future plans
5:10 closing remarks
