Abstract: We describe a segmentation component that utilizes minimal syntactic knowledge to produce a lattice of word candidates for a broad coverage Japanese NL parser. The segmentor is a finite state morphological analyzer and text norrealizer designed to handle the orthographic variations characteristic of written Japanese, inclading alternate spellings, script variation, vowel extensions and word-intemtl parenthetical material.
