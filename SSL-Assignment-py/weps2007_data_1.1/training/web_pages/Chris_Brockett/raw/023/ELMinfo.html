The Evil Little Men have been playing their particular
variety of rock 'n roll since early 1938. Over the years,
the band has had 479 different members.

The lineup these days is:

Andrew Moss: Guitar, vocals, lyrics, and songwriting.
Todd Tibbetts: Bass and vocals.
Michelle Quigley: Saxophone and world travel.
Ben Thompson: Drums. Just drums.
