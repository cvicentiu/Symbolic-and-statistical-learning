Natural Language Processing (NLP) Reading Group at the University of Washington

Goal: To broaden our knowledge of the various sub-areas of natural language processing research. (For another reading group dedicated purely to machine translation research, see MTRG.)

Schedule:

There will be no NLP reading group this quarter (Winter 2007).
