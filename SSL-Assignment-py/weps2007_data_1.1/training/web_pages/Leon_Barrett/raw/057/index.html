About Oasis

The Oasis Sunday School class is a warm, friendly class who enjoys studying God's word together.  We welcome individuals and couples of all ages; but, just to give you some idea of our demographics, most of our members are thirty- to fifty-something and married with kids of all ages.  Our lead facilitator, Jerry Johnson, is a wonderful leader and an exceptional teacher who encourages discussion and class participation.  We are blessed with a number of musically-gifted class members, so we start each lesson by singing our favorite hymns.  We often use books to guide our studies, but we are not tied to any particular series of lessons.  Our discussions are lively, informative and often inspirational.  We laugh a lot, support each other in big and small ways, and really enjoy our time together.  We have social events outside of church, and we've found that our class is a great place to make life-long Christian friends.  We meet in room 206, and we try very hard to start Sunday School by 9:50.  We would love for you to join us!  To learn more, please email our prayer leader, Joe Cosper.

 
 

Oasis Mission and Vision Statement

 Oasis Sunday School class exists for the primary purpose of Bible study.  Our goal is to become more like Christ by growing in our knowledge and understanding of God's word.  We accept that not everyone has the same understanding or the same faith.  We welcome all who come seeking the truth in God's word. 

Oasis is a church family within a church family.  As such, we strive to love one another unconditionally as Christ loves us.  Our love is demonstrated through giving of our time, our talent, and by serving one another and our community.  Through fellowship and service, we are sharing in the love of Jesus with the world around us.

Oasis functions uniquely by offering a refreshingly interactive time of singing, prayer, and participatory learning during the Sunday School hour.  We strive to create an environment where all members feel comfortable actively participating.  We all share the responsibility of enriching one another's understanding of the word during our study time.  We have no final authority other than Jesus Christ, The Living Word.

Our name signifies a place of welcome relief.  Oasis is a warm and friendly group who welcome any and all seekers.  We hope to welcome new members to our little church within the church so that we may share more broadly the love we have for one another and for Jesus Christ.
