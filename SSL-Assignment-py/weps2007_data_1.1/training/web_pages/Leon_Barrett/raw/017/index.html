MINUTES OF THE SPECIAL SCHOOL BOARD MEETING OF ISD NO

MINUTES OF THE SPECIAL SCHOOL BOARD MEETING OF ISD NO. 38, RED LAKE, MINNESOTA, HELD ON FRIDAY, APRIL 1, 2005 AT 2:00 PM AT THE RED LAKE ELEMENTARY LIBRARY. 

 

MEMBERS PRESENT:  Arnold Pemberton, Keith Defoe, Roy Nelson, Kathryn Beaulieu, Dorothy Cobenais, Tom Barrett, Stuart Desjarlait, Superintendent, Sherri May, Secretary.

 

GUESTS AND DELEGATIONS:  Malcolm Wax, Delores Cloud-Hammitt, Monte Hammitt, Jean Whitefeather, Pat Mills, Duwayne Dow, Arlys Stillday, Ed Strong, Mary Kay Klein, Carol Coley, Rebecca Barrett, Leon Barrett, Charlys Blue, Chris Dunshee, Keith Lussier, Sr., Floyd Jourdain, Bob Meeks.

 

Meeting was called to order by Chairman Pemberton at 2:08 PM.

 

Moved by Dorothy Cobenais seconded by Tom Barrett to approve the agenda.  For 6, Opposed 0, Abstain 0.

 

HEARING OF DELEGATIONS: 

 

A.  Discussion of re-opening school.  The board discussed Deer Lake School site for high school.  Moved by Kathryn Beaulieu seconded by Roy Nelson to start Red Lake & Ponemah Elementary Schools April 4, 2005 & Red High School & Red Lake Middle School April 5, 2005.  For 2, Kathryn Beaulieu, Roy Nelson, Opposed 4, Arnold Pemberton, Keith Defoe, Dorothy Cobenais, Tom Barrett, Abstain 0.

 

Moved by Tom Barrett seconded by Keith Defoe to start school Monday, April 11, 2005.  For 6, Opposed 0, Abstain 0.

 

B.  Mary Kay Klein.  Introduced Shamus O'Mears  to the board.

 

C.  Pat Mills.  Pat Mills had emergency had to leave meeting.  DuWayne Dow informed the board the investigation is still going on.  Security in school buildings need to be improved.  Investigation going on, may be more people involved.  Would like schools to be safe before sending students back.

 

D.  Monte Hammitt.  Discussed the bonding.

 

E.  MSBA.  Offered their help to the board in anyway they need.

 

MISCELLANEOUS

 

Special school board meeting:  Wednesday, April 6, 2005 at 1:00 PM at the Administration Office.

 

 

ADJOURNMENT

 

Moved by Dorothy Cobenais seconded by Tom Barrett to adjourn.  For 6, Opposed 0, Abstain 0.  Adjournment at 5:15 PM.

 

 

 

Kathryn Beaulieu

Clerk, ISD No. 38

Red Lake,  MN

 

 

 

 

Sherri May

Secretary to Board of Education
