Washington University Department of Music in Arts & Sciences concerts -- April 14-28

[St. Louis, MO., 4-9-02]

Stephanie Scherrer presents Graduate Voice Recital in Graham Chapel April 14
Mezzo soprano Stephanie Scherrer, an MFA degree candidate at Washington University, will present a graduate voice recital for the Department of Music in Arts & Sciences at 7 p.m. Sunday, April 14, in the University's Graham Chapel.

The program includes songs of Henry Purcell, Benjamin Britten and Maurice Ravel as well as Gustav Mahler's "Rueckertlieder. " Scherrer will be accompanied by Henry Palkes on piano.

The concert is free and open to the public. Graham Chapel is located just north of the Mallinckrodt Student Center, 6445 Forsyth Blvd. For more information, call (314) 935-4841.

Great choruses sing-along at Washington University April 14
The Washington University Department of Music in Arts & Sciences will present a sing-along of great choruses from opera and oratorio at 3 p.m. Sunday, April 14, in the University's Graham Chapel.
John Stewart, director of vocal activities, directs the concert, which features the Washington University Vocal Ensembles and organist William Partridge, Jr.

The program includes the Bridal Chorus from Richard Wagner's Lohengrin; the Anvil Chorus from Giuseppe Verdi's Il trovatore; the Humming Chorus from Giacomo Puccini's Madame Butterfly; Dona nobis pacem from Bach's B-minor Mass; and Hallelujah from Beethoven's The Mount of Olives.

The event is free and open to the public. Graham Chapel is located just north of the Mallinckrodt Student Center, 6445 Forsyth Blvd. For more information, call (314) 935-4841.

Those who wish to may sit in special sections arranged according to voice type (soprano, alto, tenor, baritone), though those who choose not to sing are also welcome to attend. Copies of the music will be available for those who do not bring their own scores.

Scott Levin and Karen Hetzler give voice recital for Department of Music April 15
Junior baritone Scott Levin and soprano Karen Hetzler, a master's candidate in vocal performance in Washington University's Department of Music in Arts & Sciences, will give a voice recital at 8 p.m. Monday, April 15, in Graham Chapel.

The concert is free and open to the public. Graham Chapel is located just north of the Mallinckrodt Student Center, 6445 Forsyth Blvd. For more information, call (314) 935-5581.

The pair will be joined by Henry Palkes, accompanist in the Department of Music.

The concert is free and open to the public and is sponsored by the Department of Music in Arts & Sciences. Karl Umrath Lounge is located in Karl Umrath Hall, just north of the Mallinckrodt Student Center, 6445 Forsyth Blvd. For more information, call (314) 935-4841.
