Leon Barrett to present guitar recital in Umrath Lounge March 27

By Liam Otten

Washington University senior Leon Barrett will present a guitar recital at 4 p.m. Saturday, March 27, in the university's Karl Umrath Hall Lounge.

The program includes music of John Dowland, J. S. Bach, Franz Joseph Haydn, and Benjamin Britten.

The concert is free and open to the public and is sponsored by the Department of Music in Arts & Sciences. Karl Umrath Hall is located just north of the Mallinckrodt Student Center, 6445 Forsyth Blvd. For more information, call (314) 935-4841.
