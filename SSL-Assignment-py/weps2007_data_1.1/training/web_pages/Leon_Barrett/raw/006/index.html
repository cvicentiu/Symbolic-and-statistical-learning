Minnie Elzora Byrd


541. Minnie Elzora9 Byrd (Sarah Frances8 Breazeale, Jefferson Barzella7, Drury Buchman6, Drury Woods5 Breazell, Drury4 Brazeal, Henry3, Henry2 Brazeel, Henry1) was born in Neshoba Co, MS December 10, 1883. Minnie died January 11, 1953 in Clarkedale, Crittenden Co, AR. Her body was interred at Mars Hill Cemetery in Winston Co, MS.

She married Willie Evans Barrett December 5, 1900 in Leake Co, MS. Willie was born July 2, 1880 in Neshoba Co, MS. Willie died July 21, 1958 in Brazoria, Brazoria, TX. TX Death Certificate #37317 for Willie Evans Barrett states his DOB as July 1, 1880, DOD as July 21, 1958. Street address is 109 Alabama St., and had lived in Brazoria for 5 years. Occupation is Minister, Place of Birth is Mississippi. Father's name is Limiel Barrett, mother's name unknown. Informant is Mrs. Hazel Crommer. Cause of death is acute internal hemorrhage due to aortic aneurism, due to old age. Physician is Dr. Montgomery of Angleton, who treated Willie from April 1958 until his death. Also, remains will be removed to Philadelphia, MS for burial at Mars (sp. Myrs) Hill Cemetery.

His body was interred at Mars Hill Cemetery in Winston Co, MS.
