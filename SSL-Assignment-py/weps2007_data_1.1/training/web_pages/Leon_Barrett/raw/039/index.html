6 weddings, 9 engage......................

Weddings...........

Kuykendall | Spamer

Debra A. Kuykendall and Jay Spamer were married May 21, 2005 in a private residence in Scottsdale, Ariz. A reception was held at the Center for Performing Arts in Munster.

Mr. and Mrs. Spamer spent their honeymoon in Rome, Italy. They live in Scottsdale.

Parents of the couple are Ron and Mary Ann Kuykendall of Surprise, Ariz., and Robert and Marge Spamer of Princeton, N.J.

The bride is a graduate of Bishop Noll Institute and University of Illinois. She is employed at International Cruise & Travel.

The bridegroom is a graduate of Rutgers Law, University of Massachussettes Amherst. He is employed at Thomas & Thomas Appel, P.C.

Marty Coughlin was maid of honor and Robert Spamer Jr. was best man.

Carter | Jones

Candice Carter and Brian Jones were married April 30, 2005, at Augsburg Lutheran Church in Porter. A reception was held at Casa Del Roma in Valparaiso.

Mr. and Mrs. Jones spent their honeymoon in Florida. They live in Porter.

Parents of the couple are Wayne and Peggy Carter of Chesterton, Sandy Boothe of Porter and William Jones of Lakewood, Calif.

The bride is a graduate of Chesterton High School. She is Burberry.

The bridegroom is a graduate of Lakewood High School, Lakewood, Calif. He is assistant manager at KMart.

Deanna Brueggert was matron of honor and Brad Jones was best man.

Moore | Kruit

Rebecca L. Moore and Jonathan M. Kruit were married June 18, 2005, at St. John's United Church of Christ in Crown Point. A reception was held at Operating Engineers Local 150 Hall in Merrillville.

Mr. and Mrs. Kruit spent their honeymoon in Jamaica. They live in Crown Point.

Parents of the couple are Kenneth and Caryn Moore of Highland, and Terry and Laura Kruit of Hebron.

The bride is a graduate of Highland High School and Indiana University Northwest. She is employed by the School City of East Chicago.

The bridegroom is a graduate of Crown Point High School. He is employed at Crown Cabinet.

Heather Mason was matron of honor and Carl Kruit was best man.

Allen | Sparger

Brenna Allen and Marvin Sparger were married June 18, 2005, at First Baptist Church in West Lafayette, Ohio. A reception was held at River Grille in West Lafayette.

Mr. and Mrs. Sparger spent their honeymoon in the bridegroom's home city of Hammond. They live at Ft. Bliss, El Paso, Texas.

Parents of the couple are Tracy and Joann Allen of West Lafayette, Ohio, and Robert and Sandra Albrecht of Hammond.

The bride is a graduate of Rich Ridgewood. She serves with the United States Army and will be stationed in South Korea.

The bridegroom is a graduate of Morton High School. He serves with the United States Army and will be stationed in South Korea.

Tamara Cognion was honor attendant and Jason Sparger was best man.

Owens | McBroom

Jessica Lynn Owens and Benjamin Albert McBroom were married June 4, 2005, at the home of Jon and Ginger Evans of Dillsboro, Ind. An outdoor reception was held immediately after the ceremony.

Mr. and Mrs. McBroom spent their honeymoon in Gatlinburg, Tenn. They live in Dillsboro.

Parents of the couple are Kenneth and Jeri Owens of Vincennes, Ind., and Frank and Cheryl Lambert of Portage and Gary McBroom of Hammond.

The bride is attends Northern Kentucky University.

The bridegroom is a graduate of Vincennes University. He is employed by the Dearbourn County Sheriff's Department.

Lindsay Like and Ginger Evans were honor attendants and Derek DePew and Jon Evans were best men.

Walters | Barrett

Amanda Louise Walters and James Leon Barrett Jr. were married Feb. 26, 2005, at Trinity Lutheran Church of Valparaiso. A reception was held at Casa del Roma in Valparaiso.

Mr. and Mrs. Barrett spent their honeymoon in Athens, Greece. They live in McHenry, Ill.

Parents of the couple are Maggie and Thomas Walters of Valparaiso, Patricia Walters of Youngstown, Ohio, and Patricia and James Barrett of McHenry, Ill.

The bride is a graduate of Augustana College in Rock Island, Ill. She holds a supervisory position working with developmentally disabled adults for the Pioneer Center of McHenry County.

The bridegroom is a graduate of McHenry High School. He is manager for Applebee's Restaurant.

Carrie Reed was matron of honor, Kristina Bedard was maid of honor and Eric Thennes was best man.
