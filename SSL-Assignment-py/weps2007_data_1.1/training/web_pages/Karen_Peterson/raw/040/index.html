Profile of Peterson Economics’ Staff

Jon Peterson graduated Phi Beta Kappa from Dartmouth College in 1991 with a B.A. in geography, and then earned an M.S. in economic geography from the Pennsylvania State University. He is joined at Peterson Economics by Karen Peterson, Chief Operating Officer; Jean Anderson, Associate; and Chris Dorociak, Associate. Karen Peterson, co-founder of Peterson Economics, graduated Magna Cum Laude from Dartmouth College in 1991 with a B.A. in anthropology and environmental studies; and then earned a law degree from the University of California at Berkeley. Jean Anderson graduated Phi Beta Kappa from George Washington University in 2003 with a B.A. in international affairs. Chris Dorociak graduated Phi Beta Kappa from the University of Florida in 2004 with a B.A. in economics, and then earned a Master of Professional Studies from the Department of Applied Economics and Management at Cornell University.
