01/31/07

Reader asks about bridge toll transponders

Posted by Karen Peterson @ 11:51:32 am

Arthur Kroeger posted the following note at askeditors@thenewstribune.com:

The News Tribune,

In the past I have seen advertisements from the WSDOT about the bridge transponders, but have seen none recently. There have also been articles which talked about the need for the transponders and the estimate of what the toll will be using one. But, I have seen nothing which tells some other needed info. For instance:

How and where do we obtain a transponder?

How much will a transponder cost us to obtain, if anything?

If I have two or more vehicles do I need to have a transponder for each vehicle, or can they be transferred from one vehicle to another?

If one my vehicles is a motor home, will the fee charged using a transponder be the same as for a smaller car?

There must be other things that the travelling public will want to know about transponders. I think it would be a good service if you would print an article giving answers to these questions and any other pertinent facts.

Thank you,

Arthur Kroeger
Allyn, WA
ahkroeg@aol.com

Here are the answers Arthur's questions. More information can be found on the WSDOT website.

How and where do we obtain a transponder?
Answer: Signing up is free of charge. You will be able to open a Good To Go! account over the internet, by phone or through the mail although the stickers are not yet available. WSDOT asks you to sign up at their website and then they’ll let you know when it is ready. The url: https://business.wsdot.wa.gov/GoodToGo/signup.aspx

How much will a transponder cost us to obtain, if anything?
Answer: Initially, e-stickers will be distributed free of charge to drivers who open a prepaid Good To Go! account with WSDOT.

If I have two or more vehicles do I need to have a transponder for each vehicle, or can they be transferred from one vehicle to another?
Answer: Customers can request more than one e-sticker, and all e-stickers can be linked to a single account. The e-stickers cannot be transferred from one vehicle to another. Removing an e-sticker destroys and deactivates it.

If one my vehicles is a motor home, will the fee charged using a transponder be the same as for a smaller car?
Answer: The bridge financial plan currently assumes an initial $3.00 toll for the first year. When the Tacoma Narrows Bridge opens, the assumed $3.00 toll would be the same for all vehicles, including vehicles with additional axles. However, all toll setting and adjustments will be made according to a schedule to be determined by the Transportation Commission, and vehicles with additional axles may be subject to a different toll schedule in the future.

Source: WSDOT website

Thanks for asking, Arthur.
