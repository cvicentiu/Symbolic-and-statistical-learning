PETERSON, John C.

Wednesday, December 10, 2003

  • Printable Version
  • Email This Article

PETERSON, John C. - At rest in Stanford, California, December 6, 2003. Beloved husband of the late Mary E. Peterson; loving father of Gary J. Peterson of Spokane, WA, Richard J. Peterson of Menlo Park and the late Scott T. Peterson; dearest father-in-law of Cecily A. Peterson and Karen Peterson; devoted grandfather of two; great-grandfather of four; and great-great-grandfather of one; dear brother of William and Donald Peterson and the late Bud Peterson and the late Mary McCarthy; dearest brother-in-law of Helen Peterson of San Francisco. A native of Denver, CO. Age 83. J.C. Peterson Company 1942-1953 Building St. Charles School, Selby Lane School and West Lake School. Head supervisor with Wagner-Martinez Construction Company Building Stanford Shopping Center 1955 and Stanford Hospital 1957. Visitation will be held on Tuesday, December 9, 2003 from 2:00 - 9:00 P.M. and Wednesday, December 10, 2003 from 9:00 A.M. - 9:00 P.M. with Rosary Service at 8:00 P.M. at Redwood Chapel 847 Woodside Rd., Redwood City. Friends and family are also invited to attend the Graveside Services on Thursday, December 11, 2003 at 12 Noon at Alta Mesa Memorial Park, 695 Arastradero Rd., Palo Alto. Donations to the American Heart Association, 1710 Gilbreth Rd., Burlingame, CA 94010, in Memory of John C. Peterson preferred. Arrangements by REDWOOD CHAPEL 847 Woodside Rd. Redwood City, CA (650) 369-5537
