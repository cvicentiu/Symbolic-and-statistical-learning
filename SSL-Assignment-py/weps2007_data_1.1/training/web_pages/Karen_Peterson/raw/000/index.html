Karen Peterson and Dancers Inc., founded in 1989 as a non profit dance organization has been dedicated to the development, education and presentation of dance through quality based workshops and performances. The company's repertory is performed by dancers with and without disabilities and an annual season of new work fosters creative opportunities for choreographers and dance artists in the community. The organization creates and presents "mixed-ability" dance, promotes International Cultural Exchange and provides opportunities for Miami based dance artists at Excello, the company's black box performance venue established in 2002.
