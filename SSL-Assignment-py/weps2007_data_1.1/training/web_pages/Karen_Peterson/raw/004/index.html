Karen Peterson and Dancers perform at
the Edinburgh Fringe Festival, Scotland

KAREN PETERSON AND DANCERS performed at the EDINBURGH FRINGE FESTIVAL for a five-performance series at the Garage Chapiteau Theatre, from August 12th through the 16th, 2003. The company performed within The 23rd International American Choreographers' Showcase. A new "mixed-ability" work, "Perfume de Gardenias", featured dancers JOHN BEAUREGARD, LETTY BASSART, JENNY LLEWELLYN-JONES AND KAREN PETERSON. The dance was be presented in Miami on April of 2004, within the company's annual concert season.

The Edinburgh Festival draws thousands of people from all across the world and promotes the work of international artists. Special thanks to the International Cultural Exchange Program of the MIAMI DADE COUNTY CULTURAL AFFAIRS COUNCIL, THE BOARD OF COUNTY COMMISSIONERS AND THE STATE OF FLORIDA DIVISION OF CULTURAL AFFAIRS, for making the travel and performance opportunity possible.
