Diet is a Four-letter Word

 
Your Future is up to you

  
A Brand New Book By Karen Peterson

This book is the story of my journey from overweight and yo-yo dieting to slow permanent weight loss. I didn’t use a diet. I ate my favorite foods and I don’t work out at the gym.

The common sense truths that are offered in this book are the results of understanding that I was telling myself lies that allowed me to overeat and gorge myself on foods that were not good for my body.

This book is the trip I went on to discover these lies that I was living and how to change them into truths.
