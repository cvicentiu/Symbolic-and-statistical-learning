Guest Speaker Elizabeth "E" Slater,
from In Short Direct Marketing, will present two marketing sessions. She will also conduct one-on-one appointments throughout the conference. Bring your direct marketing samples and ideas for E to review.
Read E's Bio

Guest Speaker Karen Peterson,
of Davidson-Peterson Associates will present the results of consumer research conducted over the past six months to assist in "Defining the Michigan Wine Experience." Melinda Remer and Dave Morris from Travel Michigan will also be available to answer questions about the implications of this research for Travel Michigan’s marketing planning. Results of Phase One of this two-year project were presented at the 2005 Annual Meeting and are available on the Council’s website under "Council Business" in Industry Resources. Davidson-Peterson conducted consumer focus groups in Detroit and Chicago in October. Tapes of the focus groups will be available for viewing at Crystal Mountain in the exhibit/registration area. An online survey was conducted to further contribute to an understanding of consumer attitudes and behaviors regarding wine, Michigan wine and Michigan winery touring.
