Dancer & Choreographer Karen Peterson

Miami, Florida
Photo Credit: Claudio Luzarraga


Karen Peterson is the Artistic Director of Peterson and Dancers and the Excello Dance Space.  She graduated from the Boston Conservatory of Music and has been committed to quality performances, educational possibilities and community outreach in Florida since 1989. In addition to the fellowship award, she has also been awarded New Forms Florida Grants, an Artist Access Development Grant, a “Stay Pure Award” from the Sauza Tequila Company / South Florida Art Center, a City of Miami Award for her work with persons with disabilities and the first annual danceAble Award from the Florida Dance Association and Tigertail Productions. She has conducted workshops and produced performances throughout the United States, Brazil, Italy and Scotland, and is a founding member of “danceAble”, an annual conference of “mixed-ability” research and “dancersImprovgroup”, an improvisational womens troupe.
