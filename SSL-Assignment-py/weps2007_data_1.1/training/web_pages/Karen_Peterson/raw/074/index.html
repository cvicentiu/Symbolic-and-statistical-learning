Karen Howard Married

  • Print
  • Save
Published: August 26, 1985

The marriage of Karen Peterson Howard, an actress, to Victor Montagne Earl 3d, general counsel for the accounting firm of Peat, Marwick, Mitchell & Co., took place Saturday at the bride's home in Amagansett, L.I. The Rev. Frederick Rapp performed the Episcopal ceremony. Mrs. Earle is the daughter of A. Hawley Peterson of Naples, Fla., and the late Medora Peterson. The bridegroom is the son of Mr. Earle Jr. of Bridgehampton, L.I., and the late Marian Webster. Previous marriages for both bride and bridegroom ended in divorce.
