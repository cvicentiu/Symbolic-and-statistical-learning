Psychic healing program to be aired on Channel 26
Staff Report
Article Launched: 10/24/2006 12:38:50 AM PDT

Sounding Board, a cable television program produced by Soroptimist International of Novato, will offer a broadcast on "psychic healing" at 7 p.m. Wednesday.

The program, to be broadcast on Channel 26 of Novato Public Access Television, will feature Karen Peterson, a professional "psychic medium" who has helped people overcome their grief at the loss of a loved one.
