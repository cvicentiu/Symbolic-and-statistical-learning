Karen E. Peterson, R.Ph., J.D.
Pharmacist - Attorney
Pharmacists Mutual Insurance Company

Karen Peterson is a pharmacist and an attorney. Karen graduated from the University of Iowa College of Pharmacy. Following graduation, she served as Executive Resident with the American Society of Consultant Pharmacists (ASCP). Upon completing her residency in pharmacy association management, Karen moved to Portland, Oregon, where she worked as a long-term care pharmacist. After serving as a staff pharmacist, she was promoted to pharmacy manager of a specialized compounding and long-term care pharmacy.

Karen then moved to Buffalo, New York, where she worked in a home health and hospice pharmacy setting. Upon acceptance to law school at the University at Buffalo, Karen took a position as a staff pharmacist with a national chain, where she worked while attending law school full time.

Karen graduated cum laude from the University at Buffalo Law School. She then joined Pharmacists Mutual Insurance Company as a pharmacist-attorney in the pharmacy professional liability claims area. She is admitted to the bar in New York and Iowa. She is a member of several professional organizations, including the American Society for Pharmacy Law, ASCP, APhA, and the Iowa Pharmacy Association.
