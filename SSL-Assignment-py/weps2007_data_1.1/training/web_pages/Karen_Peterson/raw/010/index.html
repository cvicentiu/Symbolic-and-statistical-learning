  Karen S. Peterson

Karen Peterson leads the firm's thriving real estate practice.  She concentrates her entire law practice in the area of Real Estate Law.  She is a leader in the real estate community in Central Virginia and is a well respected practitioner in all aspects of the industry including financing, brokerage, and construction.   She is a member or affiliate member of  the Richmond Association of Realtors, Women's Council of Realtors and the  Home Building Association of Richmond. 

Ms. Peterson is a CRESPA registered Real Estate Settlement Agent.
