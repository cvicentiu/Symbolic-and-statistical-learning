Philosophy excites the imagination, expands and broadens the mind like no other subject. At Liverpool, our department is a thriving community for independent and creative thought.
Research   Teaching   International
Our research interests are broad and include Philosophy of Mind, Ancient and Medieval Philosophy, Indian Philosophy, Ethics and more.   Our teaching excellence is reflected in our score of 24/24 in the most recent teaching review.   We have an outstanding international reputation for Indian Philosophy and strong international links with philosophers working in this area.
