Dr. Stephen Clark, DO

Dr. Stephen Clark was born in Salt Lake City and raised in Southeastern Idaho. He is a bilingual family Practice Physician who provides obstetrical care for the Community Health Center patients.

Dr. Clark has been employed by Intermountain HealthCare since September 2005 after completing 7 years of active duty service in The United States Navy. He enjoys activities such as SCUBA diving, cycling and surfing. He is the father of 5 daughters.

Education

  • Ricks College, Rexburg Idaho: Associates in Sociology
  • Utah State University, Logan, Utah: Bachelors of Science in Biology
  • Michigan State University, Lansing Michigan: Doctor of Osteopathic Medicine

Post-graduate Training

  • Camp Pendleton Navy Family Practice Residency program, Oceanside California

Leadership Positions

  • Student Body President, Ricks College
  • President Pre-medical Honor Society, Utah State University
  • Chief Resident, Camp Pendleton Navy Family Practice Program
  • Vice president medical staff, Naval Hospital Guam

Certification

  • Board Certified, American Board of Family Practice
