    Notes

    Littell, p. 77 says Stephen married Joanna Miller, daughter of Jacob, of Long Hill, and went to Butler County, Ohio. After Stephen's death, Joanna married Jacob Morris and had two daughters, Mary and Hannah Morris. Mary Morris m. William Jordan, and Hannah m. Charles Porter. Joanna lived with the Porter family after Jacob Morris died, in Vermilion County, Indiana.
