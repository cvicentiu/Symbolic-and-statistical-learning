E-mail confidentiality

You have requested to send us a message. We believe in protecting your privacy. When you send us e-mail, we won't share your e-mail address with anyone outside HUD. We'll only use it to respond to your requests.

Don't forget to include in your e-mail message:

  • your city and state, so we can ensure that the right person in your local area responds to your message
  • the location (city, state, street address) if you're writing about a HUD property
