Basic overview

This website is designed to allow for shopping one of the finest fashion jewelry lines in the USA.  It is meant to be available to my existing customers and to help  find new ones.

Fashions by Hugo are represented by a sales force covering most of the country. Every representative is permitted to offer the line in a specific territory.   My region includes most of New York, part of Pennsylvania and part of New Jersey.
