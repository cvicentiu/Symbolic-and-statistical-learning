Stephen Clark
 

Born in 1959 in St Albans, England. Moved to Normandy in 1985, living in Neuchâtel, Switzerland since 1998.

 
About the Artist

A self-taught amateur artist, I started painting in the 1990s. I have always been attracted by mountains, and so naturally tend to paint mountain landscapes, although not exclusively so.
