Christmas Believe (TM) is set in the fictional northernmost town of Bumpton Bay. Little 10-year-old Sammy has christened the day before Christmas with a new name that, for him, is descriptive of how faith works at Christmastime. His older brother, Stinz (a nickname accidentally given him by Sammy) has grown up hardened and cynical. He rebuffs little Sammy's innocent beliefs about the season but longs for more of something. Their mother, Margaret, is the only bright star in their lives, and works diligently to provide for their needs and keep them both on track. They live on the poor side of town, Lower Bumpton, in a one-room converted garage. Just prior to this particular Christmas, Margaret suddenly learns of the tragic death of her husband in a bizarre accident. Joe had walked out on her and the boys ten years prior but has left behind a special and surprising treasure. The story reveals how Sammy, Margaret, and especially Stinz, come to terms with this second loss of a father and husband. In the midst of their pain, each experiences the joy, wonder, and blessings of Christmas.
