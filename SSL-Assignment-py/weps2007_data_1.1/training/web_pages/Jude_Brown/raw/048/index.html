 ST JUDE  Brown  /  1987  /  16.1 HH
GODSWALK* - Champion UK Sprinter; 6 wins, Royal Ascot King's Stand S-G1. Champion UK Sire (winners); Champion UK Juvenile Sire (winners), twice; Sire of winners of $17 million including 38 stakes winners. His sons at stud include CELESTIAL DANCER*, ST JUDE, POTTER McQUEEN. He is broodmare sire of winners of $16.5 million - 20 stakes winners including MOONSHINE, RACER'S EDGE, SHOWBROOK, RENO BELLE, KEY TO MY HEART, DANCER'S CHOICE, CLASSIC RULER, LIVELIHOOD, etc.
