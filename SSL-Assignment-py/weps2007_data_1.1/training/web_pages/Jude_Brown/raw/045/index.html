Jude Browne - Research Associate

Research Gender segregation and inequality in labour markets, including corporate governance strategies for diversity and inclusion. Political and social theories of equality, capabilities, and social rights. Quality of employment, labour market operations, future of professional work and social risk.

Projects
Corporate Governance Programme
  • Social Dialogue, Employment and Territories: towards a European politics of capabilities
Email   jmb63@cam.ac.uk
Personal Background
PhD in Social and Political Sciences (ESRC Scholar, Cambridge). Fellow of Downing College, Cambridge. Research Fellow, Centre for Research in the Arts, Social Sciences and Humanities.
