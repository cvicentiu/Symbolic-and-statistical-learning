St. Mary Magdalene Dedicates New Church In Coweta County

Clergy and altar servers gather at the altar during the Liturgy of the Eucharist at St. Mary Magdalene Church’s dedication Feb. 8. The San Damiano Cross behind and over the altar is a focal piece of the worship space. The 10-foot by 12-foot cross was painted by Atlanta’s Grant Park artist Jim Yarbrough. (Photo by Michael Alexander)

NEWNAN - St. Mary Magdalene was established as an east Coweta County mission of Holy Trinity Church in 1999 and quickly outgrew three temporary worship sites before building and moving this year into its new church, which was dedicated Feb. 8.

Catholic Credit Union Group Committed To Serving

ATLANTA - At a special Mass of dedication for the Catholic division of the Georgia Federal Credit Union, members and employees honored the history of the organization while looking ahead to its bright future.

Held Feb. 2 at Immaculate Heart of Mary Church, the Mass was celebrated by Archbishop-emeritus John F. Donoghue and concelebrated by Msgr. Stephen Churchwell, head of the Catholic Advisory Board for GFCU.
