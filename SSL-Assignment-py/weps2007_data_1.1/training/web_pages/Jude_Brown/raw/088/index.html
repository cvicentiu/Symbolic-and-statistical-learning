  • North Ayrshire Skate Park | 0 comments | View/add comments
    Construction starts on possibly the 3rd Largest outdoor concrete skatepark in Europe. It will be located next to Auchenharvie Leisure Centre in Stevenston Which is in the West Coast Of SCOTLAND.The project has taken 3 years to get to this stage and will be operated by the North Ayrshire Skate Group. The park will include 3 metre high anti-climb fencing a gate entry system, full time supervision,office ,shop, toilets,first aid room, workshop,entertainment arena,eating area, floodlighting and CCTV.You can see plans on our website.

  • Indoor Skate/Bike training centers - Cedar Rapids Ia | 0 comments | View/add comments
    We have one outdoor skate/bike park for half-pipe riding and such(exposed to the weather), and one BMX dirt track. I am desperately seeking to have someone help me with the idea of having an indoor skate/bike training center so that these kids can stay local and not have to go to Waterloo or Davenport for these services. I am giving myself about a year to get to a financial stand point to begin building process on one at that time. If you are interested in donating funds for this project, please email me at tlevans@mcleodusa.com and we can discuss it in further detail. I believe with the number of youth in this area that have an interest in this particular game/sport, it will be a huge gathering ground for these kids and could promote some big names to come in and bring sponsors in the door. This type of place is not offered near cedar rapids and would be a benefit to the kids and the parents who drive them around.
