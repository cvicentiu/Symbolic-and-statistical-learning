Bio

I'm Matthew Jude Brown, aka Morven (my ever-present online handle). Born February 7, 1973 in Hampstead, a borough of London in England. Raised all over the UK, latterly Oxfordshire; went to college in London and earned a B.Eng. in Computing from Imperial College. Worked as an anti-virus researcher and software developer from '94 through '97, and as a UNIX systems administrator thereafter. I married a Californian girl, Alana, in 1996; she lived with me in England through late 97, after which I moved to Southern California where I've been ever since. Lived for the past five or so years in Anaheim, home of Disneyland.

I was obsessed with photography and cameras as a kid, and have rediscovered that fascination in my adult life. I got myself a Toshiba digital camera with a now-paltry 1.3 megapixels; after using that for a few years I obtained a Kodak DX6440, with which I really took off. I've added a few film SLRs (Canon T70, Minolta XG-M) since then, as well as a Polaroid 360 Land Camera and a few other oddities.

My fascinations include trains, transportation, the urban landscape and its inhabitants.
