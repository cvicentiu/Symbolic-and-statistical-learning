Subject: Engagment break off

Question
My ex-finace broke off the wedding 2 weeks ago and I know we will not be getting back together. My mom said that I need to send out notes to people that I sent out save the date cards too. What kind of wording should I use, and what kind of cards.

Thank you,
Deb

Answer
Dear Deb,

I'm always sorry to hear about this happening, but in the long run it's less of a heart-ache than going into a marriage that doesn't work.

You can send out a note saying that you just want to inform them that the wedding will not take place.  You don't owe anyone an explanation.  If you had sent the invitations, then the wording would be more formal announcement such as:

Mr. and Mrs. Andrew Jay Forrester
are obliged to recall their invitations
to the marriage of their daughter
Deborah May
to
Mr. Nicholas Jude Brown
as the marriage will not take place.


Hope this helps.  Please let me know if I can be of any further assistance.
