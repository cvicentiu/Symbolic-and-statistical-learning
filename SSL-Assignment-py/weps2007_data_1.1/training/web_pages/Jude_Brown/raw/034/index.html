It has been several days sence you last saw Jude. You would sit in your room and cry all day. You haven't eaten in a while. You lost 13 pounds. All you dreamed about was Jude. Her being tortured by the demons and vampires. The angels were doing everthing they could to find the destination of Jude. The angel from the night Jude was taken was now "part of your team". She hung out with the boys. She was the angel of love. She was sorta like cupid. But cupid was not real. Her name was Amoree (A-MORE-E).

Jhonz and Hanz walked in with a tray on a cloud. The tray had smoked salmon, brocolli, water and a beautiful rose. They were saying things to you. But you were thinking of Jude again. You had no clue what they were saying. Hanz noticed your frown and sat by you. Jhonz on the other side. They hugged you so hard you had no breath left. You had to laugh at the way you sounded when you tried to talk. You all laughed. They left the room, and you decided to eat the food. Besides, you hadn't eaten in days.

You fell asleep clutching the rose. You dreamed of Jude. She was sitting in a flower bed. She picked a rose and smelled it. Then the roses turned to ash. The freen grass turned to dirt. flames swirled around us. Jude's brown hair was now white. Glowing red eyes. Pale as ever. Her outfit was a black skin tight dress. She smiled reveiling vampire teeth. You ran to her and graped her. Then you felt her teeth sink into your neck. You fell to the ash ground paralized. Then you awoke screaming. An invisible hand clasped your mouth. You could no longer scream.

"Yura, honey." the invisible hand appeared. And was attached to Quitich.

"I wouldn't scream if I were you." he emoved his hand. You began to scream but his hand met your lips once again. He swayed his hand in the air. A a bubble appeared with Jude in it. She was in a cage. She was crying softly.

"Let my sister go Quitich." you whispered.

"Me? Quitich. How dare you. I am Vivik." he smiled playing with your face.

"I don't care, just let go of my sister."

"May I have you then my sweet?"

"Yes! Just let her go." you put your head down. You just hoped for Jude to be safe.

"Thank you my darling Yura." he flipped his hair back with his hand. Then swayed his hand once more. Jude appeared with a thump. The you heard Hanz tap the door twice. Jude looked up at you. You hugged her for a second then chains appeared around your neck, wrists, and ankles. Vivik tugged and he entered his portal. Hanz and Jhonz burst open the door and eyes went wide. Your head was all that was left to enter the portal. Jude's chains and mouth peice disappeared. She began screamming your name. Jhonz held her. Hanz was shoked. Your head entered the portal and you vanished.
