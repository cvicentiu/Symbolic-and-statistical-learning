Monique Grier (TEAM, New Jersey, fall '02-'03) was promoted in May 2006 to Manager, HR Planning and Administrative Services within Corporate Human Resources at Prudential Financial, Inc. In Monique's new role, she will use her financial knowledge and technical expertise to focus on supporting the HR Talent Acquisition Group and HR Planning and Administrative Services Team. Monique joined the HR Reporting Unit seven years ago as a Senior Financial Analyst. During this time, Monique was integral in streamlining the Human Resources and Community Resources planning process and monthly reporting while providing excellent customer service. In addition to producing a quality work product, Monique is a role model for all associates. She is an active member in Toastmasters, BLF (Black Leadership Forum), NABA (National Association of Black Accountants) and the Prudential Tutoring Program.

Janine Chupa (LEAD, New Jersey, fall '00-'01), Colgate-Palmolive Company, was was honored in May 2006 with the Tribute to Women in Industry (TWIN) Award. The 2006 TWIN Awards, which stands for Tribute to Women and INdustry, was sponsored by the YWCA in Central New Jersey. The TWIN Awards Program honors women who demonstrate excellence and top leadership. The TWIN Awards selection criteria include having senior-level functional management responsibility, possessing outstanding leadership qualities and serving as a successful role model for other women transitioning into executive positions. For more information, visit www.ywca.org.

Jude Brown (LEAD-San Francisco, spring '04-'05) was promoted in May 2006 to Director of Merchandising, Williams-Sonoma eCommerce. Jude has been with Williams-Sonoma for 11 years, briefly as a merchant with retail before transitioning to the catalog team where she held increasing levels of responsibility. Jude joined the eCommerce team in 2000. Since that time, Jude has played a key role in the development of William-Sonoma's online merchandising efforts, including crafting their current merchandising strategies for eCommerce and iBridal over the past year. Jude and her team also developed new infrastructure to allow Williams-Sonoma to better report against and manage critical online merchandising metrics. Jude has also driven the merchandising projects for the brand wide bridal initiative.
