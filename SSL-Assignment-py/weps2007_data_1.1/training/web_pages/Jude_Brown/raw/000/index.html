Chris Brown visits St. Jude

Photography provided by Peter Barta (BMC)

Jive Records recording artist Chris Brown, 17, visited St. Jude Children's Research Hospital on September 14 just prior to taking the stage for his concert that same evening at the FedEx Forum in Memphis, Tennessee. Brown donated 25 cents from each ticket sold at the concert to St. Jude. Brown toured the hospital, visited in-patient rooms, signed autographs and took photos with many patient fans. He also stopped by the Ronald McDonald House to see St. Jude patients before heading off to his show.
