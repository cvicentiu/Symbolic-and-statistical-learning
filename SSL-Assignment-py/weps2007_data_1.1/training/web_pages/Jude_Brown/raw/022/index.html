Jude Brown

Jude, along with husband Frank (The Soap Maker), own Natural Selections - The Island Hemp Shop on School Road.  Her son, Matt, is a waterman and carpenter on the island.  Her daughter-in-law, Mary, also works at The Village Craftsmen. 

Her beautiful, 4-year-old granddaughter, Elicia, is the delight of her grandma's heart! 

Her son, Travis, who worked for The Village Craftsmen from age 14 to 21, is now attending college in Indianapolis.

"After living here for 10 years, I'm still in love with the island.  The only things I miss are my Mama, Travis and Lawton Howard."
