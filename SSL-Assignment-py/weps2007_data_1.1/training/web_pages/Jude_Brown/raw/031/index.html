Jude Brown - Consultant


With over 10 years of experience, Jude is well acquainted with the skills necessary to provide efficient, courteous and professional service to Summit Search Group and its clients. Jude has developed a thorough understanding of sales, planning, education and international governance as a result of working in Canada, the United Kingdom and France. His time brokering agreements between various sectors in both the public and private domains has provided him with the expertise necessary to successfully recruit and place Accounting and Finance Professionals with the utmost level of professionalism.

As a firm believer in solution based systems, Jude is adept at matching specific candidate qualifications to the individual needs of employers in Calgary's growing accounting and finance sector. Jude will ensure that all potential candidates are appropriately screened, interviewed and referenced prior to being introduced to potential employers. A graduate of Simon Fraser University, Jude is currently pursuing designations in both accounting and finance.
