Leadership Research and Evaluation Programme

The NHS Leadership Centre has commissioned a series of Research into Leadership projects to support the development of leadership capability, capacity and the modernisation of the NHS. It takes the findings of important leadership and related research projects and looks at how these can be adapted and developed to influence leadership and related practice in the NHS.

The centre has also developed a programme of work to evaluate the relevance and effectiveness of its leadership development initiatives and activities.

To browse through the documents available, please use the index of documents to the left.
