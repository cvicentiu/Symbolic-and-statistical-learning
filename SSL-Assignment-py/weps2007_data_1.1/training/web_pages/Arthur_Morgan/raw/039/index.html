Tim O'Brien/Darrell Scott: Arthur Morgan School Benefit Concert

8 May 2005

On May 8th in Asheville, NC, outstanding musicians Tim O’Brien and Darrell Scott will perform a concert to benefit the Arthur Morgan School, located in Celo, NC.

Tim is one of the most dynamic performers and writers on the contemporary Bluegrass scene, well known for his pitch-perfect voice and amazing talent with multiple instruments. Darrell, one of the most successful songwriters on the country and bluegrass scenes, is also a singer and master of a number of instruments. In January 2005, his Theatre of the Unheard won the 4th Annual Independent Music Award for Album of the Year.

Tim O’Brien and Darrell Scott are combining their talents to support this unique learning opportunity right here in Western North Carolina.

They will be performing May 8th, at 7pm at the Grey Eagle Music Hall, 185 Clingman Ave. Asheville, NC

Tickets cost $20 and are available in advance at Arthur Morgan School and Grey Eagle Music Hall or can be purchased at the door

For concert/ticket information please contact Dudley Wilson at 252-2212 or the Arthur Morgan School at 675-4262.
