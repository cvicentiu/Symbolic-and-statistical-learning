The Center for Inspired Education is a private, non-profit educational organization whose mission is to inspire the growth of all children—mentally, emotionally, physically, and spiritually.  Our services include programs for students and teachers, academic coaching, advocacy for students with disabilities, and one-on-one tutoring. We are committed to maximizing the potential of each child regardless of any physical, cognitive, emotional, or learning disability, and we dedicate 10% of our profits to national and global relief organizations serving children in need.
