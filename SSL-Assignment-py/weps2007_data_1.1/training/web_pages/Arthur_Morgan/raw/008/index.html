Policies

Check Out 11 AM / Check In 3 PM.

Breakfast: Rates include a great breakfast.

Credit Cards accepted: Visa, MasterCard, Discover, American Express. Also checks and cash are accepted.

Deposit policy: 50% of the pretax cost of one night's stay guarantees the room. Your deposit, less $10 fee, is refundable if you must cancel or change your reservation more than two weeks before the date of your arrival. If you cancel or change your reservation less than 48 hours before checkin, the entire amount will become due unless the room is booked by someone else.

Weekend stay: A two-night stay is required; this policy is suspended on Monday before the specified weekend if rooms are available.

Inquire about: Discount for single occupancy; Additional cost for additional person in room; Government/corporate rate.

Room choice: Every effort will be made to place you in your chosen room; however, in some circumstances you may be given a comparable room, without prior approval.

Tax: Ohio sales tax of 7% and Greene County bed tax of 3%, for a total of 10% tax on the total, is added when balance is paid.

No smoking anywhere in Arthur Morgan House, but you are welcome to smoke on the screened porch.

Pets are permitted only in special circumstances, and usually as day visitors only.

TV: There are no televisions but there is a small collection of DVDs that you can watch on your laptop.

Wireless internet is accessible throughout the house. If you would like to check your e-mail or browse the web you can use Susanne’s laptop.
