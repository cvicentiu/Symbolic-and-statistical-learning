Antioch University

 The papers of Arthur E. Morgan, which Antiochiana began accumulating in 1963 while Morgan was still alive, occupy 235 linear feet of stack space in cartons with an additional 6 linear feet of oversize documents.  44 rolls of microfilm represent 41 linear feet of Tennessee Valley Authority (TVA) papers transferred to the National Archives' Southeast Region in 1981.  A pamphlet collection from Morgan’s Community Service, Inc. office and part of his personal library are shelved with the manuscript collection, occupying 34 and 85 linear feet, respectively.  For further collections information, contact Antiochiana at 937-769-1237.
