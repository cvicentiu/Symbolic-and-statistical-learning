Arthur Morgan Students at Highlander

February 27-28, 2006

On February 27th, 2006, a group of students and teachers from the Arthur Morgan School stopped at Highlander on a field trip. The next day, they spent part of the morning helping members of our staff rake and clean up along the driveway leading to our office. We thank them all for their help.
