Arthur Morgan School Open House

19 Feb 2003

The Arthur Morgan School is hosting an Open House Wednesday, February 19, 10:30-12:30. Students will be presenting their work to the community-at-large. Take this opportunity to get a close look at a one-of-a-kind junior high school, http://www.arthurmorganschool.org. Call 675-4262 for directions.
