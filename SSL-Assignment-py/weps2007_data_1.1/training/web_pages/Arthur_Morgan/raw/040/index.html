  Arthur Morgan   Contributor 
  Henry98@btopenworld.com 
Arthur Morgan is an author from the UK. He was brought up during the early 60s in a poor town in Wales and finally managed to see his work in print, on the stage and on the net.

He's delighted to discover sites like this exist and disappointed - though not surprised -- that few are based in the UK. He's written for academics and porn maestros alike and is just about to lose his present job editing two online business magazines.

Although he has written for UK women's magazines and studied feminism in university as a mature student, he is heartily sick of the pap such rags trundle out to ensnare the unsuspecting -- mostly women.

He sounds angry but he's not -- just pragmatic and apathetic.
