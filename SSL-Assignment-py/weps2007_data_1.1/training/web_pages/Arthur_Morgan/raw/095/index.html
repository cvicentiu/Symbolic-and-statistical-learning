In Something So Right
Archer, Tucker and T'Pol consummate their relationship.		 In Something So Right
reliving her relationship with Merlin for the timetrack's sake as much as her own, Nimue learns that she is actually Merlin's soul ever since spell worked by Nimue at Merlin's original death, and that the newhistory Merlin contains what was originally Nimue's soul.
In Dirty Girls
a preacher named Caleb reveals himself to Buffy as the chief of the First Evil's minions.		 In Dirty Girls
Emperor Nero reveals himself to Pudentiana as the chief of Gwyn ap Nud's minions.
In Judgment
Archer is put on trial for his life in the Klingon system, with the Klingon Kollos as his defense advocate. In the end Reed through T'Pol's diplomatic contacts must rescue Archer from a prison planet.		 In Judgment
Arthur is put on trial for his life in the Gaelic system, with the Gael Cador as his defense advocate. In the end Tristram through Lancelot's diplomatic contacts must rescue Arthur from a prison planet.
In cartoons
Arthur's trial in Gaelic space, at the beginning of his career rather than at the end (see Undiscovered Country above), is seen by Nimue and Merlin as evidence that the enemy in the temporal cold war is using the same principles of resonance as they but abusing them with the purpose of further disrupting the timeline.
In Horizon
Mayweather visits his family on the cargo ship where he grew up, when he learns that his father has passed away. His Brother is resentful of Starfleet and of Mayweather's Enterprise service, but Mayweather's experience saves the family business from alien pirates.		 In Horizon
Gawaine visits his family on the cargo ship where he grew up, when he learns that his father is the Gaelic officer discredited by Arthur, Morgan is Gawaine's maternal aunt, and his family is resentful of Arthur and of Gawaine's service with him, even after Gawaine's experience saves the family business from pirates.
In cartoons
Lancelot's growing respect for Arthur, and resulting jealousy on the part of the queen, begin to come between him and Guenevere.
In Cogenitor
while Archer is off the ship Tucker flouts a first-contact people's customs; in the end the person he was trying to help commits suicide.		 In cartoons
Tucker's blunder drives a wedge into his relationship with Archer and T'Pol.		 In Cogenitor
while Arthur is off the ship Guenevere flouts a first-contact people's customs; in the end the person she was trying to help commits suicide. The incident drives a wedge between her and both Arthur and Lancelot.
In Home
when Angel's evil law firm enemies concede him all their resources in exchange for his "defeating world peace" (destroying the powerful mind-controlling proto-demon Jasmine), he uses their resources to undo his son's unhappy history and insert him into a normal, happy family's life.		 In Home
when Genius' evil law firm enemies concede him all their resources in exchange for his "defeating world peace" (destroying the powerful mind-controlling titan Lotus), he uses their resources to undo his son's unhappy history and insert him into a normal, happy family's life.
In Chosen
the First Evil's plan to ener the human world is foiled when Buffy kills Caleb, Buffy has Willow imbue every potential Vampire Slayer in the world with full Slayer powers, Spike sacrifices himself and Sunnydale falls into a pit. BUFFY THE VAMPIRE SLAYER ends.		 In Chosen
Gwyn ap Nud's plan to ener the human universe is foiled when Pudentiana kills Nero, Pudentiana has Genuissa imbue every potential Fairie Bane in the world with full Bane powers, Nails sacrifices himself and Rome burns. SAINT PUDENTIANA THE FAIRY BANE ends.		 The Emperor Nero had Rome burned because he wished to redesign the city layout. As it burned he played his lyre.
In The Expanse
Earth is attacked by an anonymous enemy and Tucker's sister is killed. Silik's mysterious benefactor informs Archer the attack is the result of a faction breach within his people; the attackers, from Xindi space, are retaliating for future human incursions and are building a superweapon. Meanwhile the Klingon Duras, dishonored by the events leading up to Archer's trial and then by his escape, pursues vengeance against Archer. T'Pol defies her superiors in order to remain on the Enterprise on the new mission to Xindi space, three months' journey away with, unbeknownst to Enterprise, Duras' ship behind them all the way.		 In The Expanse
Arthur's home system is attacked by an anonymous enemy and Guenevere's twin sister is killed. Morgan's mysterious benefactor informs Arthur the attack is the result of a faction breach within his people; the attackers, from Indian space, are retaliating for future British incursions and are building a superweapon. Meanwhile the Gael Lot, dishonored by the events leading up to Arthur's trial and then by his escape, pursues vengeance against Arthur. Lancelot defies his superiors in order to remain on the Enterprise on the new mission to Hindu space, three months' journey away with, unbeknownst to Excalibur, Lot's ship behind them all the way.
In cartoons
Buffy reacts to release from the responsibility of being the only chosen Vampire Slayer by traveling with the Doctor; returning to 2003 the moment after she left and going on to form a new Watchers' Council with Giles to replace the one destroyed by the First to render guidance to the world's new Slayers.
In Mixed Blessing
Arthur gives tacit approval to Lancelot to resume his affair with Guenevere, if it will help her get over her sister's death.
One of Angel's first trials as new CEO of the L.A. branch of Wolfram & Hart is the sudden resurrection of Spike, through an amulet Angel had passed on to Buffy given him by the senior partners. One of Genius' first trials as new CEO of the Ostia branch of Aireslupi Suloque is the sudden resurrection of Nails, through an amulet Genius had passed on to Pudentiana given him by the senior partners.
