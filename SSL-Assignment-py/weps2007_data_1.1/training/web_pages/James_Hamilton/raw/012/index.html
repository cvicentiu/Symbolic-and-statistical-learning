JAMES HAMILTON
Magical Entertainment



 

Bay Area magician and magical entertainer, JAMES HAMILTON, is in great demand as one of the West Coast's finest magicians. A native of the Hollywood area, he has performed on television, in movies and at private parties for many Hollywood celebrities.  Now residing in San Francisco, he has been acclaimed by his peers as the Best Magician in Northern California.  He is equally adept at intimate gatherings, as well as large stage productions.

In addition to being a consumate performer, Mr. Hamilton has achieved an international reputation as a Magical Historian and Lecturer. He is the Magic Consultant for the San Francisco Performing Arts Library & Museum and is a member of the world-famous Magic Castle in Hollywood, as well as the prestigious Magic Circle of London.  He is recognized as the world authority on the legendary magical Herrmann family.

Mr. Hamilton's versatility, extraordinary magic skills, and charming personality make him the perfect entertainment choice for any event. He adapts his performance to suit each occasion. From elegant black-tie affairs to children's parties to large-scale variety shows, James Hamilton guarantees to enchant and delight audiences of all ages with his captivating presentation and his amazing magic.
