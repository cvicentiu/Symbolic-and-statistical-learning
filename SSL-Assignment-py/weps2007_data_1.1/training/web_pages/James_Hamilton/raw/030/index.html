JAMES HAMILTON
Principal
Email James

Mr. Hamilton has extensive background in business development, marketing and corporate development. His business career began as part of TicketMaster's New Market Development Team which had responsibility for establishing new business opportunities in over 20 different markets across North America.

After TicketMaster, Mr. Hamilton went on to a business development position with The Active Network.  In this role Mr. Hamilton was responsible for business development and marketing transactions with a wide variety of companies. His responsibilities included managing all stages of deal development from proposals and requirements definition through financing, development, testing and implementation and launch. Subsequently, Mr. Hamilton performed corporate financial analysis with G2 Capital, a La Jolla based hedge fund, prior to joining Shoreline.

Mr. Hamilton has a BA degree in both in Sociology and Spanish Literature from San Diego State University.  He studied Spanish Literature and art history at the Universidad Del Pais Vasco in Spain as well as at the Cuernavaca Language Institute in Mexico. He went on to earn an M.B.A at the Graduate School of Business at San Diego State University.
