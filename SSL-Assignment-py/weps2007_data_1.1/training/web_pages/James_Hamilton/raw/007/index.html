Based out of Philadelphia, James Hamilton Photography works with musicians, dancers and artists to provide imagery that depicts their style and energy. Equipped with a full array of professional digital camera equipment and accessories I am able to capture the moment with precision and clarity.

As a band or performer your image can make or break you. How you display yourself means almost as much as how you sound or perform. Blurry pictures from a friendâ€™s point and shoot do not do justice to you and the time you have put into your talent.

I am available for Live Performance Photography of any kind as well as location shoots. Whether it is a dance performance, an art exhibit or your band headlining the local venue, I have the experience, talent and desire to get the shot!

Images are provided in a "web ready" format as well as professional print options. All editing and processing are done in house with the latest tools including multiple safety backups.
