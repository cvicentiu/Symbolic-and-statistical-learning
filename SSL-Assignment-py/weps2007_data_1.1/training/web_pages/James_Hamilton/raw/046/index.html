As a professional full-time hypnotherapist in Sutton James Hamilton DHyp MIAH helps people from all walks of life with every kind of emotional, nervous or psychological disorders.

From his office at the Chiltern Health Centre he has assisted individuals with stopping smoking, losing weight, eating disorders, insomnia, phobias, fear, anxiety, depression, blushing and much more.

James is especially specialised in the treatment of confidence issues, shyness, depression, anxiety, fears and phobias and panic attacks, as well as many other symptoms and conditions. He is an expert in smoking cessation (in a single session) after almost a decade working to help smokers quit.
