James Hamilton & Co. (Lurgan) Ltd. was started in the 1920's by James Hamilton, selling wrapping papers and twine mostly to the local linen industry which was widespread in the Lurgan and Portadown area.

With the linen industry in decline in the 1960's, the company went into commercial printing and general food packaging.

A natural progression from the printing was to add on office stationery supplies. Craigavon Office Supplies in Lurgan began trading from the High Street in 1973.

Sister company JH Office Supplies in Ballymoney came about in the 1980's, both complimenting the printing very well.
Founder James Hamilton

In 1982 Hy-pro (formerly Industrial Disposable Wipes) was set up to supply paper wipes, cleaning, hygiene and safety work wear products.

Finally in 1991 JH Bearings was formed.

Original Factory at King Street, Lurgan

The Company is still privately owned by the Hamilton family, now in the 3rd generation.


In 2002 all companies except for JH Office Supplies (located in Ballymoney) moved into one large complex in Lurgan. This brings together the many resources shared throughout the group, increasing efficiency tremedously. Craigavon Office Supplies also have their original retail shop located in the town centre.
