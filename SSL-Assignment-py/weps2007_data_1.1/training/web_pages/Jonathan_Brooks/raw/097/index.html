Although Jonathan spends a lot of his time travelling he is always available to schedule training courses and a lot of fun diving. Jonathan runs an annual trip to the Andria Doria every summer plus in the winter months he runs trips to North Florida for those interested in Cave Diving and Technical diver training programs. Jonathan can be reached either by e-mail or telephone anytime to answer any questions you might have regarding training and diving.
