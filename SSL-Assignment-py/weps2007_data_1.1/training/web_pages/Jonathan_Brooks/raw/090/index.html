About Mr Brooks

 

Jonathan Brooks trained at Cambridge University and St Thomas’ Hospital London, qualifying MB BChir 1973. His specialist (Board) qualifications include:

  • FRCOG  (Obstetrics and Gynaecology)

  • FRCS    (General Surgery with Urology)

  • DCH      (Child Health)

After 27 years in the National Health Service, Mr Brooks established in full-time Independent practice in London where he has admitting rights to:

  • The Portland Hospital

  • The London Clinic

  • The Wellington Hospital

  • The Princess Grace Hospital

  • St Luke’s Hospital

Mr Brooks is now in his 50’s and is married with four children. He lives in central London, close to the Portland Hospital and London Clinic.
