Jonathan Brooks1 (M)
Pop-up Pedigree

     Jonathan Brooks was born on 25 June 1759 at Grafton, MA.1,2 He was the son of Simon Brooks and Rachel Drury. Jonathan Brooks died on 22 July 1759 at Grafton, MA.1,3
     Both his birth and death are listed twice in the published vital records: once as Jonathan and once as Jonah. It seems highly unlikely that twins should survive birth and four weeks of infancy, only to die independently the same day. The two entries therefore must refer to one baby. The confusion undoubtedly stems from the common use of 'Jona' as an abbreviation for Jonathan.
