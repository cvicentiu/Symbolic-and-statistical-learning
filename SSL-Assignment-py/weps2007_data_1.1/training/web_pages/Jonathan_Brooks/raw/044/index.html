About me
I am a skilled producer with experience in factual and light entertainment, comedy and observational documentary both in studio and on location.

Key skills include scriptwriting, setting up shoots, interviewing, development, researching, edit producing and contributor/contestant finding. My work has ranged from posts on a hidden camera comedy show to working on a series of historical documentaries.

My technical skills are numerous and varied, I am an accomplished self-shooter (PD150/170170, DSR-500 & Z1) with a background in animation, graphics and editing (have three years experience as a After Effects/Final Cut Pro Editor). I also have extensive experience location lighting and sound recording, (see Technical Skills).

Alongside my work in television I have been lucky to have achieved notable successes as a visual artist, exhibiting in galleries and film festivals across Europe. I have taken part in various panel discussions with other professional artists and given seminars and lectures on my work.
