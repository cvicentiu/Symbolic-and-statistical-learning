jonathan brooks

reading collage

What reading means to me: “It isn’t one of my favorite things to do, but when I am reading something and it really seems like an interesting subject I tend to enjoy it. When I am forced to read some thing I do it”. -I am not one to give up!!

 
 

*Background*

When I was in elementary reading was the best I loved reading and being read to it was like my most favorite time of day.

 

***

 

I love the picture books, they maid it so easy to understand what was going on, this is when my reading was at the right level (haha).

 

“I don’t really remember when reading got so hard for me but I know it did at some point. I truly believe that I started to get bad at reading when I started to not take school seriously”. - I was a good student until a certain grade where it was all about popularity.

 
 

*Reading HaHaHa*

 
 

“I don’t have a problem with reading I just can’t do it as good as most”-I will never give up on what I am reading even though I am not the best this is funny to me, because I would think that this would help me to become a better reader. Maybe I am just stuck.

 

Don’t get me wrong I don’t think that reading or just plan old learning is a joke, there are just certain things about reading that are hard to do or understand.

 

*What so bad about it*

 
 

“The worst thing about reading is getting an assignment from a teacher and he or she wants a certain amount of pages read in certain amount of days. The problem is sometimes I can’t fill the page requirement, because I get really bored with what I am reading”. –I can never read as long as I want to or plan for them to be, because something always comes up and it stops me from reading. Reading and making sense of what I read really Is some thing that is a problem with me.

 
 

*Belief*

 
 

“Although my reading isn’t the best I know that I can improve my reading through hard work and dedication, because anything is possible with enough time”. –I know that someday I will be at the level that I need to be, if I work hard enough.

 
 

*My plans*

 
 

“I plan on doing my best to catch up in class and passing through hard work”. -I fell behind but I will do my best to keep up in class. I truly don’t think that catching up will be a problem.

 
 

*Keeping it real*

 
 

Jennie (a friend)- “Writing helps me a lot with my emotions, it helps me get things of my chest. I always write in my journal every night”, then I trade journals with my best friend and we read each other jornals. - I agree when I get to do my free writing it kind of fun. shhhh!!! Don’t tell anybody! But I don’t want to read anybody else’s writing in their journal…well not all the time.

 

Cam (a friend)- “The only time I like you read is when I am reading graffiti art on walls, and when I am writing or drawing but only legal stuff”. –I like reading graffiti, because you are not only reading but you are looking at art.

 

Sal (a friend)- “I like to read song lyrics, because it helps me with my own lyrics. I love to sit down listen to a beat and just write what ever comes to mind”. - I don’t know anything about song lyrics, but I do know they have to be written and read off the page when you are recording.

 

*Reading assignments*

 
 

“Reading is fun but the problem with reading in class is that it is in class; there is something about reading for homework that just make it hard. When I am reading for class there are rules to follow that is when reading becomes a job”. – I like to call this reading with a leash.

 
 

*Are you trying to trick me*

 
 

Luisa ( 6 years old)- “teachers try to trick you when they give you home work sometimes. They just want you to spend your time doing homework and they don’t even check it. I think they are on teams with my mom and dad”- sometimes a student really needs the teacher to check their work so they can know what to work on. They also need compliments sometimes. Anyone can lie and say they read something just by talking to others you can learn about a book.

 
 

*Feelings*

 
 

“It’s sad when someone graduates from high school but don’t really feel like they learned anything from their high school, it like those four years were a waste of time”. –This happens to a lot of students including me. I feel like my high school experience has brought me nowhere now I have to work so much harder just to keep up. I still wont give up though.

 
 

*The truth*

 
 

“Everyone reads and writes weather they like it or not, so people should try to do there best to become better reader and writers; it only makes sense”. -Just about everyone on plant earth is asked to write or read something in there life so why not try to be the best you can be at both, it wont hurt you.

 

*What to do*

 

Everybody knows that practice makes perfect so I’ll continue to work hard (practice) then it will pay off (perfect)
