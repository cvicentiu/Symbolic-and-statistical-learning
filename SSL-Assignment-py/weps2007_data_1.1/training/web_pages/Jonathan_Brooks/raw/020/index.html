    Jonathan Brooks has a degree from the University of Miami in advertising, a fine arts co-major in photography, and a minor in marketing. His studies in graphic design and architecture, and extensive backgrounds in the fashion industry and music industry have helped to influence his work. The story and images detailing the renovation of his condo appeared nationally in the Fall 2000 issue of Venus magazine. He has numerous photography exhibitions to his credit and is currently working on his next. Other samples of his works and writings have appeared in various local and national media.
