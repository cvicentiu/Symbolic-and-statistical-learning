About Us

 

Jonathan has been a full time professional wedding and portrait photographer for more than twenty five years, giving a vast experience and knowledge to his photography.

"Running a small family business gives us time to spend with you and gives his work a personal touch, often lacking in larger studio organizations."

Jonathan has won many International Awards including some of the most prestigious in the world. His creative, artistic flair and masterful techniques combine to give you a superior photographic coverage. Amongst the many honours received Jonathan is most proud of his "Masters" degree from the Professional Photographers of America. He was only the fourth person in the U.K.to obtain this level of qualification at that time.

Now teaching his art to other photographers, Jonathan has lectured extensively for all the major photographic organizations in England, Scotland, and Ireland. As part of his "Masters" Degree he undertook a lecture tour of California U.S.A.

The Studio:

 
  Situated near the centre of Aldridge, our Edwardian studio offers a warm family atmosphere in which you will feel relaxed and comfortable whilst having your photographs created. Recently refurbished to give a modern, light and airy feel, our studio is one of the best kept secrets in the region.
