Jonathan Brooks

Before PADI's Tech Wreck, there was TDI. Before TDI, there was IANTD. Before IANTD, there were a few cutting edge individuals writing the book that all the others would eventually use. We know, because we were there. In advanced and technical diving, one thing counts more than your training program, your education, your training dives and certainly the agency name on your c-card - experience. If you missed the experiences of the first decade of technical diving, your next best option is to take advantage of the instructors that were there.

Jonathan Brooks, TDI/SDI/ERDI IT #CI24 (#1540 U.S) was the first recipient of the "TDI Outstanding Training Award" in 2000.  Additionally, Jonathan is a Senior Instructor Trainer for TDI, SDI and worked as the Midwest Regional Sales Manager for International Training until June 2003.

Jonathan has been featured on Sport Diver Television as a Technical Advisor on three Great Lakes episodes of Undersea Explorer. He has explored underwater caves, sunken shipwrecks and deep walls throughout the Great Lakes and the world.

Jonathan is also in charge of Training and founder of Over The Edge Educational Expeditions, Inc. along with being one of our boat captains. With over ten years of experience in the SCUBA industry Jonathan is a very thorough and well respected trainer. He is available for Instruction at all levels
