Objectives and Agenda

 

 

DAY 1 - Monday 25th  October 

Objectives for Day 1 are for participants to:

·         Increase their knowledge of UNDP’s work with business at both the global and regional level.

·         Understand the perspectives of how Microsoft are trying to meet their CSR/ Sustainable Development goals

·         Actively contribute during the day through sharing case study experiences and through Q&A

DAY 2 – Tuesday 26th October

Objectives for Day 2 are for participants to:

·         Discuss visions for UNDP and the private sector, with an external speaker and with the Regional Director,

·         Highlight key challenges to developing business partnerships, clarify further information/ support needed by C.O.

·         Share C.O. strategic thinking, with all participants starting the process of developing a private sector strategy

DAY 3 – Wednesday 27th October

Objectives for Day 3 are for participants to: 

·         Present prioritized actions to develop a more vibrant C.O. private sector network throughout RBEC

·         Discuss and debate the different views

·         Come to a firm, documented, agreement on the way forward for RBEC

·         Clarify roles of (i) C.O.s, (ii) The regional bureau and (iii) BRSP in building the RBEC network
