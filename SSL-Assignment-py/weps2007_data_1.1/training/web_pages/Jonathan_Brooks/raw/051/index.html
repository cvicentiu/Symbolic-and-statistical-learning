Brooks

 Updated December 1, 2005
   

This is a database compiled mostly from FHL microfiche of the Albany City Directory of New York State. It currently covers the years 1813-1860. There is no directory for 1836. The two gentleman in bold are brothers John and David Brooks. John Brooks is my ancestor who moved to Vermont by 1860. It is possible that the Dinah listed in 1816 and 1817 is their mother (who is named as Diana on John Brooks death registration). Their father's name was also John Brooks. John Brooks was born in 1815 and his brother David was born about 1812.
