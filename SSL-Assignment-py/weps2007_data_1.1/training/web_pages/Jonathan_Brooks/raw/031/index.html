Children of Jonathan Brooks and Rebecca:
   i.    Elizabeth bp. 4 June 1727.
   ii.   Ann, bp. 22 Jan. 1737.
   iii.  Maria, bp. 4 Feb. 1739.
   iv.   Catarina, bp. 6 Aug. 1749.

Jonathan Brooks was taxed in Rombout from 1758-72 and
his estate from 1773-79. Jonathan Brooks Jr. was taxed
from 1761-67. The will of Jonathan Brooks of Rombout was
written 30 Dec. 1772 and proved 16 April 1773. He
mentioned his wife Elizabeth, his dau. Elizabeth and
his son Jonathan. William Brooks was a witness to the
will. [WN-YHS VIII:108]. Jonathan Brooks Jr. m.
Elizabeth Griffen 17 July 1763. [RPCh].

Children:
   i.    Elizabeth.
   ii.   Jonathan, b. before 1741 and was taxed in
         Rombout from 1761-1767; m. Mary, dau. of
         Gerardus and Jane Vermilia. [DCW B:138].
         She d. 15 Nov. 1811, age 46-1-21 and her
         death was mentioned in the Oswego MM.
         Jonathan Brooks was a witness to the will of
         George Jewell of Fishkill dated 24 May 1810.
         [DCW C:479]. He was also an executor to the
         will of Garardus Vermilya of Fishkill dated
         25th day 2nd month 1808. [DCW C:258].
         Jonathan Brooks was a witness to the will of
         Richard Jackson of Freedom dated 9 April
         1824. [DCW G:364]. A Jonathan Brooks d. 3
         April 1829, age 91. A Vermilyea Brooks d.
         16 Dec. 1857 a. 53y Friend's Gnd Arthursburg.
         [OGDC 144].

Jonathan Brooks m. Catherine, dau. of Fletcher
Matthews. She b. ca. 1750. [RECORD 1873:55]

A Jonathan Brooks of Woodbridge, NJ m. as her 2nd
Hannah, b. 9 Dec. 1725, dau. of Jeremiah and Katherine
(Weeks) Bloomfield. He was administer of his brother-
in-law Jeremiah Bloomfield's estate in 1757. [RECORD
1937:61].

Maria M. Brooks and D. Brooks and Daniel N. Brooks
were witnesses to the will of Leonard Pells of
Poughkeepsie dated 9 Aug. 1819. [DCW G:298]. Maria M.
and David Brooks were witneses to mortgage between
Joseph Lockwood of Beekman and Thomas Tillotson of
Rhinebeck 1 May 1811. [M 17:412].
