Jonathan Brooks
Partner
Osborne Clarke
2 Temple Back East
Temple Quay
Bristol
BS1 6EG
UK
t +44 (0)117 917 3462

Jonathan's expertise lies in contentious and non-contentious construction and engineering law. He has wide experience of acting for employers, contractors, sub-contractors and consultants, together with public corporations and local authorities. He handles cases in the High Court and County Court and deals with adjudications and arbitrations (domestic and international).

Recent matters that Jonathan has acted on include a successful adjudication claim entitling the client to terminate a contract for poor performance. Jonathan is presently acting for a national mining company on a number of earthwork arbitrations and for a national utility company on a number of projects. His practice spans national and international markets, and includes work on a claim against a dam construction company in Indonesia, and advising a client on a claim related to the construction of a steel rolling plant in Saudi Arabia.

Jonathan qualified in 1993 and was working head of the northern contentious construction practice for Nabarro Nathanson before he joined Osborne Clarke in May 2000 as a partner in the litigation department construction practice. He is a member of South Yorkshire Focus (where he was previously an administrator), the Society of Construction Law and the Chartered Institute of Arbitrators. Jonathan is also a member and South West Regional Co-ordinator of the Adjudication Society and a member of TeSCA.
