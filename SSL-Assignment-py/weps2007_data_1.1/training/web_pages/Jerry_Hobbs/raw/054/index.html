Rap Sheet On Jerry Hobbs

E-mail this story | Print this story
Sign Up for Breaking News Alerts
Jerry Hobbs
Jerry Hobbs was charged Tuesday with two counts of first-degree murder in connection with the deaths of Laura Hobbs, 8, and Krystal Tobias, 9, who were found slain in a nature preserve field Monday morning.

Hobbs' record shows 10 previous arrests. The following information is from the Texas Department of Public Safety Web site.

  • Arrested July 27, 1990 for aggravated assault with a weapon. Convicted Aug. 24, 1990.

  • Arrested July 3, 1994 for driving while license suspended, misdemeanor. Convicted Aug. 22, 1994.

  • Arrested July 30 1994 for possession of marijuana less than 2 oz. Convicted Sept. 12, 1994.

  • Arrested Dec. 11, 1995 for failing to identify fugitive from justice, misdemeanor. Convicted Feb. 12, 1996.

  • Arrested Jan. 16, 1997 for assault causing bodily injury, misdemeanor. Convicted March 18, 1997.

  • Arrested Aug. 10, 1997 for driving while license suspended, misdemeanor. Convicted Oct. 9, 1997.

  • Arrested Sept. 13, 1997 for resisting arrest, misdemeanor. Convicted Nov. 17 1997.

  • Arrested Sept. 27 1997 for evading arrest, misdemeanor. Convicted Dec. 1, 1997.

  • Arrested Sept. 15, 1998 for possession of marijuana, less than 2 oz., misdemeanor. Convicted Nov. 9, 1998.

  • Arrested Aug. 4 2001 for aggravated assault, causes severe bodily injury, second-degree felony. Convicted Dec. 5, 2002.

Hobbs' probation was revoked Sept. 4, 2003.

He was incarcerated at the Texas Department of Criminal Justice in Middleton for the Aug 4, 2001 offense. He started his incarceration on Oct. 17, 2003. Hobbs was released April 12, 2005.

He was taken into Zion, Ill., police costody Monday, where he remains.
