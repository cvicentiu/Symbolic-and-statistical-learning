Jerry Hobbs's Artist Portfolio
Welcome to Jerry Hobbs's Portfolio. Browse Jerry Hobbs's body of work: The heavily wooded hills of Van Zandt county in East Texas raised a boy named Jerry Hobbs. He loved nature and especially took notice of trees. And seeings how his ancestors had been in this area since 1850 there was plenty of know-how about trees to pass along. Jerry also did his own discovering as he grew up. In the years since, he and Brenda, his wife of 35 years, explored the woods and forests in many parts of Texas. Jerry is acquainted with about every kind of tree in the state.
