Missy's Gift
Like most families in North Carolina during the 1800’s, the McDeens were proud, hardworking and honest. They valued their privacy more than most, yet were quick to offer a helping hand whenever needed. That’s one of the reasons Doc Crawford sent the horribly scarred, young Cherokee woman out to their place, certain they would take her in without question. Young Hammond suspects the woman is terrified, but it’s years later before he learns her secret. What she tells him then not only threatens to destroy her, but also the entire McDeen family. This emotion-filled, action-packed historic novel takes place in the southern Smoky Mountains of western North Carolina. It describes the impact a young Indian woman has on this rural family as they go through life learning to rely on each other’s strengths. The question is, will these strengths be enough to keep them alive when they come face to face with the ultimate challenge?

The Lovebug Device
Orphaned at three, Doc was hardworking, brilliant and had a delightful sense of humor. Life wasn’t always easy, but that never slowed him down. To know Doc was to love him. Everyone, that is, except his wife Gloria. She was having far too much fun planning his murder to think about love. It wasn’t until a stranger follows him to work one morning that Doc discovers he has become an unwilling participant in a deadly game of winner take all. Using skills learned in grade school, along with his knowledge as a cellular biologist, he devises a perfect plan to come out on top. The one factor he hadn’t counted on, however, was that an old girlfriend would be called in to head the investigation. Will she find him innocent or guilty? This novel will hold your attention all the way to the end.
