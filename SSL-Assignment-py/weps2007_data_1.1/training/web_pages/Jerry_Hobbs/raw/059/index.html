in July 2002 I corresponded with Col. Hobbs, asking him about his Toyama Ryu connection. Here is his response; sorry for the loooong pause, but I couldn't find it until now:
Quote:
...Anyhow, regarding Toyama-Ryu: I was fortunate enough to have studied it while stationed in Panama in the mid-90s. My principal teacher was Stavros Costarangos, a student of Fumio Demura. I also trained with Demura Sensei whenever he visited Panama. ...
Demura sensei studied kendo under Nakamura sensei in grade school -- he dropped in on Nakamura sensei one day while I was visiting; what a surprise!
