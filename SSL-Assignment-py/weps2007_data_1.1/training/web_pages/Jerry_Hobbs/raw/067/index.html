Father admits to killing daughter and friend
5/11/2005 4:30 PM
By: The Associated Press

An Illinois prosecutor described it as "a slaughter of two little girls." And he said the father of one of those girls confessed that he stabbed them to death.

Wednesday, a judge in Waukegan, Illinois denied bond for Jerry Hobbs -- after prosecutors described the videotaped interview in which they said he admitted the killings.

They said Hobbs told investigators he was angry at his 8-year-old daughter, Laura, for taking money. Hobbs is said to have admitted that he tracked her and her friend, Krystal Tobias, in a wooded park on Sunday -- and then punched his daughter and killed both girls.

The bodies were found Monday in the park in Zion, Illinois. It was Hobbs who led police to the bodies -- saying he had come across them while looking for his missing daughter. But they said he eventually told them he killed both girls after Laura refused to leave the park, and that he then dragged their bodies into the woods.
