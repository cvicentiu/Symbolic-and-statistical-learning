ABOUT HANSHI ROY JERRY HOBBS

[back to history]

 Hanshi Hobbs is the highest-graded Seidokan Shorin-Ryu Karate Master in the Western world and, by virtue of that, the international community’s leading authority on Seidokan. He is a living encyclopedia of the Oriental Martial Arts, an all-rounder in this field; a complete martial artist. A most inspiring teacher acknowledged and commanding great respect and admiration all over the world. A learning encounter with Hanshi Hobbs is an experience of a life-time. Highly recommended.
