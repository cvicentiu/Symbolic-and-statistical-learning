The Lovebug Device 

 By Jerry Hobbs

Orphaned at three, Doc was hardworking, brilliant and blessed with a delightful sense of humor.  Life wasn’t always easy, but that never slowed him down.  To know Doc was to love him.  Everyone, that is, except his wife Gloria.  She was having far too much fun planning his murder to think about love.  It wasn’t until a stranger follows him to work one morning that Doc discovers he has become an unwilling participant in a deadly game of winner take all.  Using skills learned in grade school combined with his knowledge as a cellular biologist, he devises a perfect plan to come out on top.  The one factor he hadn’t counted on, however, was that an old girlfriend would be called in to head the investigation.  Will she find him innocent or guilty?  This novel will hold your attention to the very end.
