David J. McClosky

I'm a 3rd year doctoral student working with Eugene Charniak and Mark Johnson in the Brown Laboratory for Linguistic Information Processing (BLLIP). I'm interested in statistical natural language processing and artificial intelligence, specifically language modelling and parsing, speech recognition, and morphological rule induction. In linguistics, I'm interested in how morphology, phonology, and syntax interact.
