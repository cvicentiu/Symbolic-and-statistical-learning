Reading and Research Group

There are many people here in Edinburgh working on statistical machine translation. This group meets once a week to exchange ideas, report on own research, or review work in the field. The format is informal.

The meetings take place Tuesdays at 3pm in the seminar room at 2 Buccleuch Place. If you have any questions, please send me email.
