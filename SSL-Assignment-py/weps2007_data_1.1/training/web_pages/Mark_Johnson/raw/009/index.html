decorative painting

Mark Johnson Studio is a decorative painting and design consulting company that creates custom painted finishes and murals of unique and exceptionally rare quality. The portfolio of residential and commercial spaces seen here reflect the level of artistic excellence that is synonymous with Mark Johnson Studio.

Located in San Francisco, California, near the heart of the Design Center, Mark Johnson Studio is an elite team of accomplished artisans, artists, and trained decorative painters.

MJS provides everything from traditional faux finishes, such as wood grain and marble, glazing techniques, gold and silver leaf application, trompe l’oeil, murals and oil paintings, exotic faux leathers, crackle varnishes, subtle plaster “color washing”, and distinctive designs for unique projects.

Mark Johnson is the principal decorative painter and designer. After several years of refining his abilities as a fine art painter and faux finisher, Mark Johnson created MJS in 2000 and immediately established himself as one of the premier decorative painting artists in the San Francisco Bay Area. His custom created finishes and designs have won steadfast approval among distinguished clients and interior designers throughout Northern California, the United States, and abroad.
