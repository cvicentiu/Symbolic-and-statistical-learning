NOTES
I'm an Easy Rock Singer/Songwriter/Rhythm Guitarist/Bass Player/Producer with a lot to inspire. Growing up in the Western U.S., I was involved and toured with several well known bands, and some rock bands in which I myself created, over the past 20 odd years, on the west coast.


I seriously got into writing my own music after serving in the U.S. Army back in the mid 70's until 1980, I am a rocker at heart, full of compassion and devoted to my love for music.. My music and lyric's would and have before created excitement within the music industry and drawn in a true fan base that was sure to follow.


I'm the type of Singer/Songwriter that tries to help people remember what life is all about, and help people to feel that place in their hearts from memories gone by and reminding them that they are still alive.
