Mark Johnson’s Summary

I have approx. 15 years of HR experience in a range of positions, the last nine of which have been with large, Fortune-500 class corporations. I spent my early career in retail, ending with GM roles for very large retail operations with headcount responsibility exceeding 40 in 2 levels of management. I then spent 5+ years managing HR and Operations for a growing retail chain in California as their VP of Operations, which included retail and operations headcount exceeding 115 in 3 levels of management. Realizing that retail had little more to offer I returned to school to finish a degree combining Business Management with an HR/OD core. After graduating with distinction I spent a year + in S. Korea creating & delivering Sr. Management training products for some of Korea's largest corporations (Hyundai, Kia, LG, etc.). While we loved Korea, we returned to the US. I transitioned in to high-tech w/ Tektronix ( http://www.tektronix.com ) in a Staffing role. I grew that position as far as possible, which included management of up five direct headcount, numerous enterprise-level projects, and recruiting to the VP-level. I was recruited to The Standard (http://www.standard.com) in October of 2004. Presently I am a Sr. Staffing Consultant managing staffing, recruiting and projects for a 500-person field sales organization in addition to multiple enterprise projects, including our first ATS implementation/integration.

Next: Managing a Staffing team of at least 2 contributors, or potentially a lateral move into Comp.
