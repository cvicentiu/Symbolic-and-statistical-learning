Mark Johnson, J.D., Ph.D., is an expert in ERISA, pensions, health plans, 401(k) plans, fiduciary duties and labor union benefits. He managed $14 billion in pension assets for over 100,000 plan participants while serving as Managing Director, Benefits Compliance & Pensions for American Airlines. Dr. Johnson has been retained as an expert in many nationwide, class action fiduciary lawsuits.

Q: Please list your professional accreditations, degrees, licenses, and certificates granted:
A: Dr. Johnson holds the following degrees: - J.D., University of Houston, 1983 - Ph.D, Communication Studies, University of Massachusetts, Amherst, 1976 - M.A., Mass Communication, University of Massachusetts, Amherst, 1973 - B.A., History, University of Massachusetts, Amherst, 1971. He holds the following licenses: - licensed attorney in Texas (not practicing); - Board Certified Labor and Employment Law Specialist, Texas Board of Legal Specializtion. Prior to his corporate and legal careers, Dr. Johnson taught, researched and published as a fulltime university faculty member for five years. He continues to teach on a part-time basis, currently as a professional education instructor for the Texas Department of Insurance and as an Adjunct Professor of Business Communication at Tarrant County College.
