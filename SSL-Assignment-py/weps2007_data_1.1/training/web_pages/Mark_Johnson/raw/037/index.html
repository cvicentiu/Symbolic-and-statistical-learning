Transactions 

June 2, 1994: Drafted by the Chicago White Sox in the 1st round (26th pick) of the 1994 amateur draft. Player signed July 9, 1994.

December 3, 2002: Traded by the Chicago White Sox with Keith Foulke, Joe Valentine, and cash to the Oakland Athletics for players to be named later and Billy Koch. The Oakland Athletics sent Neal Cotts (December 16, 2002) and Daylon Holt (minors) (December 16, 2002) to the Chicago White Sox to complete the trade.

October 15, 2003: Granted Free Agency.

December 3, 2003: Signed as a Free Agent with the Milwaukee Brewers.

October 7, 2004: Granted Free Agency.

November 9, 2004: Signed as a Free Agent with the Milwaukee Brewers.

March 26, 2005: Traded by the Milwaukee Brewers to the Chicago Cubs for Travis Ezi (minors).

October 15, 2005: Granted Free Agency.
