Music Bio

OBJECTIVE: TO PASSIONATELY AND RHYTHMICALLY TOUCH THE WORLD.

Born on the Southern California coast in the small town of Santa Maria , Mark moved up and down the state from town to town searching for a niche. At sixteen when he first picked up a fender guitar and felt the rush, he was so possessed by its alluring power that he laid down a goof track on a small cassette player right then and there. Of course the appreciation by friends of what he had done was just blown off by Mark has fed his rhythmic pulse every since.

His teen years were filled with vocal experimentation and exploration in Northern California with music projects, two notable Fresno County favorites were local hero's ONYX and CHEATA , serving as a training ground for the future. His later years found him in southern California with a couple of side projects going when he found a rock trio called DEMARK doing the club circuit along Sunset Strip and in all the local southern California cities. The trio was at the time looking to recruit a lead singer and broaden and strengthen there repertoire. This they did. The new foursome known as FORTE was quickly embraced by all who heard them. During a term as house band for The Fantasia Club in L.A. County they shared the stage with numerous national acts Local heroes such as TUFF , XYZ and Roger Winfield. The Roxy, Whiskey, Gazaries, Marque, Tree House, Goodies, and countless other venue where home to the foursome.

Mark was also flattered to be featured on the Elizabeth Sabine Vocal Training series recordings along with numerous other outstanding vocalists. Elizabeth was very adamant about expressing her belief and I quote “Mark's an extraterrestrial who's here on this planet with us for a greater purpose.
