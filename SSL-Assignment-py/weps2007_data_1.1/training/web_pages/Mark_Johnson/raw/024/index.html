Lets say you were strolling down the road and met a 400 pound black bear carrying a high-powered rifle. What would you do?

 

It is highly likely that you wouldnt run. You never know; the bear may know how to use the rifle as well as carry it.

 

You would give the bear your undivided attention.

 

Once the bear left, you would tell everybody what happened.

 

And you would never forget the bear. Ever.

 

Those are the traits of effective communications.
