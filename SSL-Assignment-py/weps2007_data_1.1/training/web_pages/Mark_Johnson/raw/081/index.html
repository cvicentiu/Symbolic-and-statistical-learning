Mark Johnson



Mark Johnson is a dynamic and charismatic speaker, who has thrilled audiences worldwide with his unique messages of over twenty years spent as an officer in the Green Berets and working in nearly fifty nations on four continents. As one person put it, "Mark symbolizes what leadership is all about. His self-confidence and pride in being a leader shine through when he speaks. People listen because he delivers his message emphatically, excitedly, and with sincere fervor and genuine concern." His greatest skill is his innate ability to lead and supervise people. He has done this throughout his life to include combat and times of adversity.

During his illustrious Army career, Mark earned over twenty awards and decorations during his time in Special Forces. During the Gulf War, Johnson was decorated with the Bronze Star, among many other awards, for his actions as a leader in the Green Berets. Since his retirement he has been keeping busy speaking and training businesses and organizations throughout the world. He recently returned from three weeks speaking and providing leadership training to companies throughout the United Kingdom.

Mark has authored a book on leadership, entitled, "How To Get Anyone to Follow You Anywhere Green Beret Secrets to Leadership and Team Building!"


MOST REQUESTED PROGRAMS:

How To Get Anyone to Follow You Anywhere
(Mark's most popular keynote presentation)
This presentation dissects the essence of Leadership and is summarized into a simple formula that any leader can follow, and more importantly, understand, utilize, and put into everyday practice. It is called simply "C.E.X3" Each letter is embellished with success stories and unique experiences, gained in over twenty years as a Green Beret officer, and from overcoming adversity that is seemingly unimaginable! This presentation will inspire you to embrace the responsibilities of leadership and assist in building self-esteem in your people.

Green Beret Secrets of Team Building
There is simply nothing like this powerfully connecting presentation of team building and team leading! The audience will be gripped to their chairs as they hear of life and death situations encountered in remote and quite primitive areas of the world, as well as Green Beret combat operations during the Gulf War, Somalia, and other trouble spots. Learn how effective teams saved human lives, and how you, utilizing similar methods, can transform your team to greater responsibilities and greater rewards!

We Are Champions...Feel Good About It!
A dynamic presentation of patriotism. There is simply nothing like this powerfully delivered and moving tribute to the great strength of this nation and its people. This is an action packed and moving address which leaves the audience feeling not only good about themselves, but proud to be an important part of the bigger picture of making this nation the undeniable world leader. This keynote is an absolute 5-Star hit with business corporations, veterans groups, military organizations, and newly formed businesses. The audience is left inspired and invigorated.

Lessons in Leadership: Straight Talk from a Green Beret
Learn a detailed, easy-to-understand plan of action to elevate yourself from being a good leader to a great one. Learn valuable lessons on how to effectively lead your people through all adversity presented and with a greater commitment from all. Mark translates the leadership lessons he gained from being a Green Beret leader directly to how audience memebers can improve and excel as leaders in their daily lives.

Capture Your Energy ... Transform Your Life!
A presentation designed specifically for college/university students. Particularly popular with student athletes and student leaders. Learn to transform your current energy level into future $$$$ and a successful career. Focus on energy and strengths and relate that directly to your future. Each student will be inspired and motivated to be a contributor and a producer in the 21st century. Numerous practical examples are utilized to provide positive reinforcing of the challenges of being a successful leader!

Motivate from the Heart: Make the Impossible Possible
Learn valuable techniques to assist you to effectively understand what makes people go the extra distance. Learn how to utilize the proper motivation, at the right time, in the correct manner, to achieve remarkable results. Mark was sucessful in motivating people in his care to fearlessly excel despite enormous odds. He will reveal his key motivational tips that will leave the audience inspired to action.
