Fafinski Mark & Johnson (FMJ) combines the depth of a large corporate law firm with a personable, team approach that is designed to provide strategic, tailor made solutions for clients.

Decision makers from across the country and around the world seek the business and legal guidance of the FMJ team. From emerging businesses to Fortune 500 companies, FMJ has gained trust and respect surpassing the typical attorney/client relationship.

Fafinski Mark & Johnson was founded by three principals with over 50 years of legal experience in corporate law, commercial litigation and aviation law. The FMJ team continues the tradition of doing more than just building cases - at FMJ, we build relationships.
