Coin Collecting Supplies

Statehood Quarter Supplies
The hugely successful Statehood Quarter program continues through 2008. Wall-mountable, illustrated State Quarter Maps by Harris, State Quarter Folders licensed by the US Mint and Frosted Plastic Display Cases by Harris make great gifts for all Statehood Quarter Collectors.
Coin Albums
The Dansco Supreme coin album series offers the widest selection of coin albums available. From 18th century type coins to modern commemoratives, Dansco albums feature update pages, optional proof ports and anti-corrosion slipcases.

All coin albums featured here offer clear plastic slides for viewing both sides of each coin. At the high end, Intercept Shield albums feature a built-in, patented, corrosion protection system.

The Whitman Classic albums , the US Mint-licensed Heirloom albums and the Littleton coin albums each offer a convenient, attractive and inexpensive way to store and display your coins.
Coin Folders
The original blue Whitman Coin Folder, one of the most enduring icons of coin collecting is still popular today. Coin folders are often used as an affordable introduction to coin collecting. Harris Coin Folders feature acid-free materials and color artwork. Both folders start at $2.99 each.
Coin Holders
AirTite Coin Holders feature a black or white inner ring of inert polyethylene to protect and cushion each coin within a clear capsule. The rings are available in sizes to fit all US coins and many others.  Starting at 85 cents each, AirTite holders are an attractive and affordable approach to coin storage and display.

Kointain Coin Holders are inert, clear plastic shells that fit snugly around each coin. Little more than the size of the coins they hold, Kointains can fit into other holders and start at $24.95 for 100.  

Whitman and Intercept Shield offer snaplock plastic holders for individual coins.  The Whitman Snaplock 2x2s feature a frosted field around a clear center and start at 69 cents each. The Intercept Shield Snaplock 2x2s start at $3.95 for 3 and feature an anti-corrosion inner core for additional protection.

Although not recommended for long term storage, Coin Flips and Fold and Staple 2x2s are available in bulk and are easily the most affordable coin holders.

US Coin Starter Sets

Many of the dates in the most-collected US coin series can no longer be found in circulation. Therefore, these starter sets are designed as an affordable way to jump-start a coin collection. Each set offers different dates in good or better condition.
