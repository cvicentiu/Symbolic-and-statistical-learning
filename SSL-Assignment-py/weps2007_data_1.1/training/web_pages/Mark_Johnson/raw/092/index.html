ERISA Benefits Consulting founder Mark Johnson has a 20 year career history bridging four closely related areas: ERISA; the management and financing of pension, 401(k), group life and health and retiree medical plans; labor relations and personnel policy.
  • served as chief ERISA compliance officer for a Fortune 500 company
  • planned and implemented changes in workforce and total compensation strategy
  • made hundreds of presentations to employees, association and union members using practical experience and humor to enliven the program
  • negotiated group life and health, disability, pension and 401(k) plans with unions
  • created new pension, 401(k) and group life and health in response to changing employer/employee needs, collective bargaining agreements or as part of mergers, acquisitions and spinoffs
  • designed and implemented early retirement bridge programs and voluntary severance plans
  • directed the ERISA appeals process with authority to decide all pension, group life and health and 401(k) cases
  • managed pension plans with over $10 billion dollars in assets
  • presented arbitration cases and represented employers in federal and state court,
In addition to a J.D. from the University of Houston, Mr. Johnson earned a Ph.D in Communication Studies from the University of Massachusetts. Prior to his corporate and legal careers, he taught, researched and published as a fulltime university faculty member for 5 years and continues to teach on a partime basis, currently as an Adjunct Professor of Business Communication at Tarrant County College.

Mark Johnson is also the founder and owner of "H.O.Heidelberg" and importer and distributor since 1984 of model railroad equipment and accessories.

Prior to founding ERISA Benefits Consulting, Mr. Johnson has served as:
  • Managing Director, Benefits Compliance & Pensions, American Airlines,
  • Attorney Southwestern Bell Corporation
  • Associate Professor of Communication, Texas Wesleyan University
  • Assistant Professor of Communication, University of Houston
Mark is a licensed attorney in Texas and is a Board Certified Labor and Employment Law Specialist, Texas Board of Legal Specialization.
