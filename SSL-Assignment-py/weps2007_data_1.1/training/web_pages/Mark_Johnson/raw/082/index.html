ABOUT MARK - Mark Johnson was born and raised in Anchorage, Alaska. He graduated West Anchorage High School in 1985 and subsequently joined the United States Marine Corps for a 4 year tour of duty. Mark has always been interested in aviation. He obtained his private pilot license on his 17th birthday. Upon his return home from the Marine Corps in 1989, Mark went to work for the local Cessna Aircraft dealer at Lake Hood in Anchorage. In 1992 he bought the business and operated it until 2004, when Mark moved to the Valley and started work as a Realtor. Mark has been married to his wife, Wrynn, for the last 10 years. Wrynn is an art teacher at Wasilla Middle School and their beautiful daughter, Reagan, is in third grade at Cottonwood Creek Elementary.

MARK'S PHILOSOPHY - Mark knows his job is all about customer service.  Lots of today's homebuyers and sellers are educated and experienced.  What they want most throughout the process is access to information, advice and guidance when they need it, and representation that will protect their interests.  This is how Mark does business.  He believes that your access to him is paramount to you having a successful transaction.  Prove it to yourself.  Give a call or email to see how long it takes for Mark to get in touch with you!

SEARCH - You have access to the Alaska MLS system right from this website.  Click on the Search MLS button at the top of this page and you will have acccess to information about thousands of real estate listings from all areas of the state.

REPRESENTATION - Before you begin working with a Realtor or real estate licensee, you should take time to learn about representation so you know who is obligated to look out for your best interests.  Be sure to visit the REPRESENTATION page to learn the details.
