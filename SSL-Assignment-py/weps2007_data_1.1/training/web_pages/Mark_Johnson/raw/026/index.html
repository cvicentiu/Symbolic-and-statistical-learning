  Mark Johnson

Class:
Freshman
Hometown:
Los Angeles, CA
High School:
Dorsey
Height / Weight:
6-4 / 230
Position:
Linebacker
Birthdate:
01/09/1988
Major:
Undecided

Buckeye Bits: Ranked the No. 6 inside linebacker prospect in the nation coming out of high school ... hopes to follow in the footsteps of former Dorsey H.S. graduate and Buckeye Na'il Diggs ... OSU coaches like his size and strength and say he could play the Mike or Sam linebacker positions.

High School: Collected 120 total tackles, including 18 for loss, and four pass break-ups as a senior ... named all-state and his league's defensive player of the year as a junior when he had 138 tackles, eight quarterback sacks and an interception.

Personal: Is the son of Darryl Johnson.
