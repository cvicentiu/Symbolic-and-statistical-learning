NOTES
I worked on this CD for about 5 months, Some of the songs included on it are political in nature, a few are derived from true stories that friends have told me about, some are fictional, and some are about true periods in my own life.

This is a true Folk Ballad Rock CD that many of my fans have been waiting for, I explored several geners on this CD due to I enjoy most every genre and am sure you do too in some degree. I hope I can generate more fans with this 3rd CD of mine.

The title of this CD: Working Together is a dream I've had most of my life, I'd like one day for the world to work together as one so we can all live free and not have hatred towards one another due to our Governments can't seem to get along. Just because the Governments of the world can't get along doesn't mean the people of the world can't get along.

Like what the ex Beatle John Lennon said years ago in his song IMAGINE, "Imagine no countries, I wonder if you can" I myself have always wondered if it could be possible for the people of the world to someday live as friends and stop fighting, because as you know, almost all wars are started by Governmental leaders with their childish attitude of "MINE, MINE, MINE," or due to their prejudice "YOU'RE A DIFFERENT COLOR OR RELIGION AND FOR SOME REASON I DON'T LIKE THAT COLOR OR RELIGION" attitude.

It makes me sick.

I have 3 CD's on cdbaby.com now, please purchase them and share them with your family and friends, lets rid ourselves of our own internal chains that bind us to not want to understand other countries and or races of people. We all need to get our heads out of the sand and usher in new and free governments worldwide, nobody deserves to be tortured or be held captive by a hateful government who only wants to have the power & control and all of our hard earned money for themselves, and then they don't help out their countries people. We need to wake up from this since we are the workers of the world, lets start WORKING TOGETHER.

I hope you enjoy this new CD, I added 2 songs I have on my first 2 CD's, I thought maybe you'd enjoy the live guitar versions of these 2 songs when I originally recorded them back in 1982.

Thank you for visiting my newest music web page.
