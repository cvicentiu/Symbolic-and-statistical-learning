Stephan Johnson

Age: 31

junior middleweight boxer who turned professional in 1987 and had a 27-8-1 record; knocked out in the 10th round of a USBA title fight by Paul Vaden on Nov. 20, 1999. He died of a brain injury.

Died: Atlantic City, N.J., December 6, 1999
