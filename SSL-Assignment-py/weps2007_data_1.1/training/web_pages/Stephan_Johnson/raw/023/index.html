STEPHAN JOHNSON

Mr. Johnson graduated from St. John's College in California with a B.A. in Philosophy. He then earned an M.A. in Religious Studies from the Catholic University of America in Washington, D.C. He has completed doctoral classes in Theology at Marquette University on his way towards a Ph.D. He enjoys politics and comedy, and the similarity between the two.
