DOCTORS HOSPITAL GUEST SERVICES
EMPLOYEES RECEIVE RECOGNITION

Doctors Hospital celebrated its Guest Services Week under the theme “Exceeding Customer Service”. Debbie Ferguson, Director of Human Resources and Training at the British Colonial was the guest speaker for the opening ceremony; Jeffrey Beckles, CEO Excellence in Motion and Nassau Cruises Ltd facilitated a customer service workshop; Philip Simon, Executive Director of the Bahamas Chamber of Commerce was the guest speaker for the awards luncheon; and Gamal Newry and Sandra Dean Patterson facilitated a security education seminar. Guest Services comprises of Security, Reception, and Volunteers, an integral part of Doctors Hospital. The following were singled out as Doctors Hospital Superstars recognized for their valuable contribution of providing excellent customer service.
