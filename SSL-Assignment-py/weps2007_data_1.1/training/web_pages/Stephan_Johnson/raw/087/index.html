Did You Know?

Speaking to the military on October 11, 1798, President John Adams stated that the government was incapable of coercing good behavior.  He explained:  We have no government armed with power capable of contending with human passions unbridled by morality and religion…Our Constitution was made only for a moral and religious people.  It is wholly inadequate to the government of any other.*
