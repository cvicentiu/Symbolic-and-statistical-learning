I Live Where Eagles Soar
by: Violet Howard

ISBN: 0-7414-0577-6 ©2001
Price: $9.95
Book Size: 8.5 x 11, 73 pages
Category/Subject: Autobiography/

Abstract:
I Live Where Eagles Soar is a heartfelt work that offers a richly detailed and intimate memoir of the many twists and turns in my life. I open my story with a recollection of the diverse and intriguing personalities of my two grandparents, as well as personal reminiscences of my family rearing children in a variety of exotic locales, such as Alaska; and I recall my brother, who was at Pearl Harbor the day it was bombed by the Japanese. This diary is a sensitive chronicle framed with personal recollections of emotions and events, remembered conversations, and insights into the importance of faith in my life. I hope this book can explain some of the hardships and good times, as it was then. Life is what we make it. We all have choices to make, be they good or bad ones.
