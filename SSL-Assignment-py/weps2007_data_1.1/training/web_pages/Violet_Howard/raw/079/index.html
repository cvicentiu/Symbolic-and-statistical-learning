In 1955 President Eisenhower was President, Ray Kroc opens his first McDonald’s restaurant, Disneyland opened in Anaheim, the minimum wage was $1.00 per hour, and first class postage was 3 cents. In that same year 558 seventeen and eighteen year olds graduated from Jordan High School in Long Beach, California.

 

In 1965 a small group of classmates, spearheaded by Bill Steuber, met to plan our first  reunion celebration. Over the years a core group of grads have met, periodically, to plan and produce several more reunion celebrations. This year, your committee has planned a week of activities befitting the significance of our 50th reunion. We have managed to keep in touch with about half of our class.  If you are reading this letter you are among the fortunate.  Don’t pass up this opportunity to celebrate this event by sharing the last fifty years with your classmates.  Attached is a list of those who have already signed up.  Please don’t miss this very special event.
