5.3.3.3. Esme Monro Wyatt Corrie, born 11 Apr 1919 in Quetta, N. W. India; married Douglas Percival. 

5.4. Ada Elizabeth Corrie, born 18 May 1842 in St. Giles, Bloomsbury, London; resided in The Hollies, Ilfracombe, 
    Devon. died 25 Mar 1912 in Barnstaple; married Oct-Dec 1883 in Barnstaple to Joseph Heginbottom who died 7 
    Sep 1885 in The Pines, Ilfracombe. 

5.5. Amelia (Minnie) Charlotte Corrie, born Aug 1849 in St. Giles, London; died 9 Jun 1943 in Paddington aged 94 
    in 171 Sussex Gardens, London, interred in Richmond, Surrey. 

5.6. Jessie Matilda Corrie, born 1850; resided 1885-1922 in 30 Sussex Square, London W.2; died 19 Mar 1922 in 
    Paddington, London; married 15 Jun 1869 in St. Stephens, Paddington, Kensington, London, to Thomas Evans, 
    born 1831 in Bexley, Dartford, Kent, son of David and Annie Evans, partner in father's silk firm of David 
    Evans & Sons, weavers and printers in Crayford, Kent, printers of bandanna squares in 36-37 Milk Street, 
    London, E.C., with a throwing mill at Tring, Hertfordshire, warehouse in 30 St. Pauls Churchyard, with 
    headquarters in 1 Wood Street, London, E.C.1; resided in Stoneyhurst, Bexley Heath, Kent; died 1 May 1884 
    in Bexley; they had four children (1) Jessie Maude, (2) Evelyn Minnie, (3) Robert Corrie, (4) David Howard. 

5.6.1. Jessie Maude Evans, born Jul-Sep 1870 in Kensington; died Jul-Sep 1950 in N. E. Cheshire. 

5.6.2. Evelyn Minnie Evans, born 1874; not married; died Oct-Dec 1944 in Conwy, Caernarfonshire. 

5.6.3. Robert Corrie Evans, born 1874; resided in The Grove, Brampton, Huntingdonshire; died 15 Apr 1948 in 
      Huntingdon; married Annie Sybil Dunkin Earle, born April 1893 in Cambridge, died 29 Jan 1971 in Cambridge. 
      They had no children, but amongst god-children mentioned in Robert's Will is Dennis Corrie Kenneth Munro. 

5.6.4. David Howard Evans, O.B.E., D.L., J.P., born Apr-Jun 1880 in Kensington, London; mayor, reserve 
      officer, in 2nd Dragoon Guards, in Jul 1914 was captain remount officer in Cambridgeshire no.5 district; 
      latterly resided in Priestwell House, East Haddon, Northamptonshire; died 13 Jan 1957 in Brixworth, 
      Northamptonshire; married Violet Isobel(la) and had three children (1) Sheila Violet, 
      (2) Veronique Beatrice, (3) Irene Elizabeth. 

5.6.4.1. Sheila Violet Howard Evans, named 30 Apr 1948 an executor in her father's Will. 

5.6.4.2. Veronique Beatrice Howard Evans. 

5.6.4.3. Irene Elizabeth Howard Evans. 

5.7. Edward Knowles Corrie, M.A., born Oct-Dec 1853 in St. Pancras, London, baptized 14 Dec 1953 in St. Pancras 
    Old Church; barrister in Lincolns Inn, London; resided 4 Chislehurst Road, Richmond, Surrey, died there 4 
    Nov 1937; married 9 Oct 1880 in Marylebone, London, to Mildred Alice Cooke, born 1853 daughter of William 
    Major Cooke, London Metropolitan Police Magistrate, died 27 Jan 1921; they had four children (1) Dorothy, 
    (2) Geoffrey, (3) Donald Welldon, (4) Guy Temple. 

5.7.1. Dorothy Corrie, born and died Oct-Dec 1881 in Kensington London. 

5.7.2. Geoffrey Corrie, born Jul-Aug 1884 and died 19 Aug 1884 in Kensington, London. 

5.7.3. Donald Welldon Corrie, M.A., M.B.E., born 29 Aug 1886 in Paddington, London; civil servant, Board of 
      Education; resided in Rottingdean, Sussex, and Westhanger Place, Westbrook, Surrey; died 19 Sep 1962 in 
      Cuckfield; married first Apr-Jun 1915 in Newhaven to Vera Maude Brown daughter of William Brown, born 
      1887, died 19 May 1954 in Rottingdean; they had two children (1) Barbara Mary, and (2) Ruth Vera; married 
      secondly Apr-Jun 1956 in Cuckfield to Elsie Winifred Gladys Smith, born 1900, died 1979 in Taormina Dean 
      Road, Godalming, Surrey.
