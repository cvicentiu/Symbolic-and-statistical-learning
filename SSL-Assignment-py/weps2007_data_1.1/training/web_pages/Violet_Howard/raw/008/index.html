Pedigree

Last Edited 29 Oct 2005

     Captain Claude Henry Meysey Meysey-Thompson was born on 7 April 1887. He was the son of Sir Henry Meysey Meysey-Thompson, 1st and last Baron Knaresborough and Ethel Adeline Pottinger. He died on 17 June 1915 at age 28 in Ypres, Belgium, from wounds received in action.
     Captain Claude Henry Meysey Meysey-Thompson fought in the First World War.
