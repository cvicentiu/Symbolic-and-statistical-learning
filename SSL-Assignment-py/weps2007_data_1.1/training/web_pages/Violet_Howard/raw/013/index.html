February 26, 2007
  
Howard, Violet (Pikeville)
posted January 11, 2007

Violet Howard, 76, died Tuesday, Jan. 9, 2007.

She is survived by six children, Gracie, Barbara, Terry, Ricky, Glenn and Jimmie.

Funeral services will be at 10 a.m. CST Friday in the chapel. Burial is in Hitchcox Cemetery.

Arrangements are by Putnam-Reed Funeral Home, Highway 127 South, Pikeville.
