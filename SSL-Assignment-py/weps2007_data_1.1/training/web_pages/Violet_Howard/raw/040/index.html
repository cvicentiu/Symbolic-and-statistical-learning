1960 BACK.  1961 FORWARD ALL HAVE ANNUALS (PINECONES) AND YOU CAN LOOK AT THEM AT
http://www.bellcountypubliclibraries.org/crm/pinecones  CLASSES WITH AN * HAVE AN ANNUAL.  WHEN CLASS NAMES ARE MARKED TO SHOW WHERE THEY ARE IN PHOTO. f =1st row  s =2nd row  t =3rd row  o =4th row  i =5th row.
numbers will always be from left to right, front to back. examples f1 is the first person in 1st row at the left.  05 is the fifth person from the left in row 5 from the front.
