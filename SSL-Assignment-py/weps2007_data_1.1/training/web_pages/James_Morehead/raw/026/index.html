Morehead, Joseph M. (Joseph Motley), b. 1840.
Papers, 1753-1919 (bulk 1880-1913).
5,450 items (6.0 linear feet).

Morehead was a Confederate officer and a lawyer of Greensboro, N.C.
Legal and business papers, chiefly 1880-1913, of lawyers Joseph Motley
Morehead and James Turner Morehead, Jr. (1838-1919), of Guilford County,
N.C.  Papers include briefs and correspondence with clients and other
lawyers.  Also, papers concerning the Guilford Battleground Association of
which Joseph Morehead was president; family correspondence after 1900 of
James Morehead; and antebellum papers of their father.  Volumes include
records of cases and estate settlements.  Few papers pertain to the two
men's careers as Confederate officers and in Reconstruction politics.
James Turner Morehead should not be confused with his cousin, James T.
Morehead (1840-1908), North Carolina and New York City industrialist.

Gifts prior to 1940, and 1953.
