Description (revised)




JAMES MOREHEAD. First Day Cover signed: "Jim Morehead", 5½x3½ postcard. FDC honoring US Coast Guard Cutter "Eagle", embossed with 14-cent postage, postmarked Seattle, Washington, August 4, 1978, FIRST DAY OF ISSUE. Army pilot Morehead became an ace in the European Theater, World War II, downing 8 enemy planes. Fine condition.
