Events- Fall 2006
AUDITION & ONGOING TECHNIQUE CLASS

CLASS: Our weekly modern dance technique class encourages participants to enjoy as well
as challenge themselves as movers. A director of the company will conduct class each week:
Larisa Eastman, Allyson Esposito, Stacy Thomas Wolfson.

AUDITION: TSMP needs dancers for our 2007 Season. Instead of holding a traditional
“audition”, we will be seeking dancers through their attendance of our Sunday night class,
anytime from now through January 2007. This gives you the chance to try us on before
committing your time!

WHAT WE ARE LOOKING FOR: Dancers will need to be able to commit to Sunday evenings
6.30pm-9pm and Friday evenings 4pm-9pm (not every dancer will be needed for the whole
time on Friday). We are looking for dancers who are committed, creative individuals and who
have a great working ethic. We are interested in people with different kinds of movement
training, including improvisation skills.

WHEN/WHERE: Sundays, 6.30-8.00pm, $7 at Rast Ballet Studios, 1803 West Byron, Suite
201 (parking in lot behind building on weekends, Brown Line Irving Park stop).

CONTACT: For more information see www.thespacemovement.org or call 312 307 2168.
