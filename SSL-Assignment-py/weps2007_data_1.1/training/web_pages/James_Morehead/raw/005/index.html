James Morehead’s Summary

James Morehead joined SupportSoft in October 2003 and is currently Vice President of Product Management and Marketing. James leads a global team responsible for crafting and evangelizing SupportSoft’s strategy and product direction for the service provider (including DSL, cable and wireless service providers) and enterprise markets.

Prior to joining SupportSoft, James spent nearly four years leading Portal Software’s Solutions Marketing team for key markets including broadband, wireless, wireline, digital media and online content. James also spent eight years with Nortel Networks primarily in Nortel’s Wireless Networks business unit with roles in strategic marketing, sales and network engineering.

As a Computer Engineering graduate from the University of Waterloo, James started his career as a real-time software engineer. James then went on to complete his MBA at the University of Toronto. James has participated in Consumer Marketing Strategy courses at the Kellogg School of Management.

His speaking experience spans numerous industry events in North America and internationally including Broadband World Forum Asia 2006 (Hong Kong), Broadband World Forum Europe 2005 (Madrid), Broadband World Forum Asia 2005 (Yokohama), DSL Forum 2004 (Toronto), SSPA 2004 (San Diego), Mobile Billing 2003 (Cannes), Wireless Location and Positioning 2003 (San Francisco), UMTS 2002 (Paris), Billing World 2002 (Baltimore), NWEC 2002, Billing World 2001 (Orlando), Supercomm 2001 (Atlanta), Mobile Commerce Conference 2001 (San Jose), Mobile Internet 2000 (Paris).

James has been interviewed for and/or placed articles in numerous publications including European Communications, Telecommunications Magazine, Wireless Business and Technology, RCR Wireless, America’s Network, Wireless Week, Telephony, Billing World, Billing Magazine, Billing Plus and Wireless Review. James has had articles published in prestigious industry journals including ECTA Review 2001 and IEC Review 2002.
