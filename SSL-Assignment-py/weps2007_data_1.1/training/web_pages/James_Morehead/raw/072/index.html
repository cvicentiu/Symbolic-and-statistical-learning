SupportSoft Unveils Vision for the DSL Home of Tomorrow at Broadband World Forum 

    REDWOOD CITY, Calif., Sept. 16 /PRNewswire-FirstCall/ -- SupportSoft,
Inc., (Nasdaq: SPRT), a leading provider of Real-Time Service Management
(RTSM(TM)) software, today announced that James Morehead, Senior Director of
Product Management for SupportSoft, will unveil the Company's new Digital 360
vision for triple play service delivery in conjunction with the DSL Forum's
DSLHome Roadshow, taking place at Broadband World Forum in Venice, Italy on
September 20 - 23.  Digital 360 explores the ability for service providers to
have one view of both the last network mile and customer premises' equipment
-- combined with one integrated set of IP-based service automation tools to
manage, diagnose and resolve customer problems in real time.
    "By developing new standards and embracing new applications, the DSL Forum
is tailoring DSL to meet the needs of the next generation of multi-media
services and the online community, to drive the technology's adoption around
the world. It could not be done without the industry vision of our members,
such as SupportSoft, who are helping to shape the future of DSL services,"
said Tom Starr, President of the international DSL Forum.
    Whether VoIP, video or broadband-related, SupportSoft's Digital 360 vision
provides a strategic framework to give customers, field technicians, network
operations staff and call center personnel a consistent way to pinpoint
problems, understand interdependencies between the network and premises
related issues, and choose the best means to resolve issues once identified.
Installation and management of wireless networks, reliable connectivity,
consistent voice quality, clear video signal and dependable network access are
just a few of the basic needs that every consumer or small business has -- or
will demand -- of their DSL provider.

    The DSL Forum is an international industry consortium of about 200 leading
service providers, equipment manufacturers and other interested parties,
focused on developing the full potential of broadband DSL to meet the needs of
the mass market. With the established goal of 200 million customers by year
end 2005 and 500 million by 2010, the DSL Forum works to streamline processes,
develop specifications and share best practices that set the stage for
effective deployments, and explosive global DSL growth. By developing new
standards and embracing new applications, DSL Forum is tailoring DSL to meet
the needs of the next generation of multi-media services and the online
community. Established in 1994, more information about the DSL Forum is
available from its industry site http://www.dslforum.org.

    About SupportSoft
    SupportSoft is a leading provider of Real-Time Service Management
(RTSM(TM)) software designed to ease enterprise technical support, provide for
IT endpoint automation and enable triple play service automation for VoIP,
video or broadband delivery by service providers to their customers. Digital
service providers benefiting from SupportSoft solutions include Adelphia
Communications, BellSouth, Charter Communications, Comcast Communications, Cox
Communications, Time Warner, TeliaSonera and UPC. Enterprises that have
licensed SupportSoft software for IT requirements include ADP, Bank of
America, Cisco Systems, IBM, Procter & Gamble, Siebel Systems, Sony and
Thomson Financial. Managed service providers that utilize our solutions to
provide outsourced services to their enterprise customers include Accenture,
ACS, CGI, CompuCom, CSC and IBM Global Services.
