MEET THE GAELS

27 • Neil Clark
Freshman • Left Winger • 6-2 • 200
Coqulitan, B.C./Cowichan Valley Capitals (BCJHL)

GENERAL:
Arrives in New Rochelle for his first season with the Gaels from Cowichan Valley of the BCHL...Possesses good size, speed, and solid offensive abilities...A well-conditioned athlete who works hard both on and off the ice...Born 7/25/81...Majoring in Business...Shoots Left.

BEFORE IONA:
Clark spent most of his three-year BCHL career with the Langley Hornets before finishing up with Cowichan Valley last season..Tallied 28 goals and 36 assists (64 points) in 55 games last season with Cowichan...captained Langley for two seasons...Tallied 14 goals and 34 assists for Langly in 1999-2000...1999 graduate of Gleneagle Secondary School.
