Dr. Neil Clark Warren, founder of eHarmony, has counseled thousands of singles and married couples, spoken extensively at conferences and seminars across the country, and written eight books on love, marriage and emotional health. Well over 750,000 copies of Dr. Warren's best seller for singles, "Finding the Love of Your Life, " have been sold, and it has been translated into ten languages. He has been recommended as a Must Read by Time Magazine, and his book "Date ... or Soul Mate?," directly complements eHarmony's objectives. Since receiving his doctorate from The University of Chicago in 1967, Dr. Warren has become one of America's best-known experts on mate selection.

Practicing Psychology: Marriages and Divorces
Since earning his doctorate at The University of Chicago, Dr. Warren has used his role as a psychologist to learn WHY relationships fail, and he has applied that research to help thousands of people build stronger, more satisfying lives. eHarmony is transforming that knowledge into an effective web-based tool for finding a compatible long-term partner and creating a brilliant, loving relationship.

Divorce Autopsies: Why Marriages Fail
In over 30 years of practice, Dr. Warren has seen literally hundreds of failed marriages and the damage they cause to children, family, and friends. He began inquiring into each failed marriage, dissecting it to understand what went wrong and when. Twenty years of study and research led to one overwhelming conclusion: "In almost every case, these were two persons who should never have married each other! They really didn't belong together. They thought they did, but they were not well matched."

Which Marriages Succeed: A Scientific Approach
At the same time, Dr. Warren was also able to uncover a set of principles that consistently appear in successful marriages. Testing these principles with a scientific model and over 5,000 married persons, Dr. Warren was able to develop a predictive model of compatibility. Using the 29 dimensions that he found correlate to marriage success, Dr. Warren began to help singles nationwide make the right choices and build successful relationships.

Online Matchmaking: Putting Principles into Practice
After publishing many books on relationships and emotional health, and leading hundreds of seminars around North America, Dr. Warren founded eHarmony, adding the power of the Internet to his strategy for creating healthy relationships. This online tool is the culmination of his mission to help millions of people find their Soul Mate and fall in love for all the right reasons.
