Neil Robertson-Ravo
London, United Kingdom
 

Neil is an analyst programmer for Reed Exhibitions, the world's leading exhibition organiser and a division of Reed Elsevier. He has developed applications with ColdFusion since 1997 and with Macromedia Spectra since its first release. He is also a heavy-duty Macromedia beta tester, with Dreamweaver, Fireworks, ColdFusion Server/Studio, Sitespring, and Spectra under his belt; he has tested many of these products since their first release.

Current passions include developing with ColdFusion MX and ColdFusion Components, XML, Director MX, Contribute and, recently, Macromedia Flash.

With an eye for design and development, he is an ongoing voice and listener around the ColdFusion community. He continues to forge and strengthen his position in browser-based application development, focusing particularly on Content Management Systems.

Born and bred in Scotland, he now makes his living in London. He plays hockey for the Old Cranleighan Hockey Club.
