Practicing Psychology: Marriages and Divorces. Since earning his doctorate at The University of Chicago, Dr. Warren has used his role as a psychologist to learn WHY relationships fail, and he has applied that research to help thousands of people build stronger, more satisfying lives. Harmony is transforming that knowledge into an effective web-based tool for finding a compatible long-term partner and creating a brilliant, loving relationship.

Divorce Autopsies: Why Marriages Fail. In over 30 years of practice, Dr. Warren has seen literally hundreds of failed marriages and the damage they cause to children, family, and friends. He began inquiring into each failed marriage, dissecting it to understand what went wrong and when. They thought they did, but they were not well matched."

Which Marriages Succeed: A Scientific Approach. At the same time, Dr. Warren was also able to uncover a set of principles that consistently appear in successful marriages. Using the 29 dimensions that he found correlate to marriage success, Dr. Warren began to help singles nationwide make the right choices and build successful relationships.
