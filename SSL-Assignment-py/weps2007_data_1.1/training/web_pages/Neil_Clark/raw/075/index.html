Neil Clark joins Aviagen's European Customer Focus Team

Pictured is Neil Clark, who has recently joined the Aviagen European Customer Focus Team as a Technical Services Manager. Neil has been with Aviagen for many years, having previously worked in the pedigree selection team and most recently managing the pedigree broiler farms.

Neil now undergoes an intensive induction and training period before assuming responsibilities for supporting customers in Europe.  He will initially focus on broiler management, followed by hatchery and Parent Stock management.

Dinah Nicholson, Aviagen's European Technical Director, comments: "This switch in roles is a development step in Neil's desire to learn new skills and be exposed to new experiences within customer focus teams. Aviagen are committed to career development for our staff and we are continually looking at ways to enhance our service to customers."
