Neil Clark

Neil Clark writes regularly for The Guardian, The Australian, The Times, New Statesman, The Spectator, Racing Post and The Fleet Street Letter.
18 Apr, 2006 - The cost of living lawfully in Britain
...The cost of living lawfully has outpaced inflation more than three times over the last 13 years. The TV licence fee has risen 43%, road tax & motor insurance are up nearly 46%, and council tax has more than doubled on average. Together, and weighted in line with the official statistics, these costs of compliance have risen by 82.2% since 1993...
13 Apr, 2006 - The cost of living lawfully in Britain
