Studio 9 one 2

Architecture is a varied field, rich with history yet constantly evolving. It is on that basis of tradition and innovation that Patrick Killen founded STUDIO 9 ONE 2. With the client's objective in mind, our goal with each project is to fuse the best of Architectures past with imagination, original designs and contemporary technology. The result of this philosophy whether applied to architecture, planning or urban design is a quality environment that addresses human needs for comfort, safety, efficiency and aesthetics, as well as the cultural need for progression. Patrick Killen serves as Managing Principal of the firm and is charged with design and planning responsibilities. Mr. Killen leads the design team with his seventeen years of experience to ensure all client needs and specifications are met. From the programming phase through design development and construction, STUDIO 9 ONE 2 provides superior service and designs with a commitment to client and profession always at the forefront.
