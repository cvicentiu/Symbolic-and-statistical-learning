Patrick Killen ... has been covering some of Asia's most dynamic economies for Market News Service , a financial news service with bureaus in New York , Washington , London , and Frankfurt. He is the editor of No. 1 Shimbun , the Foreign Correspondent's Club monthly journal. He was UPI's bureau chief in Chicago and managing editor of the Yomiuri's Report from Japan , a newsletter for executives. He has covered the Tokyo Olympics , the wars in India and Vietnam , race riots in Malaysia and Singapore , and martial law in the Philippines.
