About Our Family Research


Legend has it that the original family name was KILLEEN but when Patrick & Winifred, who were both born in Ireland, settled down in Heworth, County Durham, England the local Catholic schoolmaster got them to Anglisice their name by dropping an “e” from the spelling to make the present name of Killen.
Patrick Killen was born in Ireland circa 1829. He emigrated to England where he married Winifred Gray (who was from Sligo, Ireland) on 10th May 1856 in the Roman Catholic Chapel at Felling Shore, Heworth, County Durham.
Their seven children were all born in Heworth and baptised in the local Roman Catholic church.

Thomas and Margaret Anna Hart were both born in Ireland; all their eight children were born in Heworth, County Durham and baptised in the local Roman Catholic church.

Details shown are the bare essentials of information I hold.
Further information on more than 300 family members is held on file and can be made available to members of the Killen Family
tracing their roots.
