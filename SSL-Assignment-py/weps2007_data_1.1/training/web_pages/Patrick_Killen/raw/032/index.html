The Gibson Home
2162 Circle Drive
Hermosa Beach

The award winning Gibson Home is located in the sand section of Hermosa Beach on a hillside overlooking the Pacific Ocean. Designed by renown contemporary architect Patrick Killen and built by Widmann Development this modern masterpiece was the recipient of the 2001 AIA Merit award and was featured in Better Homes and Gardens "Beautiful New Homes" (Winter 2002). Waterfalls at the front and rear of the property feature a one-sheet flow design inspired by architect Louis Barragon.

This magnificent home features almost 8,000 sq ft of living space including five bedrooms, ten bathrooms, a home theater, gym and private guest quarters. Alabaster Travertine walls, Pakistani Marble flooring, built-in Lace Wood and Tulipwood cabinetry all contribute to the great warmth this contemporary masterpiece conveys. The panoramic white water views from this home are daily reminders of the unique gem that is the Gibson Home.
