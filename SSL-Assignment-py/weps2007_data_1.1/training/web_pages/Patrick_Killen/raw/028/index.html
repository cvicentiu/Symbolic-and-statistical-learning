Cletus' First Collegiate Dictionary of Japanese Chin Music

wa

Harmony, unity, concord. When the star pitcher of the Press Club Alley Cats in Tokyo defected in spring 2004 to a rival softball team, he told the manager that the Cats didn’t have wa anymore. Manager Patrick Killen later remarked, “I don’t remember anyone by that name.”
