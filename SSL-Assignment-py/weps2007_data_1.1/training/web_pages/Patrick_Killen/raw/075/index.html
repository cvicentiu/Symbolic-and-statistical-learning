Top U.S. Architects Tour European Architecture and Manufacturing Facility

NanaWall Systems Provides Architects With A Tour of European Architecture and Its Factory

  • ArchitecturalTour2006.doc
®, opening glass wall system, offers an architectural tour to some of the country’s most innovative architects. Ten of the top U.S. residential architects joined NANAWALL SYSTEMS in Germany for the NANAWALL SYSTEMS Architectural Tour 2006, March 25th - March 29th. NANAWALL SYSTEMS flew its guests to Germany, all expenses paid, for a tour of opening glass wall applications that marked the birth of the NanaWall® over 15 years ago.

Through tours of Amsterdam, Netherlands, Osnabruck and Boissendorf, Germany, architects saw first-hand how Europeans continue to utilize opening glass wall systems, and saw where and how the trend originated. NANAWALL SYSTEMS also arranged for a tour of the Solarlux manufacturing facility where U.S.-bound NanaWall® systems are manufactured everyday.

"We are providing the top residential architects of the United States with the opportunity to see first-hand the use of large, operable glass walls by showing them applications in areas where the technology originated," says George Wiedenhofer, sales manager of NANAWALL SYSTEMS. "We hope that these talented architects will come away with an appreciation for this technology and understanding how the NanaWall® can dramatically enhance their architectural designs."

Architects selected to participate in the NANAWALL SYSTEMS Architectural Tour 2006 were: Gary Beyerl, VP and principal Burns & Beyerl Architects, Chicago; Louis DesRoiser, president, DesRosiers Architects, Bloomfield Hills, MI; Luben Dimcheff, Smith-Miller & Hawkinson Architects, New York; Curtis Gelotte, senior principal Curtis Gelotte Architects, PS, Kirkland, WA; Christopher Heinritz, managing principal Pachowski Heinritz Association Sacramento, CA; Edward M. Jones, principal-in-charge of design Jones Studio, Inc., Scottsdale, AZ; Patrick Killen, principal Studio 9 one 2, Hermosa Beach, CA; Andrew Scott Kirschner, principal Jackson Kirchner Architects and Associates, P.A., Melbourne, FL, Mark P. Nasrallah, principal architect Nasrallah Fine Architectural Design, Winter Park, FL; and Alfred F. Plarr, Jr, principal Platt Architecture, Brevard, NC.

For more information about the architects and NanaWall® opening glass wall products, call NANAWALL SYSTEMS, INC. at (800) 873-5673, or visit the award-winning Web site (including an extensive photo gallery, case studies, and complete animations of the NanaWall®) at www.nanawall.com.
