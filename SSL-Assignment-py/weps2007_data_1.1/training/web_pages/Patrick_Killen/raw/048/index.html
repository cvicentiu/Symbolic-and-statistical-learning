Margaret "Inez" Cowley
died September 7, 2006


Margaret “Inez” Cowley, 89, of Highlands, TX went to be with the Lord on Thursday, September 7, 2006.

She was born in Yoakum, TX and graduated from Crosby High School. Margaret loved feeding birds especially Hummingbirds almost as much as spending time with her grandchildren, great-grandchildren, great great-grandchildren and her puppy “Penny”.

She was preceded in death by her husband: L.G. Cowley; daughter: Kathy Killen; mother: Vera Caver; father: W.I. Caver; sisters: Ivy Henderson and Nell Sellers; brother: Bill Caver.

She is survived by her sons and daughters-in-law: Wayne and Mary Ann Cowley and Bubba and Nancy Cowley; sisters: Alvis Faubian, Eleanor McNatt, Doris Davis, Helen Jacobs and Ruth Davis; brothers: Dale and Tommy Caver; grandchildren: Tim Luedicke and wife Debra, Kim Davis and husband Doug, Michelle Cowley and Patrick Killen; 9 great-grandchildren, 2 great great-grandchildren and numerous nieces and nephews.

The family received friends 6:00 p.m. to 8:00 p.m. Friday, September 8, 2006 at Earthman Funeral Home. Funeral services will be held at 1:00 p.m. Saturday, September 9, 2006 at Earthman Funeral Home with Brother Jim Lamb officiating. The interment will follow at Memory Gardens Cemetery, 8624 Garth Road, Baytown, TX.

Donnie Jacobs, Wayne Luedicke, Justin Music, Ronnie Beard, Joshua Vaught and Tim Benoit will serve as pallbearers.

Honorary pallbearers will be Charles Sanders and Milton Sellers.
