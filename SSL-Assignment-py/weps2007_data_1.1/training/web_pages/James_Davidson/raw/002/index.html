This memorial website was created in the memory of our loved one, James Davidson who was born in Tennessee on March 02, 1945 and passed away on November 05, 2004 at the age of 59. We will remember him forever.
He was a kind and loving man, soft spoken and gentle. He loved his family and his friends. He died the day before our 28th anniversary. He had End Stage Renal Disease. He never complained about his illness. He struggled  with it for two and a half years before he passed away. He would talk to me at the end and tell me that he saw Angles hovering about the room. I truely believe he saw them. I believe that the Angles came to take him to Heaven that day. There was a look on his face when death came to him that was so peaceful, that only God could put there.
I know that he is in a far better place than we can ever dream about. We will love him and miss him forever.

JAMES' POEM  

Prepared to rest
lay still, my James
to become of the earth
to blend with the rain.
Mother Nature's gentle hand
had slapped me in the face
when I learned that my true love
had traveled to a glorious place.
Stars, never seen again
by his lovely eyes
His lips never touched again
despite my bitter cries.
Petals sleeping on the ground
marks the place my love is found
and when the cold wind sobs his name,
know my James is cold, but safe . . .
And though November's sun is gone,
in its place, his love shines on . . .
