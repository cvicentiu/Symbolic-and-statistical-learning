DESCENDANTS OF
JAMES DAVIDSON and MARY CAREY



Descendants of James DAVIDSON

First Generation

1.   James DAVIDSON was born 1773 in North Carolina. He died about 1852 in Jones County, Georgia.
James married Mary CAREY, daughter of Mr. CAREY and Ann. Mary was born (1766?) in Ireland. 
She died after Aug 1852 in Jones County, Georgia.
They had the following children:
   +   2   M   i.     Carey DAVIDSON was born 1803.
   +   3   M   ii.    James C. DAVIDSON.

Second Generation

2.   Carey DAVIDSON (James) was born 1803 in Georgia.
Carey married Leah LESTER on 5 Mar 1829 in Jones County, Georgia.
They had the following children:
        4   M   i.    James C. DAVIDSON was born 1831 in Georgia.
       5   M   ii.    George DAVIDSON was born 1833 in Georgia.
       6   F   iii.   Mary Ann C. DAVIDSON was born 1835 in Georgia.
       7   F   iv.    Lucy DAVIDSON was born 1837 in Georgia.
       8   F   v.     Camilla DAVIDSON was born 1846 in Georgia.
       9   M   vi.    William DAVIDSON was born 1848 in Georgia.

3.   James C. DAVIDSON (James).
James married Sarah CAREY on 5 Sep 1820 in Jones County, Georgia.
They had the following children:
      10   M   i.     Julius DAVIDSON.
