TOMMY JAMES DAVIDSON

Tommy James Davidson, 64, of Reno, Nevada passed away on Friday, December 8, 2006 at the Veterans Affairs Medical Center in Reno, NV. Mr. Davidson was a retired Chief Petty Officer, with the US Coast Guard. He is survived by his son James Davidson (Angel); daughters Tammy Weeks (Brandon), Sabrina Ladd (Blaine); sister Linda Mears; four grandchildren. He was a devoted father and grandfather who was always there for his loved ones. He was a man of great strength and courage who often helped others in need. We love you and will miss you, Dad.
