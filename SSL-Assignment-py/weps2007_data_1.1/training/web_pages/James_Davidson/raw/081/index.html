James Davidson
 Official Number
47790

The James Davidson was a barque built at the Shepherd and Leech shipyard at Whitehaven, launched on the 24th April 1865. Like the Chrysolite, built at the same shipyard, she was owned by G.D.Nelson & Co. of Whitehaven.  The half model of the James Davidson shown above was presented to the Whitehaven Public Library in 1947 (see Source 2).
