The last Will and Testament of James Davidson of Saint George in the County of Charlotte, Farmer.

1stly I will and bequeath to my daughter Ellen Clinch wife of Patrick Clinch the sum of Twenty pounds payable at the expiration of six months after my decease.

2ndly I will and bequeath to my grandson James Clinch the sum of Twenty pounds payable upon his arriving at the age of twentyone years.

3rdly I will and bequeath to my grandson James Alexander Davidson the sum of Twenty pounds payable upon his arriving at the age of twentyone years.

4thly I will and bequeath to my son James Davidson all the real and personal Estate whereof I shall be possessed or whereunto I shall be entitled at the time of my decease he paying the three several legacies first mentioned which said several legacies are hereby expressly made chargeable upon the said real estate so bequeathed to my said son James.

And I do further will and bequeath to my wife Nancy Davidson a sufficient annual sum for her maintenance to be paid her by my said son during the time of her natural life which I also make chargeable upon the said real estate.

And I appoint my said son James Davidson the Executor for this my last will and testament.

Signed Sealed Published
and declared by the said 
James Davidson as his                                                                            his
last will and testament                                                                   James X Davidson                                        (Seal)
in presence of us, having been                                                               mark
first read and explained to him

William Jack
John E. Messenett
Mark Young
                                        February 22, 1836
