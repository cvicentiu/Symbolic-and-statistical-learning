Bariatric Surgery Center of Dallas (BSC Dallas) was developed to support the aftercare needs of patients after Roux-en-Y gastric bypass surgery and LAP-BAND® Adjustable Gastric Band surgery.

We are proud to offer conventional gastric bypass surgery, LAP-BAND® Adjustable Gastric Band surgery, and advanced laparoscopic surgical techniques to our patients. Our Dallas-based Bariatric Surgery Center provides the most individualized, comprehensive long-term aftercare programs to meet the patient's needs. Although our gastric bypass and LAP-BAND® Adjustable Gastric Band surgery center is located in North Texas, we extend our programs to patients outside of the North Texas area to offer you a convenient option for your gastric bypass surgery and LAP-BAND® Adjustable Gastric Band surgery recovery and follow-up.

Located on the campus of Presbyterian Hospital of Dallas, BSC Dallas can maximize your outcomes by utilizing many of their wide variety of resources. We are pleased to be a part of continuing Presbyterian Hospital's long-standing tradition of excellence in care delivery. Our aftercare program includes support groups, diet education, exercise programs, and social outings. You'll be with peers who have gone through or are now going through the same weight loss surgery recovery that you are. Convenient to most parts of the Dallas-Ft. Worth Metroplex and surrounding areas at Walnut Hill Lane and North Central Expressway, our services can be accessible to many in North Central Texas and beyond.

If we can help you with your gastric bypass or LAP-BAND® Adjustable Gastric Band surgical weight loss needs, please contact us anytime.
