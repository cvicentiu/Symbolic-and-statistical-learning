Thomas KIRK was born in Church Coppenhall, Crewe 28 July 1852.   He was baptized in St Michaels, Church Coppenhall, 22 August 1852.  Religion: C of E.  He married Frances WOOD in Register Office, Wolstanton, Staffs, 23 June 1873.  On their MC certificate Thomas gave his age as 29 years, occupation as Steel Caster and Residence as Crewe. Frances gave her age as 29 years and residence as Furlong Lane, Burslem.  The witnesses were Joshua Holdcroft and Sarah Ellerton.

 

       Frances was born in Davenham, Cheshire 6 March 1853.  Frances was the daughter of James WOOD and Mary SANDBACH.  She was baptized in St Winifrids Church, Davenham, 3 April 1853. Religion: C of E.  In the 1881 census Frances gave her name as Joanna.  This may be a mistake by the transcribers.

 

Thomas KIRK and Frances WOOD had the following family:

George KIRK was born Cemetery Road, Monks Coppenhall 27 August 1874.

Florence Jane KIRK was born Spring Grove, Church Coppenhall 28 March 1876.  The birth was registered by her Mother on  2nd May 1876.  She was baptized in St Matthews, Haslington, 30 April 1877. Religion: C of E.      At the time of the 1901 census Florence was living at 376 Manchester Road, Great Lever, Bolton.  She gave her occupation as 'General Servant-Domestic'.  Florence married William MOSS in St Michaels, Church Coppenhall, 14th December 1907.  At the time of their marriage William gave his age as 24 years, occupation 'Labourer' and living in Church Coppenhall.  William was the son of William MOSS. Florence Jane gave her address as 'Off North Street, Crewe'.  The marriage was witnessed by Thomas KIRK and Sarah Emma Elizabeth CONDLIFFE

Frances Annie KIRK was born in Haslington, Cheshire 19 April 1878.  The Birth was registered by her Father on 29 May 1878.
She married George Henry KAY in St Michaels, Church Coppenhall, 1 November 1896.  Married by Banns. George was 21 years and gave his occupation as a fitter. Frances was 19 years.  Both gave their residence as Cattle Arch, Church Coppenhall.  The marriage was solemnized in the presence of George KIRK and Ellen HANCOCK who were married the following year.  George Henry was the son of Thomas KAY and Mary EGERTON.

Gertrude KIRK was born in Haslington, Cheshire 24 October 1881.  The birth was registered by her father on 14 January 1882.

Elizabeth KIRK was born in Haslington, Cheshire 13 March 1883.  The Birth was registered by her Mother on 1st June 1883.At the time of the 1901 census Elizabeth was living at 58 Starcliffe Street, Great Lever, Bolton.  She gave her occupation as 'Housemaid - Domestic'.

Thomas KIRK was born in Haslington, Cheshire 6 July1885.  The birth was registered by her Mother on 17th September 1885. 
Thomas married Martha Jane EDWARDS in St Michaels, Church Coppenhall, 8th April 1912.  At the time of their marriage Thomas gave his age as 26 years, occupation Boiler Maker living at 177 Underwood Lane, Crewe.  Martha Jane was 27 years and living at 32 Remer Street, Crewe.  The marriage was witnessed by William Charles MILLS and Amy BARNETT.

Stephen KIRK was born at 301 Stonley Road, Church Coppenhall 19 November 1888.  The birth was registered by her Mother on 21st January 1889.

Elsie KIRK was born Cattle Arch, Church Coppenhall 6 November 1896.  The birth was registered by her Mother on 14 December 1896.She was baptized in St Michaels, Church Coppenhall, 2 December 1900. Religion: C of E.  Baptism record states abode as Cattle Arches.

Martha Ellen KIRK was born Maw Green Road, Church Coppenhall 29 January 1898.  She was baptized in St Michaels, Church Coppenhall, 2 December 1900. Religion: C of E.  Baptism record states abode as Cattle Arches.
