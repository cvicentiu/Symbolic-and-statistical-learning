Welcome to the Department of Mental Health and Addiction Services (DMHAS) website. DMHAS is a healthcare service agency whose mission is to provide comprehensive mental health and addiction services to the citizens of Connecticut.

The DMHAS service delivery system is a dynamic environment—one that changes rapidly to address cultural concerns, respond to research findings, and meet the needs of the people we serve. This website is designed to provide you with accurate and timely information about mental health, substance abuse, our agency and its comprehensive services. I hope that you find the website easy to use and helpful.

Thank you for visiting us. Please let us know if you have any comments or suggestions.
