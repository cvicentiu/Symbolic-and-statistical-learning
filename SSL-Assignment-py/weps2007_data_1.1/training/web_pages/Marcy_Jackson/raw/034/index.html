LifeChurch.tv is One church in multiple locations. The Edmond Campus has been around since 2000, when it merged with what used to be Metro Church. The Mission of LifeChurch.tv is to lead people to become fully devoted followers of Christ . It is the groundwork for everything we do.

Worship Experiences - The worship experiences at LifeChurch.tv appeal to those who are not currently familiar with a church environment and at the same time minister to believers through powerful music and relevant teaching. LifeChurch.tv offers multiple worship experiences each week that vary in worship style.

LifeGroups - LifeGroups are where the real relationships take place. Life Groups are small groups (8-12 people) that meet regularly to study God's Word, and meet each other's needs or share common interests. Hundreds of LifeGroups meet each week in homes, coffee shops, restaurants and corporate offices. There is a lifegroup for everyone!
