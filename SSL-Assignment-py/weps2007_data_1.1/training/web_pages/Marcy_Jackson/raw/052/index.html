Dear Friends,

The Colonel Daniel Marr Boys and Girls Club is a vital community asset. Dorchester relies on us to continue offering our youth a positive place, off of the streets, to learn and grow. Our board, volunteers and staff embrace this opportunity and are dedicated to providing enriching experiences for our kids.
I often hear from the young adults who are now “Alumni” of the Colonel Daniel Marr Boys and Girls Club. Each alum can recall, in detail, how our dedicated volunteers and staff positively influenced their lives. Each story is very personal and varies greatly from person to person, but each story always carries the same message – gratitude for the after school hours and vacation time spent here at the Club and recognition that this place provided the inspiration they needed to become the successful adults they are today. These stories re-affirm how meaningful the work of our Club is…to help each child reach their full potential.

The Club has grown significantly since many of our alums were last here. We now have three facilities and nearly 200 programs; everything from art and music classes to after school tutoring; athletics and leadership development. Not only do our Club members learn the task at hand, they take away something more valuable – a belief in themselves. We owe this success to our most loyal donors and friends. Your commitment makes all of this possible.

I ask you to make a donation to the Club during this season of giving. In 2006 we hope to reach out to even more Dorchester kids and your financial support will make this possible.
Thank you so much for considering our request. Your gift to the Colonel Daniel Marr Boys and Girls Club will have an immediate and lasting impact on our kids, and, will help Dorchester today and in the years to come.
Best wishes to you and your family for a peaceful and healthy holiday season!

With sincere gratitude,


Bob Scannell
President & CEO

P.S. Please visit our web site, www.danmarrclub.org and make your donation today.

Sincerely,

Bob Scannell
President & CEO
