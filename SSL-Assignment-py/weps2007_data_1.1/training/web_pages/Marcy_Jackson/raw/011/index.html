Motorcycle Safety Program

Thank you for your interest in Montgomery College’s Motorcycle Safety Program, administered by the Maryland Motor Vehicle Administration. Please take note of the Policies and Procedures for the program, as some of them differ slightly from the regular Open Enrollment classes you might take at Montgomery College.

The next Registration will be held in February. The Spring 2007 schedule of classes will be posted in January. For further information about registration procedures and other commonly asked questions please visit Motorcycle Safety FAQs or Motorcycle Safety Policies and Procedures.
