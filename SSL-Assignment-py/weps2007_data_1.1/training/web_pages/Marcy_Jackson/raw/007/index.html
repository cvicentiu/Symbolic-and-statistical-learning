About our charity:
Colonel Daniel Marr Boys and Girls Club
The Colonel Daniel Marr Boys and Girls Club is the Positive Place for Kids. From preschoolers to preteens to high schoolers, the Club is a safe haven where children's curiosities and talents are explored and nurtured. Paying annual dues of only $5, they are entitled to participate in over one hundred different programs in art, music, sports, education, peer leadership and life skills at our three campuses in the Dorchester communities. On behalf of the almost 4,000 children whose lives will be brightened by the Boys and Girls Club in the upcoming year, thank you.
