VARIETY SHOW 2003
Thank you everyone for a great show! We were very excited to put together our second variety show. All of our performers were extremely talented. The show was held at Martin Luther Church on Addison the weekend of May 2nd. We were delighted to open each act with a 10-minute band performance, followed by piano playing, tap dancing, singing, monologues, and trunck acts. Even though we did not hold a third variety show this year, we will never forget the great times that were had at the Vshow.

Positions:
Director - Ashley Gronek
Assistant Director - Marcy Jackson
Stage Manager - MaryBeth Gronek
Lighting and Sound - Matt Kucharski
Trunk Coordinators - Wanda Mae Suba and Matt Kucharski
