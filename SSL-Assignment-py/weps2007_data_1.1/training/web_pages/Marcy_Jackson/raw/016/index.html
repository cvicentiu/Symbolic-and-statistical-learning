Christmas Party

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Saturday, December 2, 2006 –  Our Christmas Party was held at  the Cornucopia, Oldfield Bed & Breakfast, 782 Main Street North, Southbury, http://www.cornucopiabnb.com.  In the top two photos Innkeeper Ed Edelson spoke to us concerning the history of Southbury and of the beautifully restored Cornucopia, an antique home (circa 1818) which is on the National Register of Historic Places.  In the lower left photo, Marcy Jackson welcomes everyone and explains the planned events.  “Graces 3” (bottom right photo) presented a program of French music from the thirteenth, fifteenth, and sixteenth centuries.  At the conclusion of the instrumental program Sarah Jane Chelminsky invited everyone to sing well-known Christmas carols (en français).
