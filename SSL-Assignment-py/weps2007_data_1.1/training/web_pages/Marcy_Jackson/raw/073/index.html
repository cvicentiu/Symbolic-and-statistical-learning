Education Lesson Plans and Research Papers
 This is to let it be known that as K-12 educators you are welcome to use any of the lesson plans below with your students in the classroom.  They are not to be published in any other format without permission of the author of this site.   I offer this suggestion for you.   Highlight all the lesson plan that you have opened up and desire to use.   Use the "EDIT" button and select "COPY."   Now open up a word processing document and use the "EDIT" button and select "PASTE."  Now with it in word processing format, you can make all the changes and additions to adapt it to your curriculum needs.   Also be mindful that web sites are here today and gone tomorrow. You may find that web sites in these lesson plans and research papers are no longer active, but don't let that stop you.   For the lesson plans, you can find current sites to use in lieu of inactive sites.    Best wishes, Lewis A. Armstrong.
