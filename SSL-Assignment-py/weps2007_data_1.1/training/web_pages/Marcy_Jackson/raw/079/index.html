To get your friends and relatives listed please cut and paste the message at the bottom of this page and e-mail it to them. Please report any emails that do not work to the webmaster, Thanks.

The Alumni Association tries to maintain an mailing list of the snail-mail postal addresses of all living Andover Alumni. Click here for a list a people we have lost track of and drop us a line if you know where they are. 

In order to prevent web harvesters from using this e-mail list to send you spam, the hot links on this list are being removed and the @ symbol is replaced with (AT).
