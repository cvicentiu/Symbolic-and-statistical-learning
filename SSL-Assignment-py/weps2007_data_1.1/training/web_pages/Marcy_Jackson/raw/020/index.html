Hi! Moses came into our lives almost 11 years ago. At that time I was new to the community and state and would spend some of my afternoons off work visiting the shelter to help fill the void of being lonely and without all my family around.
When I saw his sweet little face through the cage, I was hooked! He reminded me of a baby polar bear-all creamy white and fluffy. 11 years later he doesn't look much like that baby polar bear, but he sure is the baby of our family-all 65 pounds of him! He still acts like a puppy, though- practically bounces off the walls and furniture. He will do the craziest 'dance' if you scratch just the right spot on his back by his tail. Moo-moo or Mosey, as we nicknamed him is loving, wise, silly and sweet all rolled into one very special family memeber! Thanks for allowing us the joy of this wonderful dog into our lives. Moses says thanks too! Marcy and family
