Rec.eq Gatherings Around the World

Deborah Stevenson and Patty Schwalm made a presentation to a 4-H conference in Ohio. 1/97
Corinne Leek (Nova Scotia, Canada) visited req.eq'ers in Texas and California. While in Texas, Corinne stayed with Jane Kilberg and Bonney Epstein, and met Susan Dangar and Elizabeth Moon. While in California, Corinne stayed with Jorene Downs (me!), and met Vicki Blakslee. 2/97
The first "Internet Trail Ride" in Australia was held in the Bogong High Plains of Victoria. It was a huge success and another ride is in the planning. Jo Brock of Adelaide, Australia, is the coordinator. 2/97
Jorene Downs (me!) from California included a Rec.Eq Tour in a business trip. In Tennessee, I met Bill Kambic. In Texas, I stayed with Jane Kilberg, Elizabeth Moon, and Susan Dangar. While at Jane's, Tom Stovall made a brief appearance. While at Elizabeth's, Bonney Epstein invited us for a trail ride. In California, I met Phetsy Calderon before returning home. 3 & 4/97
