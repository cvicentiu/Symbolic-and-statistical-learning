Agency Coordinators

From the beginning of the procurement process and until the final agency comes on-line, state agencies are important partners of Project Aspire. Thanks to their commitment, the availability and quality of vital financial information will empower Florida’s leaders to better manage the State’s financial position and the services provided to the citizens of Florida.

Each state agency is working hard to ensure that state government has a smooth transition from the current FLAIR system to Aspire. Below are the agency leaders in the role of Agency Coordinator for this joint venture. If you have a question regarding how a particular agency is working with or is impacted by Project Aspire, these agency leaders are good people to ask.
