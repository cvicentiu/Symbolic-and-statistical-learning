The Wm. Dickson Co. (WDC) has been based in Tacoma, Washington since its early establishment as a contractor.  Our company has an an excellent reputation for on time quality work within both the public and private sectors and due to this we are frequently requested as an exclusive or select bidder on major projects.

Our project management team provides our clients with budgets for their projects, value engineering, critical path schedules, sound estimating and project management. Together we have over 150 combined years of experience within our office to assist you with most any project. By maintaining an open dialogue with our clients, our projects are on schedule and within budget.  We are proud to say that the WDC has provided more demolition services than any other contractor in the history of Washington State.
