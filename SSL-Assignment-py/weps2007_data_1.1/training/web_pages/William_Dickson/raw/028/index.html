Pearsall, William Dickson, d. 1892.

William Dickson Pearsall letter, 1892.

1 item.



Typed copy of a letter from William Dickson Pearsall to Philander Pearsall, answering a request

for information of historical facts of the Pearsall and Dickson families in Duplin County, N.C.
