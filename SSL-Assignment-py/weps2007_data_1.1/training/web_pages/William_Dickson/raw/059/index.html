Dickson Surname Page
(Kilham, Northumberland to
Bacchus Marsh, Victoria, Australia)

 

GEORGE DICKSON  He married JANE MOSSMAN 30 Oct 1795 in Coldstream, Berwick, Scotland. 

        Children of GEORGE DICKSON and JANE MOSSMAN are: 

                  1.    GEORGE DICKSON, b. Abt. 1793, Coldstream, Scotland..; d. 03 Aug 1885, Bacchus Marsh, Victoria, Australia.

                  2.    WILLIAM DICKSON, b. Abt. 1803, Coldstream, Berwick, Scotland. 

GEORGE DICKSON was born Abt. 1793 in Coldstream, Scotland, and died 03 Aug 1885 in Bacchus Marsh, Victoria, Australia.  He married (1) ELIZABETH TAIT in Northumberland, England.  She was born Abt. 1813 in England, and died 11 Jun 1846 in Kilham, Northumberland, England.  He married (2) ALICE JEFFREY 18 Sep 1820 in Scotland.  She was born 1801 in Northumberland, England and died 10 Dec 1843 in Kilham, Northumberland, England.       

Child of GEORGE DICKSON and ELIZABETH TAIT is: 

                   i.    CATHERINE DICKSON, b. 16 May 1846, , Northumberland, England.; d. 29 May 1846,, Northumberland, England.     

Children of GEORGE DICKSON and ALICE JEFFREY are: 

                  ii.    ANN DICKSON, b. 25 Aug 1821, Kilham, Northumberland, England; d. 25 Mar 1878, Lisburn St Lane, Alnwick, Northumberland, England; m. WILLIAM WOODCOCK, , Northumberland, England.; b. Abt. 1823, Newheaton, Northumberland, England; d. Aft. 1881, Northumberland, England. 

                 iii.    GEORGE DICKSON, b. 01 Mar 1823, Kilham, Northumberland, England.; d. 10 Aug 1900, Bacchus Marsh, Victoria, Australia; m. ANNIE KELLEY, 1880, , Victoria, Australia; b. 25 Aug 1821, Kent, England..; d. 31 Jan 1904, Bacchus Marsh, Victoria, Australia. 

                 iv.    JANE DICKSON, b. 12 Nov 1825, Kilham, Northumberland, England.; d. 08 Jan 1896, Bacchus Marsh, Victoria, Australia; m. MATHEW DAVISON/DAVIDSON, Gretna Green, Scotland; b. 08 Aug 1822, Gretna Green, Scotland; d. 08 Oct 1901, Bacchus Marsh, Victoria, Australia. 

                  v.    ALICE DICKSON, b. 08 Oct 1827, Kilham, Northumberland, England.; d. 30 Aug 1911, Bacchus Marsh, Victoria, Australia. Married William Jeffrey.

                 vi.    MARGARET DICKSON, b. 07 Oct 1829, Kilham, Northumberland, England.; d. 25 Jul 1831, , Northumberland, England. 

                vii.    JOHN DICKSON, b. 23 Aug 1831, Kilham, Northumberland, England.; d. 25 Jan 1906, Bacchus Marsh, Victoria, Australia. Married Ada Pippett  5 September 1860. 

               viii.    MARGARET DICKSON, b. 16 Apr 1832, Kilham, Northumberland, England. d. 01 Dec 1869, Parwan, Victoria, Australia. 

                  ix.    WILLIAM DICKSON, b. 18 Jun 1835, Kilham, Northumberland, England.; d. 12 Jan 1920, Bacchus Marsh, Victoria, Australia. Married Margaret Hunter 

                   x.    ELIZABETH DICKSON, b. 02 Apr 1837, Kilham, Northumberland, England..; d. 03 Mar 1869, , Victoria, Australia. Married William Morton .

                  xi.    ALEXANDER DICKSON, b. 05 Jun 1839, Kilham, Northumberland, England.; d. 21 Dec 1900, Maidstone, Melbourne, Victoria, Australia. Married Frances Maria Gregg/Greigg. 

                 xii.    PETER LESLIE DICKSON, b. 11 Feb 1841, Kilham, Northumberland, England.; d. 13 May 1889, Bacchus Marsh, Victoria, Australia. Married Elizabeth Buckingham. 

                xiii.    JAMES DICKSON, b. 02 Jun 1843, Kilham, Northumberland, England; d. 09 Jun 1843, Kilham, Northumberland, England.

 

George Dickson and his children except for his eldest daughter came to Australia on the Tornado in 1858 and Settled in Parwan near Bacchus Marsh, Victoria
