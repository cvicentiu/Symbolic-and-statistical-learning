Surnames: William Dickson

Saturday, October 18, 2003 - 03:09 AM, (768 Reads)

Born: 1793, Duplin Co., NC
Married: unknown
Children: unknown, but it is presumed he is the father of William Henry Dickson.

William Henry Dickson

Born: April 9,1836
Died: Dec. 12,1918 of influenza
Buried: Union Cemetery, Dickson, TN
Married: Penina Waynick
Wm. H. Dickson was a Confederate veteran of the Civil War.
Known as "Uncle Bill"
Children of William H. Dickson and Penina Waynick:

  1. David Marshall Dickson
  2. Thomas Dickson
  3. William Metulus Dickson
  4. John Dickson
  5. Florence Dickson
  6. James Dickson

Reference:
The Primal Families of Yellow Creek Valley by William J. Nesbitt, c. 1985
