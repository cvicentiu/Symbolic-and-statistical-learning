William Haslam Dickson was born in Duluth, Minnesota in 1873 to Canadian parents.  When the Spanish-American war broke out in 1898 he volunteered with the 14th Minnesota Volunteers.  The Dickson family moved back to Canada sometime after 1900.  William died in Victoria, British Columbia in 1922.
