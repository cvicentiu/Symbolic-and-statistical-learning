Dickson is a city in Dickson County, in the Nashville-Davidson metro area.

The community was named for Dr. William Dickson, congressman


The latitude of Dickson is 36.076N. The longitude is -87.387W. It is in the Central Standard time zone. Elevation is 804 feet.





The estimated population, in 2003, was 12,688.

Traffic
The average commute time for Dickson workers is 24 minutes, compared with 26 minutes nationwide.

A total of 3 fatal motor vehicle accidents occurred in Dickson between 2001 and 2003, according to reports gathered by the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration. This is a rate of 2.5 fatal crashes per 10,000 population, which was lower than the national norm.
