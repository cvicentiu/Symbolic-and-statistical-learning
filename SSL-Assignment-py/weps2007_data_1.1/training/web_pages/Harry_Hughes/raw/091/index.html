Wicked Witch of Walnut Street, The
 
Harry Hughes, Cleveland, Buckshot, John Dudley, and Dupree had been best friends forever. Or at least as long as they could remember. They had discovered lots of things together like mug wumps and real dead bones. But this was the very first time they had ever found a witch! Well, actually they never really “found” her; she sort of snuck up on them. They spend the summer reading and spying on the witch and getting in and out of trouble.
