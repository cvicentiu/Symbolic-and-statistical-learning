Hotel information

Harry's hotel and acclaimed Harry's restaurant are renowned for high standards in terms of their facilities and service provided. Executive chef and co-owner Harry Hughes has returned to his home town after stints in various famous kitchens, including 5 years training with the famous Roux brothers. This is reflected in the demand for fresh local produce on all of the hotel's menus.

Food & Beverage

Breakfast is served from 08:00 until 09:30.
Lunch is served from 12:00 until 14:00.
Dinner is served from 18:00 until 21:15.

Room information

All rooms are en suite and a wide choice of bedrooms are available, from deluxe doubles to economy singles. All rooms contain colour television, direct telephone line and complimentary breakfast.

Area information

The popular seaside town of Aberystwyth is home to the University of Wales and the National Library, both just 0.5 miles (0.8 km) away from Harry's hotel. Full details on local attractions are available from hotel reception

Travel information

The hotel is right in the heart of Aberystwyth, just three minutes walk from the sea front.

Important information

A credit card is required as a guarantee. If the booking is not cancelled outside of the cancellation notice period, your credit card will be charged with the full amount of the booking.

The hotel has only a very limited car park for 5 to 6 cars.

The hotel does not have a lift.

No pets are allowed. The bedrooms at the Harry's are all en suite with colour television, direct dial telephone and come with full English breakfast.
