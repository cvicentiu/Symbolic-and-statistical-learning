George Foster



George W. Foster, 86, died at 2:30 p.m. Monday at Eastgate Manor Nursing Center.

Born Feb. 29, 1920, in Petersburg, he was the son of James Franklin and Lula A. (Fentress) Foster.

He was a World War II Army veteran.

He is survived by his wife, Mary J. (Beard) Foster, whom he married Nov. 13, 1939; one son, Gary Lee Foster of Indianapolis; one daughter, Mary Frances Kimsey of Columbus; three brothers, Dwayne Foster and James Foster Jr., both of Washington, and Luther Foster of Ohio; five sisters, Dorthey May Benz of Indianapolis, Alleen Mickles of Indianapolis, Annabell Sheetz of Texas, Mary Small of Vincennes, and Gettie Daffron of Wheatland; six grandchildren; and 12 great-grandchildren.

Two sons, George Leon Foster and George Earn Foster; one daughter, Linda K. Foster; six brothers and sisters, Lorina Fulcher, Claud Foster, Clyd Byrd, Eddyth McCain, and two infants, are deceased.

The funeral will be at 1 p.m. Thursday at Ed Lee Mortuary.

Burial will be in Arnold Cemetery in Petersburg.

Visitation is from 4 p.m. to 8 p.m. Wednesday at the funeral home.

Condolences may be sent online at www.edleemortuary.com

(March 20, 2006)
