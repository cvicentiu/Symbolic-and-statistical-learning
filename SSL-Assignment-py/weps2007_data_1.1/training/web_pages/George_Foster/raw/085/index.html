Areas of Practice

Mr. Foster’s practice is concentrated in the area of counseling and litigation with emphasis on construction, engineering and design, business torts and commercial disputes.  He has litigation experience in both federal and state courts, AAA/ICC arbitrations and administrative proceedings and hearings.  Mr. Foster also has experience in the negotiation and drafting of turnkey and related construction contracts for numerous industrial, power and commercial projects, mostly on behalf of both owners and contractors.  His practice has included representing owners (public and private), general contractors, engineers and subcontractors in connection with a variety of multi-billion and multi-million dollar projects involving the design and construction of LNG facilities, highways, tunnels, office buildings, light rail transit, rail spur lines, vaccine development facilities, wine production and storage facility, hotel/casino, steel mills, medical facilities, coal power plants, coke oven batteries, hydropower generation facilities and cogeneration facilities.  Mr. Foster also has substantial experience in conducting forensic investigations.
