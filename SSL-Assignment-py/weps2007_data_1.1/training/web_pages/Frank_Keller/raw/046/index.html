J. FRANK KELLER
Former Executive Vice President and Chief Operating Officer
Bill Barrett Corp.

Frank Keller was a cofounder of Bill Barrett Corp in January, 2002, an oil and gas exploration company located in Denver and funded by private equity capital. In 2004, the company raised additional capital through a public offering of common stock and is currently listed on the NYSE. Mr. Keller served as Vice Chairman of the Board, Chief Operating Officer and a director since the company's inception. The company is very active in the Rocky Mountain Region exploring and producing oil and natural gas.

Prior to forming Bill Barrett Corp., Mr. Keller was a cofounder in 1981 of Barrett Resources Corp and served as the company's Executive Vice President from 1983 until Barrett Resources was acquired in August 2001. He also served as Chief Financial Officer of Barrett Resources from 1995 until July 2001, as a director from 1983 until 2000. The company was active as and oil and gas exploration company in the Rockies.
